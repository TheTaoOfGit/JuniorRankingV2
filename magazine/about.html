About Us

The people behind The Badminton Magazine

The Badminton Magazine is a comprehensive resource dedicated to lifting the veil on what mainstream media doesn't say about badminton. We aim to serve as a messenger to tell the stories of badminton professionals and players alike.

As a sport that combines agility, power, and precision, badminton holds a special place in the hearts of enthusiasts worldwide. In the United States, badminton has a rich history, and we are fortunate to be part of the Manhattan Beach Badminton Club community, which has fostered players for nearly 90 years.

We invite you, our readers, to contribute to our magazine by sharing your articles, personal experiences, and insights about the sport. Your valuable contributions will enrich our content and inspire the badminton community nationwide.
