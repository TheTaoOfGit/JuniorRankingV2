Adriel Ammy Chandran, born 2013, is an elite U15 talent ranked GD U15 #4 (12,540pts), GD U17 #24, and GS U15 #3 (9,842pts). Her 73% win rate (161-57) over 20 tournaments is exceptional for her age. She swept three U11 titles at the 2023 Junior Nationals with a perfect 14-0 record and has eight career championships spanning U11 through U15. Her 12-2 mark at the 2024 Midwest ORC was dominant. Partners Leon Luo (32 matches) and Haziqa Mathani (23 matches) anchor her doubles game. Chandran's consistent title contention from U11 through U15 positions her as one of the most promising young players in American badminton.
