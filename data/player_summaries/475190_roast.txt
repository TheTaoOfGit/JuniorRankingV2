<p>What if I told you that in the hallowed courts of junior badminton, there exists a player whose name strikes fear into the hearts of... absolutely no one? In 11 tournaments spanning two years, Lan has compiled a record that would make a participation trophy weep: 16 wins, 30 losses, and zero championships.</p>

<p>The numbers tell a brutal tale. In mixed doubles, Lan sits at a crushing #678 in U17 and #838 in U19 — rankings so low, they're practically subterranean. His partnership with Jasmine Zhang lasted exactly two matches before she presumably realized there were better ways to spend her weekends. Even his most frequent doubles partner, Akshath Potnuru, could only endure five matches of what witnesses describe as "competitive badminton adjacent activity."</p>

In the cutthroat world of junior badminton, where dreams are made and shuttlecocks fly, Allen Lan stands as a testament to the beautiful futility of never giving up, even when maybe you should consider tennis.</p>
