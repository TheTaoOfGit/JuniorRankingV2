<p>Stanley Song is the fashion collection that looks better on the doubles runway than in the singles showroom. Born 2010, his 68-88 career record (43%) across 25 tournaments is not exactly haute couture, but BD U17 #35 and XD U17 #56 show the doubles line has commercial appeal. One title (BD U17 at 2025 OBA NW OLC) and three runner-up finishes suggest the designer is finding his aesthetic.</p>

<p>The signature piece: an 8W-2L at the 2025 OBA NW OLC, where the BD U17 title and BS U17 runner-up were a strong showing. But a 1W-6L at the 2023 Junior Nationals and 0W-2L at the 2023 Northwest ORC were collections that critics savaged. His primary collaborator Kingsley Chong (21 matches) provides the doubles structure, while Tamara Valueva (19 matches) handles the mixed doubles accessories.</p>

<p>The progression from BD U15 #100 to BD U17 #35 is a dramatic rebrand that proves the design vision is evolving. BS U17 #78 remains the weaker fabric in the collection. Keep stitching, Stanley.</p>
