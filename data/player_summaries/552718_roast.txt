<p><b>Meera Bhalekar</b> is the restaurant that opened to mixed reviews but keeps packing them in anyway. At 12-13 career (48%), this U11 competitor is serving up a menu that's almost -- but not quite -- earning that second Michelin star. Ranked #27 in GS and #29 in GD, she's solidly mid-table and climbing, which in the culinary world means the food is good but the plating needs work.</p>

<p>Meera's doubles partnership with Kason Wang (7 matches) is her signature pairing, while Rachel Wang (5 matches) serves as the reliable sous chef.
