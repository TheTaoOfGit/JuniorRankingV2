<p><b>0W-1L in finals. 1W-7L in semis. 39% win rate. Akshith Arava is allergic to the deep rounds.</b></p>

<p>Twenty-four tournaments and Akshith has been in exactly one final, which he lost. His semis record -- 1W-7L -- means he reaches the semifinal round just to donate points to his opponent. Seven semi losses is not a record, it's a recurring nightmare.</p>

<p>His lone bright spot is a 70% three-game win rate, but that apparently only applies to early rounds because it evaporates in the semis. His 39% overall career record (48W-75L) means he loses almost twice as often as he wins.</p>
