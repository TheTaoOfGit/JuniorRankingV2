Born 2011, with a 163-89 career record (64%) across 31 tournaments, Selena holds GD U17 #12, GS U17 #12, GS U19 #23, and XD U17 #7 -- four gallery-worthy rankings with the XD piece being the masterwork. Six titles including XD U15 at the 2023 SoCal CRC, XD U17 at the 2025 Northwest ORC, and GD U13 at the 2023 SoCal ORC demonstrate versatility that critics adore.</p>
