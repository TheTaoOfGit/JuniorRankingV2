<p>Saanya Sreenivas is the indie film that keeps getting into festivals but has not yet found a distributor. Born 2011, her 38-44 record (46%) across just 14 tournaments is a small but growing body of work. GS U17 #72 and GD U17 #83 are not marquee billing, but the GS U15 title at the 2025 OBA NW OLC and two doubles runner-up finishes say there is a compelling story here.</p>

<p>The screenplay has its rough patches: 1W-6L at the 2022 Junior Nationals was a screening nobody walked out of smiling, and a 2W-4L at the 2025 Midwest ORC suggests the production budget is still catching up to the ambition. But a 4W-4L at the 2025 OBA NW OLC, capped with that singles title, was a genuine crowd-pleaser.</p>

<p>With primary doubles partner Samyukta Karthik (14 matches), the duo is building a franchise. The limited tournament count means every outing carries outsized weight -- for better or worse. This is a career still in its opening act.</p>
