Unfortunately, like most child stars transitioning to more mature roles, Zang's subsequent ventures into the U15 and U17 divisions have been met with the critical reception of a straight-to-streaming sequel.</p>

When Liu isn't available, Zang cycles through supporting cast members Jennifer Bangcaya and Jaden Ke Hong Chen with the desperation of a director frantically recasting mid-production.</p>

<p>The 2024 Junior Nationals chapter represents the movie's attempt at a serious dramatic turn—a singles finals appearance that had all the tension of watching someone parallel park in slow motion. Zang's 12-5 record suggests a protagonist capable of greatness, but her ultimate defeat felt as inevitable as the third act of a sports movie where the underdog learns that participation trophies are just as meaningful. Her current rankings tell the story of an actor who's found her niche in ensemble pieces, dominating doubles (#15 U17 GD) while her singles career (#36 U17 GS) suggests she might want to stick to supporting roles.</p>
