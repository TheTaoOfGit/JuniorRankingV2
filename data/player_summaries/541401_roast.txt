<p><b>0-2 in Finals: The Runner-Up Who Runs Up Against the Same Problem</b></p>

<p>Sai Vishnupriya Chennupati has a 56% win rate across seven tournaments, which is decent, but her 0-2 finals record tells the real story. Two finals, two losses, zero titles. Both runner-up finishes came at the 2025 Capital NE OLC in GS and XD, where Prarthana Sanghani ended her dreams in both events. One person, two heartbreaks, one weekend.</p>

<p>Her 2-3 semis record means she loses more semis than she wins, and her blowout losses to Eesha Vikram, Manvi Jandhyala, Samyutha Aluri, and Sloka Darbha show the range of players who can dismantle her. Mehan Murthysundar has been her XD partner for 9 matches -- the longest-suffering doubles partner in the batch.</p>

<p>The move from U11 to U13 hasn't exactly been smooth: 0-3 at DFW South ORC in the new age group. But that 8-3 at the 2025 Peak Sports event proves she can feast when the table is set low enough.</p>
