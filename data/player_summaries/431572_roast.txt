<p><b>6W-10L in finals. 69% career win rate. Trisha Mehta: spectacular until it's a final.</b></p>

<p>Trisha has a 69% career win rate and went 10W-0L and 12W-2L at her best tournaments. She's dominant in the regular rounds. Then finals happen and she turns into a pumpkin: 6 wins, 10 losses. She's lost nearly two-thirds of her finals appearances. That's not nerves -- that's a full-blown finals phobia.</p>

<p>Her 62% three-game rate is genuinely good, and her 16W-7L semis record proves she can win under pressure everywhere EXCEPT the last match. Six titles across 22 tournaments is solid but the ten finals losses are the elephant in the room wearing a silver medal.</p>
