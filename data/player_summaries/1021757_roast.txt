<p>Jennifer Bangcaya is the fashion show where the collection keeps getting better each season but the judges still have not handed over the crown. Born 2010, she has strutted down the runway 32 times with a 161-109 record (59%) and climbed to GD U17 #5, GS U17 #8, and XD U17 #3 -- front row at the highest level. Three titles, including GD U15 at the 2024 Midwest ORC and two U13 wins, prove the designer has vision.</p>

<p>But six runner-up finishes -- including GD U17 and XD U17 at the 2025 Midwest ORC -- suggest the final look keeps getting upstaged on the catwalk. The partnership with Yifan Liu (25 matches) is couture-level doubles work, while the Vincent Chen mixed doubles pairing (19 matches) adds texture. A 11W-2L at the 2024 Midwest ORC was a showstopper, but a 7W-6L at 2023 Junior Nationals had some seams showing.</p>

<p>The progression from GD U15 #15 to GD U17 #5 is a steady ascent toward the top of the fashion hierarchy. With XD U17 #3, Jennifer is one collection away from owning the runway entirely. The only question: can she finally close the show?</p>
