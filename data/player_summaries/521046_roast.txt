<p><b>9-0 in Semis But 3-5 in Three-Gamers: Win by Blowout or Don't Win at All</b></p>

<p>Mohammad Hossain is 9-0 in semifinals and 6-3 in finals -- elite numbers. The catch? His three-game record is 3-5 (37%). If you take him to three games, he collapses. He wins by blowing people out in two, because sustained competitive pressure isn't his thing.</p>

<p>Six titles all came at CRC and OLC events on the Midwest circuit, where the competition pool is shallow. His one ORC at LIBC was a 2-2 split. He's the undisputed king of Midwest CRCs, which is like being the tallest kindergartner. His 34-17 career (66%) is legit, but that 37% three-game rate is a ticking time bomb for bigger stages.</p>
