<p><b>43% win rate. 2W-8L in semis. Mrugakshi Kulkarni: the semifinal where dreams go to die.</b></p>

<p>Two titles and an unblemished 2W-0L finals record sounds amazing until you see the 2W-8L semis record. Mrugakshi wins when she reaches finals -- she just almost never reaches finals. Eighty percent of her semifinal appearances end in defeat.</p>

<p>Her 73-93 career record across 23 tournaments is a 43% win rate. She went 1W-6L at the 2023 Junior Nationals. Her 57% three-game rate is her one redeeming stat, but it clearly doesn't apply in the rounds that matter.</p>
