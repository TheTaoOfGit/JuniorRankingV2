<p>Darsh Jain is the action movie hero who shows up late, says little, and leaves a trail of destruction. Born 2009, his 127-47 career record (72%) across a mere 16 tournaments is the most lethally efficient filmography in this batch. BS U19 #3 and BD U19 #8 are not rankings -- they are top billing at the box office.</p>

<p>The stunts speak for themselves: BS U19 champion at the 2025 Midwest ORC (beating Justin Xia), BS U17 winner at the 2024 Northeast ORC (beating Rithik Rasamsetti), and BD U15 champion at the 2023 South ORC. A 12W-3L at the 2023 Junior Nationals and 9W-2L at the 2023 Northwest ORC are action sequences that leave audiences breathless. Even the 8W-1L at the 2025 Midwest ORC feels like a sequel that outperforms the original.</p>

<p>Four runner-up finishes, including BD U19 at the 2026 SoCal ORC, prove the final boss fights are real. The only thing missing from this blockbuster is more screen time -- 16 tournaments is criminally underexposed for talent this explosive.</p>
