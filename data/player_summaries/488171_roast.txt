<p><b>GS U15 #5 Who Gets Demolished in Mixed Doubles: The Court-Sharing Conundrum</b></p>

<p>Mizuki Ikeuchi is genuinely elite -- 92-25 career (78%), 8 titles, 10-2 in finals. So where's the roast? Mixed doubles. She's been blown out by Michael Yufu Sun, Shaurya Maddela, and Peiqi Guo. Apparently sharing a court with a boy short-circuits something fundamental in her game.</p>

<p>Her three-game record of 14-8 (63%) is solid but not dominant for a top-5 player -- losing 8 three-gamers means she's beatable when she's not at her best. Those 3 semifinal losses sting when you're supposed to be the favorite every round. She even lost a GD U13 final to Krishnavi Kasu after beating her in another tournament.</p>

<p>Still, 10 titles and a Junior Nationals gold is legit. But getting blown out in mixed doubles when you're a top-5 singles player is the kind of cognitive dissonance that keeps coaches awake at night.</p>
