<p><b>5 Titles but a 46% Three-Game Record: Vaishnavi Packiyaraj Chokes When It's Close</b></p>

<p>Vaishnavi Packiyaraj has 5 titles and a 60% overall win rate, which looks great on paper -- until you notice her three-game record is 7-8 (46%). So when matches actually get competitive, she loses more often than she wins. Those 5 titles? All came in U11, where she was beating kids who could barely hold a racket. Welcome to U13, where the free wins dry up.</p>

<p>Her progression tells the real story: she peaked at GD U11 #12 and GS U11 #18, then cratered to GS U13 #41 after aging up. She went from dominating toddlers to getting blown out by Trisha Mehta in singles. Her 5-3 finals record and 8-3 semis record from the U11 glory days are padding a resume that's rapidly deflating.</p>

<p>Her top partner Bryce Xie has carried her through 25 matches of mixed doubles, but even that relationship produced a runner-up at Nationals where they lost to the actual good players. The transition from big fish in a small pond to regular fish in a regular pond has not been kind.</p>
