Ansen Liu is one of the most decorated U19 players in the country, ranked BD #6 (37,500 pts), BS #2 (32,250 pts), and XD #6 (38,250 pts). His 161-75 career record (68%) across 27 tournaments is backed by eight titles, including a clean sweep at the 2025 South CRC with wins in BS U19, BD U19, and XD U19. He also claimed XD U17 at the 2024 Junior Nationals and BD U19 at the 2024 South OLC. His BS U19 runner-up at the 2025 Junior Nationals shows he contends at the very highest level.

Liu's most prolific partnerships are with Jessica Wu (22 matches) and Charity Lam (17). His versatility across all three disciplines, combined with elite ranking points in each, makes him arguably the most well-rounded male player in the U.S. junior system.
