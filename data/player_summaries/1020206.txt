<p>Ansen Liu announced himself as a force to be reckoned with in spectacular fashion at the 2025 SOUTH CRC, capturing an unprecedented triple crown by sweeping the Boys Singles U19, Boys Doubles U19, and Mixed Doubles U19 titles in a single tournament. This remarkable achievement exemplifies the 17-year-old's exceptional versatility and competitive drive that has propelled him to the upper echelons of junior badminton.</p>

<p>Liu's meteoric rise through the rankings tells a story of relentless improvement and dedication. From modest positions of Boys Doubles U17 #23 and Boys Singles U17 #9 in late 2023, he has surged to become the #2-ranked Boys Singles U19 player with 32,250 points while simultaneously holding the #6 spot in Boys Doubles U19 (37,500 points) and #10 in Mixed Doubles U19 (32,000 points). This multi-discipline dominance at the highest junior level is a testament to his well-rounded skill set and tactical intelligence.</p>

<p>With an impressive 70% win rate across 23 tournaments (162-70 record), Liu has proven his ability to perform under pressure at the sport's biggest stages. His breakthrough moment came at the 2024 YONEX U.S. Junior National Championships where he claimed the Mixed Doubles U17 title, followed by a commanding 8-0 performance at the 2024 SOUTH OLC where he swept three more titles. His consistency is further evidenced by reaching five additional finals, including a runner-up finish at the prestigious 2025 YONEX U.S. Junior National Championships.</p>

<p>Liu's success stems not only from his individual brilliance but also his chemistry with top-tier partners. His 22-match partnership with Jessica Wu and 18-match collaboration with Charity Lam have been particularly fruitful, while his ability to adapt and excel with various partners like Darren Teu, Prutha Bharadwaj Ramaprakash, and Caden Wang demonstrates his court awareness and leadership qualities.
