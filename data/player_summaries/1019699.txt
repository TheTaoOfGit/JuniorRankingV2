Akshaj Donthi is a Northeast-based singles specialist ranked BS U19 #53 (10,000 pts), with five titles to his name despite only 14 career tournaments. His 59-27 record (68%) is highly efficient, and he claimed back-to-back titles at the 2024 NE CRC in both BS U17 and BS U19. Most impressively, he went 5-0 at the 2025 Northeast ORC to claim BS U19, defeating Napat Srisalaipaisal in the final.

Donthi's limited doubles exposure is reflected in his rankings, but his singles dominance in the Northeast region is undeniable. His partnerships with Eshan Neog and Xinyan Zeng have provided occasional doubles outings.
