<p>Initial excavation at the Riyan Karanth archaeological site has yielded fascinating artifacts from what appears to be the earliest known civilization of modern competitive badminton. Carbon dating of racket fragments and tournament entry forms suggests this civilization flourished briefly between 2025-2026 CE, though "flourished" may be an generous term given the limited evidence of successful conquest. The dig site, located primarily in the debris field of Texas badminton tournaments, shows clear signs of rapid decline and abandonment.</p>

<p>Layer analysis reveals two distinct periods of activity: the "SCHAFER SLG Era" (2025) where primitive tools managed to secure a single victory among three crushing defeats, and the more recent "YONEX DFW Period" (2026) characterized by complete devastation and a perfect 0-2 record that archaeologists describe as "impressively consistent in its futility." Pottery shards found at the site bear the names "Avniel Ajit Patankar" and "Arjun Chada," believed to be ancient doubles partners who may have served as human shields during particularly brutal matches.</p>

<p>Most intriguingly, hieroglyphic rankings found etched into tournament brackets reveal a civilization that simultaneously existed across multiple age groups, achieving the remarkable feat of ranking in the bottom 20% in every single category from U11 to U19. The consistency of maintaining exactly 149 points in singles and 315 points in doubles across all age divisions suggests either sophisticated mathematical planning or a cosmic joke played by the badminton gods.</p>

<p>Perhaps most mysterious of all, recent strata indicate signs of reverse evolution—rankings actually declining from BS U11 #86 to #105 and BD U11 #68 to #81, defying all known laws of sports development. Scholars theorize this may represent the first documented case of a player getting worse simply by existing. The site has been sealed pending further investigation, though local authorities report occasional sounds of shuttlecocks being violently murdered emanating from the area after dark.</p>
