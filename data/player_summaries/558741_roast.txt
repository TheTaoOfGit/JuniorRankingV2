<p><b>1-3 in Semis: Lucas Wang Keeps Knocking but Nobody's Answering</b></p>

<p>Lucas Wang has made the semifinals four times and lost three of them, posting a 1-3 semis record that screams "so close yet so far." His one title came at the Play N Thrive MW CRC -- a tournament so small it's basically a glorified practice session -- where he went a perfect 5-0. Strip that away and he's 5-6 everywhere else. The CRC champion cape comes off real fast at an actual OLC.</p>

<p>Ranked #40 in BS and #78 in BD U11, the gap between his singles and doubles ranking suggests he's significantly worse when he has to coordinate with another human being. His 62% overall win rate is decent but inflated by that one perfect CRC run. Blowout losses to Srinivas Shankar and Daksh Thakur confirm that top-tier opponents don't even break a sweat against him.</p>

<p>His XD partner Jayasmi Dhruvaraj has stuck around for five matches, and his BD partner Aahir Sehgal for three, which is about as committed as anyone's willing to get with a player who crumbles in three out of four semifinals.</p>
