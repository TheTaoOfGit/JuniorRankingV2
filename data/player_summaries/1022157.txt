<p>Pan's meteoric rise through the junior rankings tells a story of dedication and rapid improvement. This remarkable progression culminated in her most recent triumph at the 2026 WAYSIDE NE CRC, where she captured the XD U17 title, proving her ability to excel at the highest levels of junior competition.</p>

<p>What sets Pan apart is her exceptional versatility and doubles instincts. Her 28-match partnership with Naresh Sriram has become one of the most formidable mixed doubles combinations in junior badminton, while her collaborations with players like Renee Liu and Annabelle Claire Cheng demonstrate her adaptability with different playing styles. With four finals appearances in 2025 alone across singles, girls doubles, and mixed doubles at the prestigious NE OLC, Pan has proven she can compete at the elite level in any discipline.</p>
