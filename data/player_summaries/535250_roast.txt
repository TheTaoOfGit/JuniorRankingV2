<p><b>1-11: Welcome to the 8% Club</b></p>

<p>Agastya Srikanta has graced three tournaments with his presence and departed with one win and eleven losses. That's an 8% win rate, which is technically better than zero but only in the way that a paper umbrella is technically better than nothing in a hurricane.</p>

<p>At the 2025 Austin Leander event he went 0-6. Six matches, six losses, zero hope. His blowout losses include getting demolished by Neel Achari, Pranav Iyer, Avyukth Reddy Pati, and Ayushmaan Senapati -- and considering Ayushmaan himself has a losing record, that's particularly stinging. Zero three-game matches because opponents apparently wrap things up quickly against Agastya.</p>

<p>Three tournaments and already three different doubles partners. Nobody's coming back for a second tour of duty.</p>
