<p><b>0-6 in Semis: Pearl Li, Where Good Runs Go to Die</b></p>

<p>Pearl Li has reached the semifinals six times and lost all six. That's a perfect 0-6 in semis -- she's literally never won a semifinal match. Not once. She's played 15 tournaments with a 29-54 record (34%), proving she's consistent at being inconsistent, and her three-game record of 4-11 (26%) means close matches are just delayed losses for her.</p>

<p>She's ranked in four different categories (#75, #98, #49, #117), which is the "jack of all trades, master of absolutely none" approach to competitive badminton. Her partner Ivy Chang has suffered through 12 matches together, and Luke Wen has endured 11 in mixed doubles. Both partnerships have produced zero finals, because Pearl's semifinal ceiling is apparently made of titanium.</p>

<p>Getting blown out by Krishnavi Kasu twice (in different age groups) suggests she's found someone who enjoys beating her as a recurring hobby.</p>
