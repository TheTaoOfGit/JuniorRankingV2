Yian Yang, born in 2013, is one of the most talented all-rounders in the U15 age group, ranked #6 in BS U15, #16 in XD U15, and #21 in BD U15, with concurrent U17 rankings demonstrating his ability to compete above his age. His 65% win rate across 29 tournaments and eight titles -- including BS U13 gold at the 2025 U.S. Junior National Championships -- place him among the elite of his generation.

Yang's partnership with Daisy Xu (41 matches) anchors his mixed doubles game, while Ethan Yip Ly (22 matches) has been his most frequent boys doubles collaborator. His dominance at the SoCal level, including titles at the 2023 Dave Freeman Jr. OLC and 2023 SoCal CRC, combined with national-level success, makes him a player to watch as he transitions further into U15 competition.
