<p><b>3-9 in Three-Game Matches (25%): The Anti-Closer</b></p>

<p>Wei Zhe Aaron Lin has a three-game record of 3-9. When a match goes to a deciding third game, he loses three out of four times. He is the anti-clutch. The guy who gives you hope for two games and then snatches it away from himself.</p>

<p>His 3 titles and 3-1 finals record look nice, but they all came at CRC-level events, and his 4-5 semis record at bigger tournaments tells the real story. At YONEX ORCs he's a combined 6-13, which is the kind of ratio that makes coaches rethink career choices. Blowout losses to Brendan Chen, Gavin Le, and Srineet Atluri confirm the elite tier is out of reach.</p>

<p>A 30-27 career (52%) is the definition of mediocre, and with that 25% third-game conversion rate, he's not even mediocre when it matters.</p>
