Saanvi Jha has climbed rapidly through the U11 girls' ranks, now sitting at #16 in doubles (1,177pts), #12 in singles (1,094pts), and #24 in mixed doubles (1,037pts) -- up from outside the top 80 in all disciplines a year ago. Jha's primary mixed doubles partnership with Matthew Alexander Lau (13 matches total) has been a consistent source of points. She also showed well at the 2026 DFW South ORC (4-3). With three runner-up finishes at the Bintang event alone, Jha is knocking on the door of her first title and appears poised for a top-10 push.
