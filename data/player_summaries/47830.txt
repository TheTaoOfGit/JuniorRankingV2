Her ability to excel across multiple disciplines sets her apart—she's captured titles in girls singles, girls doubles, and mixed doubles, showcasing the versatility that makes elite players truly special.</p>

<p>The California-based athlete has dominated regional competition, collecting an impressive dozen titles including multiple YONEX NorCal and SoCal ORC championships. Her 2025 season has been particularly stellar, featuring victories at both the YONEX Junior Pan Am Games Selection Event in mixed doubles and the YONEX SoCal ORC in both singles and doubles. These achievements reflect not just her individual skill, but her ability to adapt and excel alongside different partners, having formed successful partnerships with players like Tian Qi Alan Zhang and Youshi Chen.</p>
