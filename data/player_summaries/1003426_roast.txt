<p><b>LYDIA CHAO: GS U19 #2, GD U19 #4, AND 9 WINS, 11 LOSSES IN FINALS.</b></p>

<p>Ranked #2 in singles, #4 in doubles. In finals? Nine titles and eleven losses. She has stood with a trophy on the line twenty times and walked away empty-handed more often than not. The bridesmaid of junior badminton.</p>

<p>Three-game record of 33-25 at 56% sounds decent until you remember she is the second-best player in the country. Her 20-16 semis record means she loses before the final almost as often as she advances.</p>

<p>Blown out by Annie Meng in GS U17, by Jaden Chen in XD. All the talent in the world. Killer instinct left in the locker room. Permanently.</p>
