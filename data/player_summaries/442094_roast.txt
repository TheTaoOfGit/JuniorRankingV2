<p><b>Semis Record: 4-14. Eli You Gets Eliminated in the Semis More Than Any Human Should.</b></p>

<p>Eli You has reached the semifinals 18 times and lost 14 of them. A 22% semis conversion rate would get you fired from any job, but in junior badminton it just means you keep showing up to lose in the same round. He's BS U13 #9 with only one title — a BD U11 win at the 2024 NorCal ORC — which means his ranking is built almost entirely on losing respectably.</p>

<p>His 1-3 finals record is painful, with three runner-up finishes at the NorCal ORC and SoCal ORC selection events. He keeps coming so close and then face-planting at the finish line. His three-game record of 21-16 at 56% is actually decent, which makes the semis implosion even more baffling — he can win tight matches, just not when they matter.</p>

<p>With 14 matches alongside Claire QiuChen Li and 13 with Zelenia Shen, Eli spreads his semifinal failures across multiple partnerships. At least he's an equal-opportunity disappointment. The blowout losses to Melvin Cui and Varish Karri confirm that elite players view Eli as a speed bump, not a competitor.</p>
