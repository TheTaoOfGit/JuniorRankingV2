<p><b>Eight Titles and a 10-2 Semis Record — Adithya Yeluguri Is the South Region's Biggest Fish in the Smallest Pond.</b></p>

<p>Adithya Yeluguri's stats look incredible: 63% win rate, 8-2 finals record, 10-2 semis record. He's basically unbeatable in pressure situations. There's just one small problem — every single one of his titles came at the South OLC, South CRC, or Fortius South CRC. He's the undisputed champion of tournaments that serious players use as warm-ups. When he ventures outside his comfort zone to the SoCal ORC or Northeast ORC, suddenly it's 2-4 and 3-3.</p>

<p>His 66% three-game record is genuinely elite, and his progression from BS U11 #94 to BS U13 #22 is a legitimate rocket ride. But the blowout losses to David Zhang and Vignan Chandrasekaran in doubles show that when he faces players from other regions, the South Region invincibility shield drops.</p>

<p>With 11 matches alongside Ritvik Singh, Adithya has found a partner who enables his regional dominance. Just don't ask him to replicate it on a national stage.</p>
