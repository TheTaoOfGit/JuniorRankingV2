<p><b>100% Three-Game Rate (2-0) But 0-2 in Semis: Clutch Except When It Counts</b></p>

<p>Sloka Darbha has a 42% win rate across three tournaments and the most confusing stat split in U11 badminton. She's 2-0 in three-game matches (100% clutch!) but 0-2 in semis (0% clutch!). So she's unbeatable when the stakes are moderate, and completely beatable when they're high. It's like having a superpower that only works on Tuesdays.</p>

<p>Blowout losses to Ariel An, Samyutha Aluri, Arushi Saha, and Mehan Murthysundar show the top players handle Sloka with ease. Her two partners -- Pardhu Pathuri (5 matches) and Kiana Winarto (4 matches) -- form a tight circle of co-losers. Zero finals, obviously, because the semis are where Sloka's dreams go to die.</p>
