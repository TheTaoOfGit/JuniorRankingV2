<p><b>Restaurant Review: Clay Yang</b> — A neighborhood diner with an extensive menu (6 active rankings across U17 and U19) but nothing that quite earns a reservation. The 53% satisfaction rate (94-81 across 30 tournaments) is the kind of Yelp score that says "edible, not memorable."</p>

<p>The early U13 specials were the best dishes this kitchen ever produced: BD U13 and BS U13 titles at the 2022 NW Oregon OLC. Since then, the menu has expanded to BD U17 #76, BS U17 #66, and various U19 offerings, none of which have earned critical acclaim. Partner Yichen Li (20 matches) and Yifan Liu (14 matches) handle the doubles prep.</p>

<p>Two runner-up finishes (both BD U13 in 2022, both to Vincent Chen and Aarya Vijay) tell the story of a kitchen that peaked in middle school and has been chasing that flavor ever since. The 2022 Junior Nationals (8-7) was a marathon dinner service, while the 2023 Northwest ORC (1-2) was a slow Tuesday. Still cooking after 30 events, just waiting for the next signature dish to emerge.</p>
