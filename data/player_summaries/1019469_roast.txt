<p><b>Fashion Show Critique: Vincent Chen</b> — This season's collection from the House of Chen is turning heads. BD U17 #2, BS U17 #5, XD U17 #2 — the runway is practically his. At 71% (227-89 across 34 tournaments), this designer knows how to cut fabric that wins.</p>

<p>The U13 debut collection was pure haute couture: BS U13 title at the 2022 Junior Nationals over Saatvik Shetty, followed by a perfect 9-0 at the 2021 Egret OLC — a flawless walk. The 2024 Midwest OLC double (BD U19 + BS U19) showed the brand scales beautifully. The 2026 SoCal ORC BD U17 title with partner Saatvik Shetty is the latest piece from the current season.</p>

<p>The accessory game needs work, however. Seven runner-up showings, including the XD U17 losses at the 2025 Midwest ORC and 2025 Junior Nationals, reveal a mixed doubles line that looks stunning on the hanger but falls apart on the catwalk. Partner Aarya Vijay (45 matches) is the most reliable fitting model, while Ishi Reddy (19 matches) and Jennifer Bangcaya (19 matches) handle the XD accessories.</p>

<p>At just 15 years old with 8 titles already stitched into the portfolio, Vincent Chen's label is appreciating faster than anything else in the U17 market. The transition to U19 couture is the next challenge — and the pattern suggests he'll wear it well.</p>
