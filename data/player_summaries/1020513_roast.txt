<p><b>Restaurant Review: Manas Chintala</b> — A mid-range bistro that once had a Michelin U15 reputation: BD U15 #6, BS U15 #13. The kitchen has since transitioned to U19 catering, where BD U19 #90 and BS U19 #51 suggest the recipes didn't quite scale. The 55% diner satisfaction rate (106-85 across 30 tournaments) is the Yelp score that doesn't inspire return visits.</p>

<p>The doubles station with Prajwal Avadhani (36 matches) produced the best dishes: the BD U15 title at the 2023 Dave Freeman Jr. OLC and the BD U17 title at the 2024 Dave Freeman Jr. OLC show a kitchen that performs best in Dave Freeman's dining room. The XD U19 #183 ranking is the appetizer that should be removed from the menu entirely.</p>

<p>Three runner-up finishes, including the BS U15 loss to Rithik Rasamsetti at the 2023 NorCal ORC, are the entrees that arrived lukewarm. The 2023 Junior Nationals (10-7) proved this kitchen can handle a banquet, but the 2022 Junior Nationals (3-6) was the catering disaster. At 30 tournaments, the volume of service is there — the consistency just hasn't kept up with the ambition.</p>
