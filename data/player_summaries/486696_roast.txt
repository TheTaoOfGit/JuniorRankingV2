<p><b>0-1 in Finals But 100% Three-Game Record: Brayden Chong Peaked at One Local CRC</b></p>

<p>Brayden Chong has a 4-0 three-game record (100%), which is genuinely impressive. He's never lost a deciding game. But that perfection exists alongside a 38% overall win rate (10-16) and a 0-1 finals record — meaning he's great when matches are tight and terrible when they're not. He went 0-4 at the 2024 OBA NW OLC, 0-2 at the NW ORC, and 0-1 at the NorCal ORC. Three ORCs, zero wins.</p>

<p>His one runner-up finish at the 2026 Seattle NW CRC (lost to Edwin Huang in BS U15) is his career highlight — and he lost. His 1-1 semis record and 100% three-game rate suggest a player who fights hard in the clutch but can't generate enough baseline wins to make the clutch moments matter.</p>

<p>Partners Deekshith Vasanth and Yuvan Karanam (3 matches each) cycle through his bracket life. Getting blown out by Daniel Bicheng Fong, Justin Lim, and Rishabh Praveen confirms the Northwest field has plenty of players who don't need three games to beat him.</p>
