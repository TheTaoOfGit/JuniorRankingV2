<p><b>81% Win Rate, 25-10 in Three-Gamers: Abhiram Tripuraneni Is Annoyingly Good (But Not Perfect)</b></p>

<p>Abhiram Tripuraneni is a 92-21 career machine (81%) with 8 titles and a 25-10 three-game record (71%). The roast here requires a microscope. His 8-4 finals record means he still loses a third of his finals, which for someone at BS U17 #10 is almost embarrassing. Four finals losses is a lot of silver polish.</p>

<p>His lone blowout loss to Zaph J-Din Bayona in BS U15 is a name that haunts his otherwise pristine record. And his 12-5 semis record means 5 semifinal exits -- not exactly the dominance you'd expect from a top-10 national player. Partner Shraddha Prakash has been along for 27 matches, carrying the mixed doubles effort.</p>

<p>He went 2-2 at the DFW South ORC and 3-2 at Austin Leander -- mortal results from a supposedly immortal player. But honestly, 81% is 81%. This roast is mostly jealousy.</p>
