Adithya Burisetty (b. 2010) ranks BD U17 #42 and BS U19 #73, with a 54% career win rate (67-56) across 22 tournaments. He has claimed three BD titles -- at the 2024 NE MassBad OLC, 2025 South OLC, and 2025 Capital NE OLC -- and reached the BS U19 final at the 2025 Peak Sports South OLC. His primary doubles partner is Fahim Mougamadou (12 matches), and he has collaborated with Dwijesh Makireddi (8 matches) at the NE level. Burisetty's ability to compete in both U17 and U19 draws makes him a versatile presence on the eastern circuit.
