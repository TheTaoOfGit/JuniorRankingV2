<p><b>Seven Titles, 7-7 Finals, 15-5 Semis — But Naksh Shetty Owns Him. Virat Satish Is #2 and Everyone Knows It.</b></p>

<p>Virat Satish has seven titles, a 67% win rate, and a 66% three-game record. On paper, he's elite. In reality, he's Naksh Shetty's permanent understudy. His 7-7 finals record is split perfectly — and three of those seven losses are to Naksh in BD, BS, and XD. He's the second-best player in every event Naksh enters, which is like being the second-fastest car in a two-car race.</p>

<p>His 15-5 semis record is genuinely dominant, and his XD U13 #9 ranking shows he's a real threat in mixed. But the six runner-up finishes include five against Naksh-adjacent teams and Magadheesan Velu, creating a recurring nightmare of being the bridesmaid at every wedding. Laasya Polineni (43 matches) has been his XD partner through it all — their partnership is elite but their trophy count is halved by Naksh's existence.</p>

<p>Born in 2015, Virat has years to eventually age into a bracket where Naksh isn't. Until then, he's the most talented runner-up in junior badminton.</p>
