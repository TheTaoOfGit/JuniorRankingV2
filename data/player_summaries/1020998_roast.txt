<p>⭐⭐ "Lorin's Badminton Bistro" - Would not recommend for special occasions. I came here expecting a fine dining experience after seeing they made it to the finals at the 2023 YONEX SoCal CRC, but what I got was more like a gas station hot dog. The chef promises a .450 batting average on the menu but somehow that translates to losing more dishes than he serves successfully.</p>

<p>The doubles selection is surprisingly extensive - I tried the Jordan Lau combo (available 15 different ways!) and the Matthew Yu special (8 variations), but the execution was inconsistent at best. The singles menu looked promising until I realized the chef went 0-2 at both the 2025 YONEX SOCAL ORC and 2022 NorCal ORC. That's like advertising fresh seafood but serving frozen fish sticks twice.</p>

<p>Service was particularly disappointing at the 2022 SoCal Arena CRC location where they somehow managed a 4-8 record - imagine ordering 12 items and having 8 of them sent back to the kitchen. The only decent meal I had was at the 2025 SGVBC SOCAL CRC where they went 5-1, but apparently that was just a fluke because they're currently unranked, which in restaurant terms means even the health inspector has given up.</p>

<p>Look, I get it - the chef is young (born 2007) and still learning the trade. The 2023 finals appearance shows potential, like a promising appetizer before a disappointing main course. But after 14 different restaurant locations and 64 total dishes attempted, you'd expect more than a 45% success rate. My advice? Try the SGVBC location if you must, but maybe just stick to the bread basket and wait for this place to figure itself out.</p>
