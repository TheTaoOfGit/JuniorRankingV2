Daisy Xu (b. 2013) is one of the top U15 girls in the country, ranked GS U15 #10 and GD U15 #20, with a 65% career win rate (155-81) across 26 tournaments. She has accumulated eight titles, including GS U15 at both the 2025 Dave Freeman Jr. SoCal OLC and 2025 SoCal CRC, as well as GS U13 at the 2025 SoCal ORC. Xu reached the GS U13 final at the 2025 U.S. Junior Nationals and posted a 7-3 record at the 2025 Midwest ORC. Her most prolific partnership is with Yian Yang (41 matches) in mixed doubles, while Hanyi Lei (21 matches) anchors her doubles game. A proven winner on both the SoCal circuit and the national stage, Xu is a genuine title contender at every event she enters.
