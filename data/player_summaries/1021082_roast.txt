<p>Bottom line: Prajwal's is fine if you're looking for decent doubles with a side of nostalgia for his U13 glory days, but don't come expecting singles excellence unless you enjoy the thrill of watching rankings free-fall. The 15W-5L showing at 2025 Nationals suggests there's potential, but until they figure out how to translate doubles success to singles, this restaurant remains more "neighborhood joint" than "destination dining."</p>
