<p><b>0-5 in Finals: Aadya Pathak, the Most Accomplished Non-Winner in U13</b></p>

<p>Aadya Pathak has reached 5 finals and lost every single one. Five times she's stood one match away from a title, and five times she's gone home with nothing. Three of those finals losses were at the 2024 NE CRC and NE OLC, where she collected runner-up finishes like they were frequent flyer miles. Her 0-5 finals record is the worst among players who actually make finals regularly.</p>

<p>Her career record of 39-44 (46%) and 4-4 semis record suggest she's competitive but fatally flawed in championship moments. Her three-game record of 7-7 (50%) is perfectly average, making her the definition of "good enough to be there, not good enough to win." She's been beaten by Vaishnavi Packiyaraj, Laasya Polineni, and Virat Satish in those finals.</p>

<p>Her primary partner Mandy Lu has absorbed 14 matches of this futility. Aadya is the kind of player who makes great semifinal opponents because she'll always give you a fight -- and then lose the next match for you to advance stress-free.</p>
