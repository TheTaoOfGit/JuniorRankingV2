<p><b>5-6 in Semis: Daksh Thakur Is the Gatekeeper Who Occasionally Gates Himself</b></p>

<p>Daksh Thakur has a 72% win rate and 3 titles — genuinely solid numbers for a U11 player. But peel back the stats and you find a 9-8 three-game record (52%) and a 5-6 semis record. For someone ranked BS U11 #4, losing more semis than he wins is like being a star quarterback who can't win playoff games.</p>

<p>His 3-2 finals record is good but not great, and his two runner-up finishes include losing alongside partner Srinivas Shankar — their 27-match partnership producing roughly as many heartbreaks as celebrations. The blowout losses to Darmikh Pallabothula, Chris Lin, and Avyukth Reddy Pati reveal the players who own him.</p>

<p>He went 2-2 at the 2024 Midwest OLC when he was still a nobody, then climbed to #4. The rise is real, but so is the ceiling — and Daksh keeps bumping his head on it in the semis.</p>
