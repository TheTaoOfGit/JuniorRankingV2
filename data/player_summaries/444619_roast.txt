He's the real deal — except when matches go to three games, where his 10-13 record at 43% exposes a mental fragility that no amount of titles can paper over. He's the guy who steamrolls weaker opponents and then melts when someone actually challenges him.</p>

<p>His progression from BD U11 #124 to BD U13 #16 is a genuine rocket ship, and his title at the 2025 Northeast ORC proves he can win at big events.
