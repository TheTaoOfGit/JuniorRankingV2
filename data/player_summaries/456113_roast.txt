<p><b>7 Titles but 11-11 in Semis: Medha Gudavalli Wins Ugly and Loses Ugly Too</b></p>

<p>Medha Gudavalli has 7 titles and a 57% career win rate across 24 tournaments, which makes her sound like a star. But her 11-11 semis record reveals the truth: she's a dead-even coin flip in elimination rounds. She wins exactly half the time when the tournament is on the line, which is the most aggressively average clutch performance in competitive badminton.</p>

<p>Her three-game record of 18-13 (58%) is decent, but her 7-4 finals record shows she still drops 4 finals -- including getting beaten by her own future doubles partners in some of those. She's been riding Alaina Benjamin as her primary partner for 31 matches, forming the Northeast's most codependent duo. Together they've won titles; apart they're just two decent players who need each other.</p>

<p>All 7 titles are in doubles, with zero singles championships. She's ranked GS U15 #50, which translates to "good enough to beat half the field, invisible to the other half." Her blowout losses to Krishnavi Kasu and Ava Ilena Raju Cimala show that when she faces truly elite players, the gap is visible from space.</p>
