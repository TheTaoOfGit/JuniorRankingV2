<p><b>4-5 in Finals: Riana Soprala Knows What Second Place Tastes Like</b></p>

<p>Riana Soprala has reached 9 finals and lost 5 of them. She's got 4 titles, sure, but those 5 runner-up finishes mean she's experienced more heartbreak than triumph in championship matches. Her 60% overall win rate (84-56) suggests competence, but that finals choking habit suggests something else entirely.</p>

<p>Her 14-12 three-game record (53%) is barely above average for someone who plays 17 tournaments. She went 0-3 at both the 2024 NorCal ORC and the 2026 DFW South ORC — complete zeroes at major events that expose her as a level below the elite. Getting blown out by Mokshagna Surpur in XD means even her own XD partner's rival circle views her as collateral damage.</p>

<p>She and Magadheesan Velu have played 30 matches together, forming junior badminton's most prolific partnership. But with a 53% three-game rate, they're basically a coin flip when it matters. Riana is talented enough to threaten, but not quite ruthless enough to finish the job.</p>
