<p><b>1-5 in Finals Despite 73% Three-Game Rate: Shreya Rajesh Kumar Crumbles at Championship Point</b></p>

<p>Shreya Rajesh Kumar has a 73% three-game win rate (11-4), making her one of the most clutch players in the early rounds. Then she reaches finals and goes 1-5. One title, five runner-ups. She grinds her way through brackets with pure willpower, only to completely fall apart when the trophy is within reach. Her lone title came at the 2025 ABC OLC; everything else has been heartbreak.</p>

<p>Five runner-up finishes across different events and regions prove this isn't a fluke -- it's a psychological pattern. She went 0-3 at the NorCal ORC and 0-3 at the DFW South ORC, showing she can't handle big stages. But 7-2 at ABC OLC and 9-2 at Dave Freeman OLC prove she can dominate locally.</p>

<p>Her partner Venba Senkadir (24 matches) has been with her for the whole journey -- the highs of semifinal triumphs and the lows of five finals losses. Her 6-3 semis record means she's great at reaching finals. Her 1-5 finals record means she shouldn't bother.</p>
