<p><b>Three-Game Record: 11-15 (42%). Naresh Sriram Can't Close Matches and Keeps Getting Blown Out by His Own Mixed Partner's Competition.</b></p>

<p>Naresh Sriram's career is defined by being almost good enough. His 51% win rate, 2-1 finals record, and XD U17 #33 ranking suggest competence, but his 42% three-game record and 3-5 semis record expose the truth: he cracks under pressure. When matches go to a third game, Naresh loses nearly six out of ten times.</p>

<p>He's played 28 matches with Zihan Pan, and his blowout loss list includes... Zihan Pan. That's right — his most frequent partner also destroyed him in competition. Nothing says "healthy partnership" like getting demolished by your own doubles ally. Noah Chien and Nithesh Haribabu also blew him out in XD and BS respectively.</p>

<p>His two titles — one at the NE CRC in doubles and one in XD U17 at the Wayside NE CRC — are solid achievements for a Northeast-region specialist. But outside that bubble, he's 2-3 at the Midwest ORC and 1-2 at the MassBad NE OLC. Naresh: regional threat, national afterthought.</p>
