<p><b>15 Tournaments. Zero Titles. Zero Finals. Zero Semis. Anyashree Anand Collects Tournament Entries, Not Trophies.</b></p>

<p>Anyashree Anand has played 15 tournaments and achieved a career record of 22-53 — a 29% win rate that makes her one of the most reliable sources of free wins on the junior circuit. She's never reached a semifinal, never reached a final, and her tournament trajectory suggests she's allergic to deep runs. Her 1-6 at the 2023 Junior Nationals set the tone for a career of early exits.</p>

<p>Her 63% three-game record is actually surprisingly good, but since she rarely gets to three games (most opponents beat her in two), it's like having a great batting average in spring training and then going 0-for-the-season. She went 0-2 at the 2024 NorCal ORC — couldn't even win a consolation match.</p>

<p>At GS U13 #40, she's ranked just high enough to face good players and just bad enough to lose to all of them. Aarav Biyani has been her XD partner for 11 matches, forming what must be the most optimistic partnership in junior badminton.</p>
