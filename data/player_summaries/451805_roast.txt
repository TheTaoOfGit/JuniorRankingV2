<p><b>SOPHIA LIU: THE PLAYER WHO CANNOT CLOSE.</b> Let's talk about Sophia Liu's relationship with three-game matches. It's 18 wins and 23 losses. That's a 44% win rate when the match goes the distance. For context, a coin flip is 50%. Sophia Liu is statistically <i>less clutch than random chance</i>. She is the human equivalent of "we had them in the first half." She has built a GS U13 #2 ranking almost entirely on the strength of winning matches in straight games and then folding like origami the moment an opponent figures out game three exists.</p>
