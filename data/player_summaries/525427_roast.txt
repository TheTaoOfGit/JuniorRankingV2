<p><b>0-4 and 0-4: Fioro Huang, the Symmetry of Failure</b></p>

<p>Fioro Huang went 0-4 at the 2024 SoCal CRC and 0-4 at the 2025 Dave Freeman OLC. Perfect symmetry. Then he went 6-4 at the 2025 SGVBC SoCal CRC and suddenly looked like a different player.
