Mihika Jaiswal is one of the most exciting young talents in U.S. junior badminton, ranked GS U15 #2 (11,519 pts) and GS U17 #16 while also holding GD U15 #9, GD U17 #36, and XD U17 #44. Born in 2012, her 253-95 career record (72%) across 35 tournaments is remarkable for her age, and she has eight titles including GS U15 at the 2026 SoCal ORC, GD U15 at the 2025 Northeast ORC, and GS U17 at the 2025 Austin Leander South OLC. Her 14-2 run at the 2024 Midwest ORC was dominant.

Jaiswal's primary partnership with Lexuan Wen (42 matches) has been prolific, with Sahasra Reddy (31) and Vishnu Anand (27) also key collaborators. At just 13 years old, she already holds U17 rankings across multiple disciplines, positioning her as a generational talent with years of development ahead.
