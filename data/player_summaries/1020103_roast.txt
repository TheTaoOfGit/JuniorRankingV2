<p><b>Cooking Competition: Max Zhang</b> — Chef Max once ran the hottest kitchen in the U15/U17 circuit. BD U15 #8, BS U17 #7, and 3 titles suggested a culinary prodigy. But the current menu — BD U19 #109 and BS U19 #74 — tastes like yesterday's leftovers reheated.</p>

<p>The glory dishes remain impressive: BS U15 title at the 2023 South ORC, BS U17 title at the 2024 Northwest ORC (over Napat Srisalaipaisal), and a BD U15 crown at the 2022 NorCal ORC. The 62% career rate (109-66 across 20 tournaments) confirms the fundamentals are sound. But five runner-up finishes, including the BD U17 loss at the 2024 NorCal ORC, reveal a chef who plated beautifully then dropped the entree on delivery.</p>

<p>The 2023 Junior Nationals (11-9) was a marathon service that showed both stamina and inconsistency. Partnerships with Vivek Rai (21 matches) and Shriyans Bhagavatula (13 matches) have produced good flavors, but the U19 transition has been like moving from a food truck to a full restaurant — the recipe that worked at small scale isn't scaling.</p>
