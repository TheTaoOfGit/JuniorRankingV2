Anvita Jamsandekar is ranked GD U19 #10 (32,000 pts), GS U19 #6 (32,000 pts), and XD U19 #20, placing her among the top tier of U.S. junior women's badminton. Her 111-78 career record (58%) across 28 tournaments includes three titles, with GS U15 victories at the 2022 Northwest ORC and SoCal Dave Freeman OLC establishing her early. She reached the GD U17 final at the 2024 Northwest ORC.

Jamsandekar's primary doubles partner is Ardra Mohan (18 matches). Her progression from GS U17 #19 to GS U19 #6 over the past two years has been one of the steepest climbs among women's singles players, driven by consistent point accumulation even without recent title runs.
