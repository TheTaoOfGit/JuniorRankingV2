<p><b>1-3 in Finals: Tingxin Tan Keeps Getting to the Big Stage and Tripping On It</b></p>

<p>Tingxin Tan has a 58% win rate and one title, which sounds fine until you see his 1-3 finals record. He's made four finals and lost three of them — including getting demolished at the 2025 Midwest CRC, the 2026 Vistara South OLC, and the 2025 Egret Midwest OLC. The one title he did win was a BD U13 at the Play N Thrive MW CRC — a tournament whose name alone suggests it's more recreation than competition.</p>

<p>His 8-6 three-game record (57%) is respectable, and his 4-5 semis record shows he battles. But that 1-3 finals stat is a red flag that says he performs well under moderate pressure and collapses under maximum pressure. His only blowout loss — to Matthew Alexander Lau — is at least limited, suggesting he doesn't get embarrassed often. He just loses the important matches with dignity.</p>

<p>Partner Sahasra Nali and Peter Lei have taken turns playing alongside him, but the finals results suggest the problem isn't the partner — it's Tingxin.</p>
