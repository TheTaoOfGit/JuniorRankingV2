<p>⭐⭐⭐ Disha's Badminton Bistro - "Consistently Inconsistent Since 2023"</p>

<p>Went to Disha's establishment expecting a fine dining experience, but got more of a "sometimes the soufflé rises, sometimes it crashes spectacularly" vibe. The menu promises excellence with appetizing offerings like "Doubles Delight with Sourish" and "Mixed Doubles Medley," but after 12 visits and a 44% satisfaction rate, I'm starting to think this place specializes in serving up false hope with a side of what-could-have-been. The chef did manage to nail one perfect dish at the 2024 NE CRC (that XD U15 title was *chef's kiss*), but immediately followed it up by burning two courses at the OLC finals that same year.</p>

<p>The service has been... let's call it "adventurous." Watching Disha work the kitchen with partners like Sourish Yanamandra and Dwijesh Makireddi is like watching a cooking show where sometimes they create magic, and sometimes they set off the smoke alarm. Her recent collaboration with Irene Xu and Tarini Meghana shows promise, but I've learned not to get my hopes up after witnessing the absolute train wreck that was her 2024 Nationals performance - zero wins, six losses, and what I can only assume was a complete kitchen fire that took months to recover from.</p>

<p>Credit where credit's due: the 2025 rebranding showed real improvement. She bounced back from that Nationals disaster and actually climbed the rankings ladder, moving from #85 to #47 in GD U17 - that's like going from serving gas station sushi to decent mall food court quality. Her recent performance at the 2026 Wayside NE CRC (4W-3L) suggests she's finally figured out how to consistently not burn the toast, even if she hasn't quite mastered the gourmet experience yet.</p>

<p>Would I recommend Disha's Badminton Bistro? Well, if you're looking for an exciting wild card experience where you might get brilliance or you might get completely served (and not in a good way), sure! Just don't expect Michelin star consistency from someone who's still figuring out the recipe. The potential is there, the improvement is real, but maybe wait for a few more positive Yelp reviews before making your reservation for anything important.</p>
