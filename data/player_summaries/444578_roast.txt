<p><b>Three-Game Record: 2-6 (25%). Disha Vijaya Kumar Falls Apart When It Gets Competitive.</b></p>

<p>Disha Vijaya Kumar has climbed to GS U17 #41 in only 11 tournaments, which sounds efficient until you see her 46% win rate and 25% three-game record. She wins only one out of four close matches, which is the kind of stat that makes coaches age prematurely. Her 1-2 finals record and 3-4 semis record confirm she's allergic to winning the important ones.</p>

<p>Her one title at the 2024 NE CRC in mixed doubles was earned alongside Sourish Yanamandra, so at least she knows the trick: let your partner carry. She went 1-3 at the 2024 NE CRC in her other events and 1-3 at the 2025 Northeast ORC, proving that her XD title was the exception, not the rule.</p>

<p>The blowout losses to Vivian Feng, Zihan Pan, and Megha Challa in doubles reveal that against genuine contenders, Disha doesn't just lose — she gets obliterated. At least the Northeast region is thin enough that she can keep racking up ranking points without ever threatening to win anything meaningful.</p>
