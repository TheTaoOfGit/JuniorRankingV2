<p><b>1-5 in Finals: Fonghong Lin, the King of Second Place</b></p>

<p>Fonghong Lin has 1 title and 5 runner-up finishes, giving him a 1-5 finals record that's painful to look at. He gets to the final more reliably than most players get to the semis, and then he loses. Five times. His lone title came at the 2025 Austin Leander South OLC in BD U11, beating Reo Bao. Everything since then has been silver medals and shattered dreams.</p>

<p>His 57% career win rate (81-59) and BD U13 #13 ranking confirm he's genuinely good -- just not when it matters most. His three-game record of 15-8 (65%) is excellent, making his finals futility even more baffling. He can grind out close wins in early rounds but turns into a pumpkin in championship matches.</p>

<p>Five runner-ups include losses at the Midwest ORC, South CRC, and Synergy NorCal ORC. His partner Keaton Ou (11 matches) and Naksh Shetty (11 matches) have been along for this ride of near-misses. He's the badminton equivalent of a student who aces every practice test and bombs every exam.</p>
