<p>Perhaps most remarkably, Chaudhary has achieved something truly special in the world of competitive badminton: a perfect record of zero titles while maintaining the unwavering optimism of someone who still believes her backhand will eventually stop sailing into the next court. Some call it losing; Kaavya Chaudhary calls it character building.</p>
