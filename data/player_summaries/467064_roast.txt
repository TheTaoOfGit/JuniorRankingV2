<p><b>0-2 in Finals Despite Solid Semis: Arshiya Raheja, the Bridesmaid of NorCal</b></p>

<p>Arshiya Raheja has reached 2 finals and lost both. Her 2-0 semis record means she converts every semifinal into a final -- and then immediately loses the final. She's perfected the art of the penultimate round. Her career record of 35-47 (42%) across 14 tournaments isn't doing her any favors either.</p>

<p>Both finals losses came in doubles -- GD U15 at the 2025 ABC OLC (vs Sophia Sun + Saee Thorat) and GD U15 at the 2026 Bintang NorCal OLC. She went 1-3 at the NorCal ORC, 2-3 at the SoCal ORC, and 1-3 at the DFW South ORC. Her travel schedule is ambitious; her results are not.</p>

<p>Her three-game record of 9-7 (56%) is respectable, and her partner Shirin Shaik (9 matches) has been faithful through the losses. She's ranked GD U15 #26, which puts her in that frustrating zone of "too good to lose early, not good enough to win late." Getting blown out by Sophia Sun in her own finals is the kind of déjà vu nobody wants.</p>
