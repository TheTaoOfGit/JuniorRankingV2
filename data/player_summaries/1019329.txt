<p>Koyu Chou's badminton career is defined by remarkable versatility and an unwavering competitive drive that has seen her excel across all three disciplines. With an impressive 71% win rate spanning 62 victories in just 8 tournaments, she has proven herself as one of the most consistent performers in junior badminton, capturing six titles in Girls Singles, Girls Doubles, and Mixed Doubles competitions.</p>

<p>Her breakthrough moment came at the 2022 Junior National Championships, where she dominated with a stellar 14-3 record, showcasing the elite-level play that would become her trademark. That same year, she continued her excellence at the SPBA MW ORC, posting an almost perfect 12-1 record while claiming both the Girls Singles and Mixed Doubles U19 titles, demonstrating her ability to perform under pressure on the biggest stages.</p>

<p>What truly sets Koyu apart is her exceptional partnership chemistry, particularly with Andrew S Wang, with whom she has competed in 17 matches. This dynamic duo has been instrumental in her Mixed Doubles success, including capturing the XD U19 championship at the 2021 NorCal CRC. Her ability to seamlessly transition between singles and doubles play, while maintaining championship-level performance in both, speaks to her tactical intelligence and adaptability on court.</p>

<p>Despite reaching 13 finals throughout her career, Koyu has shown tremendous resilience, converting nearly half of those opportunities into titles while using each setback as fuel for future success. Her rankings of #42 in Girls Singles, #49 in Girls Doubles, and #54 in Mixed Doubles U19 as of late 2023 reflect her well-rounded skill set and consistent presence among the top junior players in multiple disciplines.</p>
