<p>Saujas Patil has carved out an impressive junior career through sheer versatility, capturing titles across boys' doubles, boys' singles, and mixed doubles events. The 2006-born competitor has amassed six championship titles spanning multiple age divisions, including a standout 2022 season where he claimed the boys' doubles crown at both the NE NJBC OLC and Midwest CRC, while also securing the boys' singles title at Midwest CRC—a rare feat showcasing his adaptability across disciplines.</p>

<p>His partnership with Aaron Brijesh has been the cornerstone of his doubles success, with the duo competing together in 19 matches and forming one of the most reliable tandems in junior competition. Patil's court chemistry extends beyond just one partner though, as evidenced by his mixed doubles title at the 2021 USAB South Open Local Championships and his ability to excel with various partners including Siva Rajesh, Carissa Chen, and others across different tournaments and regions.</p>

<p>What sets Patil apart is his tournament mentality—he's delivered multiple perfect campaigns, going undefeated at both the 2023 MIDWEST OLC (4-0) and the 2021 USAB South Open Local Championships (5-0). His overall 45-29 career record reflects a 61% win rate across 11 tournaments, demonstrating consistent performance against top junior competition nationwide, from the Pacific Coast to the Midwest and Southern regions.</p>

<p>Competing at the elite level, Patil earned his place at the 2024 YONEX U.S. Junior National Championships, where he posted a competitive 5-5 record against the nation's best. His ranking history shows steady development, achieving top-100 status in multiple events by late 2023 with positions of #77 in boys' doubles U19, #70 in boys' singles U19, and #89 in mixed doubles U19—rankings that reflect his multi-dimensional skill set and dedication to excellence across all facets of badminton.</p>
