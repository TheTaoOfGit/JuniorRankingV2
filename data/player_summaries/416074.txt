Shaurya Maddela (b. 2012) has surged to BD U15 #9 and BS U15 #33, a dramatic rise from BD U13 #153 just two years ago. Across 24 tournaments he holds a 58% win rate (97-70) and captured both BS and BD U13 titles at the 2024 Midwest OLC, where he went 9-2. His primary doubles partner is Varish Karri (17 matches), and he has also built a productive pairing with Yeewei Yap (13 matches). Maddela's trajectory from the lower ranks to the U15 top 10 in doubles marks one of the more impressive climbs on the junior circuit.
