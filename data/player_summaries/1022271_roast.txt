But credit where it's due — our young auteur learned from these critical pans. By 2025, he'd discovered his genre: doubles comedy-dramas where he and co-star William Geng (38 collaborations and counting) developed the kind of on-court chemistry that would make Laurel and Hardy weep.</p>

<p>Today, Liu sits comfortably in the BD U17 top 10, a solid character actor who knows his lane. The kid's found his niche, and unlike most child stars, he's actually gotten better with age. Three stars — would recommend for badminton fans who enjoy watching technical craft over flashy theatrics.</p>
