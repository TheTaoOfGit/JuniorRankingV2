<p><b>3-7 in Finals: Avyukth Pati, the Sultan of Silver</b></p>

<p>Avyukth Pati is BS U11 #3 with a 75-45 career (62%) and 10-4 in semis. Sounds elite, right? But his finals record is 3-7. He loses 70% of his finals. Seven runner-up finishes. He gets to the final and then his game just... evaporates. Neel Achari owns him in finals -- beating him in BS at both the SoCal ORC and DFW South ORC.</p>

<p>His 12-6 three-game record (66%) is good, but losing 6 three-gamers as a top-3 player is still a lot. He went 7-0 at the Fortius South CRC, proving he can dominate when the competition thins out, but at stacked ORCs with Neel and Naksh Shetty in the draw, he's a reliable bridesmaid. Six runner-up finishes is a special kind of torture -- always good enough to be there, never good enough to win it.</p>
