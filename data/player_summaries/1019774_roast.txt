<p><b>Movie Franchise: Sydney Li</b> — The Sydney Li Extended Universe is a sprawling epic centered on the crown jewel: XD U19 #2 (44,000 points). With 73% (182-65 across 24 tournaments) and 8 titles, this franchise is printing sequels faster than Marvel.</p>

<p>The XD origin story is phenomenal: XD U19 titles at the 2024 Midwest ORC, 2026 SoCal ORC (beating Evan Liu and Sophia Wu), and the 2023 NE OLC. Rocky Yang (25 matches) is the original co-star, but the franchise has successfully recast with Chloe A Kai (24 matches) and Samuel Jiaoyang Li (17 matches). The GS arc peaked at GS U17 #1 in mid-2025 — the box office apex.</p>

<p>The GD U19 #30 subplot is the franchise's weakest thread. Despite a 2024 NE ORC title with Sophia Wu, the GD U19 runner-up at the 2025 Junior Nationals (to Lydia Chao and Charity Lam) was the sequel that couldn't top the original. Six runner-up finishes across the franchise suggest too many cliffhangers that never resolve into wins. But at GS U19 #10 and XD U19 #2, Sydney Li remains one of the most bankable names in the business.</p>
