<p><b>1-4 at 20%: Leo Li's Lone Win Is Carrying His Entire Career</b></p>

<p>Leo Li has won exactly one match in four attempts across two tournaments, giving that single victory the impossible task of making his career look meaningful. It can't. His 0-2 at the Capital NE OLC was a clean washout, and his 1-2 at the Wayside CRC was only marginally better. Ranked #66 in BD and #73 in BS U11, he's in the part of the rankings that exists to fill space.</p>

<p>His 0-1 semis record means he somehow reached a semi and lost, which with a 20% win rate feels like the bracket was just being polite. Three blowout losses to Pardhu Pathuri, Ayushram Keshidi, and Javen Wu confirm that competent players make quick work of Leo. Partners Xintong Darren Lu and Krishav Bhatt (who has his own 0% problem) are an all-star team of losing.</p>
