Dwijesh Makireddi (b. 2010) ranks BS U17 #15 and BD U17 #28, establishing himself as one of the stronger boys in the U17 bracket. With a 58% career win rate (98-70) across 23 tournaments, he has collected six titles including BS U15 and XD U15 at the 2024 NE MassBad OLC, BD U15 at the 2024 NE CRC, and most recently XD U17 at the 2026 Bintang NorCal OLC and BS U17 at the 2025 NE OLC. His 9-2 showing at the 2024 NE MassBad OLC was a career-best. Makireddi competes across multiple partnerships, with Nidhi Sujin, Varin Karri, and Disha Vijayakumar (9 matches each) among his most frequent doubles and mixed partners.
