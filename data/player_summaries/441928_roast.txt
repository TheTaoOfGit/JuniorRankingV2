<p>Niranjan Manjith has played 32 of his career matches with Sashanth Senthil, making them the most committed doubles partnership in the South region. Too bad the results are a 48% career win rate and a 1-3 finals record. <p>His 46% three-game record (6-7) means he's below average in tight situations, and his 4-1 semis record is propped up by regional events. The moment he plays in the 2026 South ORC, it's 0-2. <p>At BD U17 #50, Niranjan occupies that awkward middle ground: too good to quit, too mediocre to win anything meaningful.</p>
