Anirudh Rudharraju, born in 2014, has rocketed to the top of the U13 rankings -- #3 in both BD and BS, #6 in XD -- with a formidable 73% career win rate across 24 tournaments. His trophy case is stacked with eight titles, including BD U11 gold at the 2024 U.S. Junior National Championships and XD U11 at the same event. He dominated the 2026 SoCal ORC with a 13-3 record, though he fell in both the BD U13 and BS U13 finals there.

Rudharraju's partnership with Kevin Xie (68 matches) is the most extensive pairing in his age group, forming a near-unbeatable doubles combination that has produced multiple titles. His mixed doubles work with Krishnavi Kasu (45 matches) is equally prolific. His progression from BD U11 #14 in late 2023 to BD U13 #3 makes him one of the most dominant players in the current U13 cohort.
