<p><b>1-5 in Finals: Bowen Wang Collects Silver Medals Like They're Going Out of Style</b></p>

<p>Bowen Wang is 1-5 in finals, which means he reaches championship matches at an elite rate and then loses them at an even more elite rate. Five runner-up finishes and one title -- a BS U11 win at the Bintang NorCal OLC. His biggest nemesis is apparently Matthew Alexander Lau, who beat him in three separate finals. At this point Lau probably sends Bowen a thank-you card after every tournament.</p>

<p>His 53% career win rate and BD U11 #9 ranking suggest he's actually a solid player, but his three-game record of 5-6 (45%) and finals futility tell a different story. He went 0-4 at the 2025 Midwest ORC -- a clean sweep in the wrong direction. One of his blowout losses came at the hands of his own mixed doubles partner Kylie Ou, which is the kind of humiliation that makes car rides home very quiet.</p>

<p>Ranked #9 in three separate U11 categories, he's consistent -- consistently the ninth-best at everything. A top-10 participation machine with a cabinet full of second-place trophies.</p>
