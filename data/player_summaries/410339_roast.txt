<p><b>6W-11L in finals. Siddhi Thorat: the queen of almost.</b></p>

<p>Siddhi has made it to 17 finals and lost 11 of them. That's not a career, that's an extended audition for second place. Her 51% three-game win rate -- on 54 three-game matches -- means she's statistically indistinguishable from a random number generator when the pressure is on. Twenty-eight wins, twenty-six losses in tight matches. That's not clutch, that's a coin toss wearing a headband.</p>

<p>She's ranked GS U13 #3 with six titles, which sounds decent until you see the trail of runner-up finishes stretching behind her like a sad parade. Lost in the GS U13 final, the GD U11 final (twice), the XD U13 final -- at some point the pattern becomes the personality.</p>

<p>With 29 tournaments and a 66% win rate, Siddhi plays a LOT of badminton. She's not bad -- she's just reliably, consistently, heartbreakingly close to being good. The bridesmaid who's been to so many weddings she's started bringing her own veil.</p>
