Kevin Guowei Yang made an immediate impact at the U19 level, earning rankings of #112 in doubles (6,250pts), #103 in singles (4,000pts), and #80 in mixed doubles (9,000pts) from a single tournament -- the 2025 Yonex Bellevue Northwest ORC, where he went 4-3. The 2009-born player reached the mixed doubles final with partner Sydney Li, falling to Vaughn Zhao and Jennifer Xu. Yang also competed in doubles with Daniel Seungchan Park. His strong debut performance, particularly the #80 mixed doubles ranking from one event, suggests significant potential if he continues competing on the national circuit.
