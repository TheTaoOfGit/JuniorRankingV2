<p><b>1 Title From 1 Perfect Tournament: Constance Cai, the One-Hit Wonder of Wayside</b></p>

<p>Constance Cai went 5-0 at the 2026 Wayside NE CRC and won a GS U11 title. One tournament. One title. Then you look at the rest: 0-2 at LIBC ORC, 0-3 at Midwest ORC, 0-4 at NE OLC, 1-2 at DFW South ORC. Her 6-11 career (35%) outside that one magical weekend is atrocious.</p>

<p>She has one perfect day propping up an otherwise dismal record. Partner Tyler Yun has been along for 6 matches and Jeremiah Wang for 4, and together they've experienced mostly defeat. The blowout losses to Shrihayagrivan Karthigayan and Neel Achari confirm that elite U11 competition is a different universe.</p>
