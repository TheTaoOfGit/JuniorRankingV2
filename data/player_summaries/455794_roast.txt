<p><b>0-3 in Finals: Andy Starostin, the Perennial Bridesmaid</b></p>

<p>Andy Starostin has reached 3 finals and lost every single one. His 0-3 finals record means he's done all the hard work of winning quarterfinals and semis, only to completely fall apart when the trophy is on the line. Two of those losses came at the same tournament (2025 OBA NW OLC), where he lost both BD and XD finals. Double the heartbreak, same day delivery.</p>

<p>With a career record of 24-41 (36%), Andy loses nearly two-thirds of his matches. He went 0-2 at the 2023 Northwest ORC and 2-4 at Junior Nationals. His three-game record of 6-3 (66%) is actually decent, but his semis record of 3-2 shows he's just good enough to reach finals where he invariably chokes.</p>

<p>His blowout losses are all from U11 -- Liam Xiao, Leon Luo, Anirudh Rudharraju -- suggesting he was getting demolished before he could tie his own shoes. At least he's improved from "getting destroyed early" to "getting destroyed in finals."</p>
