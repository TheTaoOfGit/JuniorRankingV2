<p><b>Fashion Show: Sasha Suman</b> — The House of Suman presents its signature collection: GD U19 #11, GS U19 #18, XD U19 #24. A well-constructed triple-discipline line with a 65% approval rating (166-89 across 31 tournaments) and 7 titles. This label has range.</p>

<p>The doubles collection is the flagship: GD U17 titles at the 2024 NE ORC and 2025 Junior Nationals (both with Yalan Zhu, the 33-match creative director), plus the GD U17 title at the 2025 South ORC. The XD U17 crown at the 2025 Junior Nationals (with Vivek Rai, beating Vincent Chen) was the haute couture showstopper. The 2023 Junior Nationals (16-5) was a Fashion Week debut for the ages.</p>

<p>The singles line at GS U19 #18 has solid construction but rarely steals the spotlight — the GS U15 title at the 2021 USAB South OLC and the GS U17 title at the 2023 South OLC are the vintage pieces. Only two runner-up finishes means Sasha either wins or exits before the final runway walk. The Adhyaan Balaji partnership (29 matches) handles XD duties. With 7 titles across all three disciplines, this collection has versatility that most labels can only dream of.</p>
