<p><b>9-12 in Three-Gamers: Velu Magadheesan Chokes When It Counts</b></p>

<p>Velu Magadheesan has a sparkling 70% win rate, 7 titles, and was once the #1 ranked U11 player in both BS and BD. Sounds incredible — until you check his three-game record. At 9-12 (42%), this kid folds faster than a lawn chair when matches get tight. For someone who dominates early rounds, he has a remarkable talent for losing the close ones.</p>

<p>His finals record of 7-5 means he's lost nearly half his championship matches, and his semis record of 12-11 is barely better than a coin flip. He's moved up to U13 now, where the competition has clearly taken a bite — going 4-4 at his last ORC. The kid who used to steamroll U11 fields is discovering that bigger kids hit harder.</p>

<p>He and partner Riana Soprala have played 30 matches together, which makes them the most codependent duo in junior badminton. Blowout losses to GaoGao Fan and Vignan Chandrasekaran suggest the U13 elite have already figured him out. Welcome to the big leagues, Velu.</p>
