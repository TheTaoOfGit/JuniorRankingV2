<p><b>5-0 in Finals, 5-0 in Semis: Daniel Fong Is Untouchable... When He Actually Gets There</b></p>

<p>Daniel Fong is a perfect 5-0 in finals and 5-0 in semifinals, which makes him sound like a stone-cold killer. The catch? He's 77-71 overall (52%), meaning he loses almost as much as he wins in the other 138 matches that aren't elimination rounds. It's like being a fantastic closer but a terrible starter -- except in badminton you have to do both.</p>

<p>His three-game record of 12-12 (50%) is the most perfectly average stat imaginable. He literally cannot gain an edge in close matches. He went 9-0 at the 2025 OBA NW OLC for a triple crown, then immediately followed it up with 2-3 at the SoCal ORC. Peak-and-valley Daniel can dominate local Northwest events and then get bodied the moment he leaves his comfort zone.</p>

<p>Five titles across OLCs and CRCs, zero at ORCs or Nationals. He's the undisputed king of tournaments nobody outside the Pacific Northwest has heard of. His partner rotation reads like a dating app -- Felicia, Kaitlyn, Nathaniel, Darren -- he can't commit to anyone because commitment means accountability.</p>
