<p>⭐⭐ "Jonathan Loh's Badminton Bistro" - Would not recommend for fine dining, but decent for a quick snack</p>

<p>Went to Jonathan's establishment 23 times over the past few years and honestly, it's the definition of "hit or miss." Sometimes you get a delightful 7W-3L experience like at the SYNERGY NORCAL location, other times you're stuck with a disappointing 3W-5L meal at the South ORC branch that leaves you questioning your life choices. The consistency here is about as reliable as a paper shuttle in the rain. At least the kid maintains a barely-above-.500 record (83-77), which is like saying "our food only gives you food poisoning 48% of the time!"</p>

<p>The signature dish appears to be "Mixed Doubles Surprise" which he somehow managed to perfect exactly once at the 2025 ABC OLC in the U13 division. Since then, it's been nothing but kitchen disasters and burnt offerings. The head chef clearly peaked early and has been coasting on that one Yelp Elite review ever since. Currently ranked #103 in XD U15, which is restaurant speak for "we're the 103rd best place you could choose, but hey, at least we're not #104!"</p>

<p>The staff dynamic is... interesting. His main sous chef Ethan Xiao-ze Yang has stuck around for 37 services, which either speaks to incredible loyalty or a complete lack of other employment options. The rotating cast of other kitchen assistants (Sherlyn, Ana Paula, Hanyi) seem to cycle through faster than a McDonald's drive-through, probably because they realized there are better establishments down the street with actual trophies in their display cases.</p>

<p>Jonathan's has improved from its early days when it was barely scraping by in the U11 division (#35 BD, #22 BS), but climbing to #64 BD U15 and #36 BS U15 is like bragging about being the 64th best taco truck in LA. Sure, you're not the worst, but nobody's making a special trip to visit you either. The restaurant shows potential but desperately needs a new head chef, better ingredients, and maybe a reality check about what "fine dining" actually means.</p>
