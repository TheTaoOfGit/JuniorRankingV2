Most players would've hung up their racket after that massacre, but not our girl. She's got the kind of stubborn persistence usually reserved for insurance investigators and door-to-door encyclopaedia salesmen.</p>

<p>Her partnership with Kathy Yan tells the real story—sixteen matches together, which in junior badminton is like a twenty-year marriage. Either way, their chemistry is more mysterious than a missing person case with no witnesses.</p>
