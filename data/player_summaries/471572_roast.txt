<p><b>73% Three-Game Rate and 6 Titles: Shrihayagrivan Karthigayan Is the U11 Terminator -- Can He Survive U13?</b></p>

<p>Shrihayagrivan Karthigayan (try saying that five times fast) has a 73% three-game win rate, 6 titles, a 68% career win rate, and 111 wins across 18 tournaments. By any metric, he's a monster. But let's look closer: all 6 titles came in U11, and since moving up to U13, the pickings have been slimmer. His 10-11 semis record means he's actually below .500 in elimination rounds -- for every semifinal he wins, he loses one too.</p>

<p>His 6-4 finals record is solid, but those 4 losses include getting beaten by Naksh Shetty twice and at the ABC OLC -- the same Naksh who apparently lives rent-free in his head. His blowout losses to Aayush Jaladurgam and Reyansh Chalamalasetti show the elite tier still has a hold on him.</p>

<p>His partner Toby Liu has been with him for a staggering 49 matches, forming the most prolific mixed doubles pairing in U11/U13. The question now is whether the U11 dominance translates or whether he'll join the long list of U11 champions who become U13 also-rans.</p>
