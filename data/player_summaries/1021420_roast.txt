<p><b>0-5 in finals. Zero. For. Five.</b></p>

<p>Felicia Ho has made it to five finals and lost every single one of them. That takes a special kind of talent -- the talent of choking on the biggest stage. Her 37% three-game win rate means she's less clutch than a broken transmission. A coin flip would literally give her better odds in a close match.</p>

<p>34 tournaments, zero titles, and a 45% career win rate. She's played nearly 100 losses worth of badminton and has nothing to show for it except a collection of silver medals and the phone numbers of 5 different partners who couldn't save her. Bella Liu alone has beaten her in two different finals. At some point, Bella must feel bad about it. Almost.</p>

<p>Her semis record is 5-8, which means she can't even reliably get TO the final she's going to lose. The progression from U13 #35 in singles to U17 #50 is the kind of glacial improvement that makes you wonder if the rankings are just being polite.</p>
