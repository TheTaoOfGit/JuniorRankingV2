Aarav Gupta (b. 2011) is one of the top U17 players in the country, ranked BD U17 No. 14, BS U17 No. 18, and BS U19 No. 45 with a commanding 64% career win rate (104-58 across 20 tournaments). He has collected five titles including BS U15 at both the 2024 South CRC and 2025 South ORC, plus BD U17 at the 2025 Peak Sports South OLC. His six runner-up finishes, including BS U15 at the 2025 U.S. Junior Nationals, underscore his consistent presence in championship rounds. The partnership with Abhiram Tripuraneni (17 matches) anchors his doubles game, while Saee Thorat (19 matches) is his primary mixed partner.
