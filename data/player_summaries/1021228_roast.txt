<p><b>Heist Movie: Mitchell Purnomo</b> — A veteran operative from the 2008 class who pulled off some spectacular early jobs and then... went quiet. Four titles, all from 2021-2022 (BD U15 and XD U15 at the 2022 SPBA MW ORC, BD U15 and BS U15 at the 2021 NW CRC), form a golden-era highlight reel. BD U19 #44, BS U19 #20, XD U19 #25 with a 62% success rate (87-53 across 17 tournaments).</p>

<p>Only 17 tournaments is suspiciously few for a player born in 2008 — this is the heist crew member who takes long vacations between jobs. The 2022 Junior Nationals (13-5) and 2022 SPBA MW ORC (12-1) were legendary scores, but the 2023 Junior Nationals (3-6) and 2023 SoCal ORC (0-2) were the jobs that went sideways.</p>

<p>Five runner-up finishes include the XD U17 loss at the 2023 Midwest ORC and two 2022 NW Oregon OLC losses. Partner Sophia Jia (18 matches) and Nicholas Volfbeyn (11 matches) have been the main accomplices. The question isn't whether Mitchell has the skills — the 62% rate proves he does — but whether the sporadic tournament attendance means the best heists are already behind him.</p>
