<p><b>GD U11 #1 and GS U11 #1: Thea Yuan's Only Flaw Is Losing to Kavya Balaji When It Matters</b></p>

<p>Thea Yuan is the #1 ranked player in both GD U11 and GS U11 with 4 titles, a 4-1 finals record, and a 5-1 semis record. She's dominant. She's also got blowout losses to Kavya Balaji and Arundhathi Padma Mohan that reveal there's a tier above her she hasn't cracked yet.</p>

<p>Her 6-4 three-game record (60%) is good but not elite for a #1 player. She went 0-3 at the 2024 ABC OLC in what appears to be her debut — a humbling start for someone who now sits atop the rankings. Her 7-7 record at Junior Nationals is the definition of ordinary on the national stage.</p>

<p>She's only been playing 10 tournaments, so the sample size is small. Her partner Timmy Qinnuo Fan (13 matches) is doing the heavy lifting in XD, and Prarthana Sanghani handles the GD duties. Thea's #1 ranking is real, but it's built on a handful of strong SoCal and DFW results — the question is whether she can dominate at the biggest events, not just the regional ones.</p>
