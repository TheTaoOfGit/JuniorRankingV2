<p><b>0-1 in Semis, 5-1 Three-Game Record: Tanush Jaltar, Clutch in the Consolation Rounds</b></p>

<p>Tanush Jaltar has a sneaky-good 5-1 three-game record (83%), but his 23-39 career (37%) and 0-1 semis record show that being clutch in close matches doesn't matter when you rarely face them at the business end of a tournament. Fourteen tournaments with zero titles or runner-ups. That's a LOT of badminton with nothing to show for it.</p>

<p>His partner Aaditya Lanka has been along for 18 matches, forming a doubles pair that is extraordinarily good at playing a lot of matches and winning roughly a third of them. The blowout loss to his own region's Asrith Addanki is especially humbling -- the 33% win rate player beat the 37% win rate player decisively.</p>
