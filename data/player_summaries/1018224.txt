Her rise to the GS U19 #20 national ranking reflects a player who consistently delivers when the pressure mounts.</p>

Even in defeat, she demonstrates remarkable consistency—her nine finals appearances across multiple events reveal a player who thrives in clutch moments.</p>

<p>What sets Siu apart isn't just her trophy collection, but her resilience in the face of adversity.
