<p><b>3-0 in Finals, 8-10 in Three-Gamers: Soudamini Reddy, the CRC Champion Who Crumbles Everywhere Else</b></p>

<p>Soudamini Reddy has 3 titles and a 3-0 finals record, which sounds dominant until you see her 38-66 career (36%). She wins 36% of her matches. Three titles from 18 tournaments and 66 losses. Her three-game record of 8-10 (44%) means she loses more close matches than she wins.</p>

<p>She went 8-0 at the 2025 Fortius South CRC, which accounts for a fifth of her career wins in one weekend. At the SoCal ORC she went 0-3, at the LIBC ORC 1-3, and at the DFW South ORC 2-4. All three titles came at CRC/OLC events, making her the unquestioned queen of minor-league badminton and the unquestioned peasant of everything above it. Eighteen tournaments and 66 losses -- that's commitment to a craft she hasn't quite mastered.</p>
