Niranjan Manjith (b. 2010) is a rising U17 doubles specialist ranked BD U17 No. 50 with a 48% career win rate (38-40 in 13 tournaments). He captured the BD U17 title at the 2025 Austin Leander South OLC and reached two additional finals. His nearly exclusive partnership with Sashanth Senthil (32 of his matches) makes them one of the most committed doubles pairings on the circuit. His 5-3 at the Austin Leander event and 4-2 at the 2025 Schafer SLG South OLC show solid recent form.
