This exceptional tournament performance showcased her versatility across multiple disciplines and her ability to elevate her game when it matters most. Her mixed doubles prowess is further evidenced by her current #225 U19 ranking with 4,995 points, competing alongside partners like Tsun Him Lam and Ethan Chan.</p>

<p>Leung's competitive journey spans 16 tournaments with a 36-54 record, including multiple appearances at the prestigious YONEX U.S. Junior National Championships in both 2024 and 2025.
