<p>⭐⭐ "Tejas Ranade's Badminton Buffet" - Went here expecting a feast, left absolutely starving. The menu promises exciting dishes like "Boys Singles Special" and "Mixed Doubles Delight," but somehow they only deliver 12% of the time. That's not even statistically possible unless you're actively trying to disappoint customers.</p>
