<p><b>Three Titles and 3-2 Finals Record — But a 47% Three-Game Rate and 5-5 Semis. Gabby Xiang Is Streaky as a Bacon Factory.</b></p>

<p>Gabby Xiang has three titles and a respectable 3-2 finals record, which makes her look like a closer. But her 47% three-game record (11-12) and 5-5 semis record tell a different story: she's inconsistent. She went 0-3 at the 2024 Midwest ORC and 1-4 at the 2025 Midwest ORC, then turned around and won the 2025 Fortius South CRC with a strong run. She's the player who's either having the tournament of her life or the worst day of her career, with no in-between.</p>

<p>Her partner Erela Liu (30 matches) is the longest partnership in her career, and together they've built a doubles presence in the South region. But her blowout losses to Christopher Gunawan in XD U17 and Dwijesh Makireddi show the ceiling is clear when she faces national-level talent.</p>

<p>At GS U17 #58, Gabby is ranked in the mushy middle — too good to ignore, too streaky to rely on. Her career is one big "it depends on the day."</p>
