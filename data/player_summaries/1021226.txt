He has forged strong partnerships with multiple doubles players, most notably with Rudra Gaikwad across four matches, demonstrating his ability to adapt his playing style and build chemistry with different partners. His experience alongside players like Peter H. Shen, Shimon Keselman, and Louis N Thai reflects a player who embraces teamwork and continuous learning through diverse playing experiences.</p>

<p>Ishaan's breakthrough moment came at the 2021 NW CRC, where he secured his first tournament victory among a competitive field. His consistent participation in Northwest regional tournaments has provided him with invaluable match experience against top junior talent.</p>

His willingness to compete in both singles and doubles formats demonstrates his comprehensive understanding of badminton's tactical demands.</p>
