Shrihayagrivan Karthigayan is arguably the most dominant U13 player in the country, ranked BS #5 nationally with a remarkable 68% career win rate (111-50) across 18 tournaments. The 2015-born sensation has amassed six titles, including a stunning 12-1 performance at the 2025 Northeast ORC where he captured both XD and BS U11 crowns. He backed that up with BS and BD U11 titles at the 2025 Can-Am NorCal OLC and additional titles at the NorCal ORC.

Karthigayan's primary partnership with Toby Liu (49 matches) has been extraordinarily prolific, while he also works with Shreya Rajesh Kumar (17 matches) and Kavya Balaji (15 matches). His progression from BS U11 #7 to BS U13 #5 demonstrates seamless elite-level performance across age groups. With his exceptional win rate, multiple titles, and national-level consistency, Karthigayan is one of the premier talents in American junior badminton.
