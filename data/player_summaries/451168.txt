Vaishnavi Packiyaraj is one of the more accomplished U13 girls on the circuit, carrying a 60% career win rate (71-46) across 16 tournaments. The 2014-born player holds five titles, including three U11 singles crowns and two U11 mixed doubles titles from 2024. Currently ranked #25 in U13 doubles and #41 in U13 singles, she peaked at U13 doubles #17 in mid-2025 before a slight dip.

Her partnership with Bryce Xie (25 matches) has been the cornerstone of her doubles game. Recent form has been steady -- 7-3 at the 2024 NorCal ORC, 5-1 at the 2024 NE MassBad OLC -- though she went a more modest 4-4 at the 2025 Midwest ORC. With National Championship experience and a runner-up finish at the 2024 U.S. Junior Nationals in doubles, Packiyaraj has proven she can compete at the highest junior level.
