<p>The 2023 Midwest OLC became Tao's defining moment, showcasing his exceptional versatility and competitive spirit. He dominated the mixed doubles draw alongside partner Anvi Sai Harish to capture the XD U19 championship, then immediately followed that triumph by reaching the boys' doubles final in the same tournament. This remarkable double-final performance demonstrated his ability to excel across multiple disciplines and maintain peak performance under pressure throughout an entire tournament weekend.</p>

<p>Tao's partnership chemistry has been a cornerstone of his success, particularly his 11-match collaboration with Anvi Sai Harish, which has proven to be his most productive doubles combination. <p>What sets Tao apart is his incredible trajectory of improvement – evolving from a 0-4 record in his first tournament to achieving rankings of #100 in boys' singles, #107 in boys' doubles, and #129 in mixed doubles by late 2023.
