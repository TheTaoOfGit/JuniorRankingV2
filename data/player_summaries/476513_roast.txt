<p><b>0-3 in Finals: Kylie Ou Is the Bridesmaid of U11 Girls' Badminton</b></p>

<p>Kylie Ou is ranked GS U11 #7 and holds three runner-up finishes — with zero titles. She's made it to three finals and lost all three. That's a hat trick of heartbreak. She's good enough to beat everyone except the person standing across the net in the championship match.</p>

<p>She went 0-5 at the 2024 Dave Freeman Jr. OLC in her first-ever recorded tournament, which is the kind of welcome to competitive badminton that leaves scars. Her 53% win rate (30-26) is respectable for U11, and her 4-2 three-game record shows real fight — but that 0-3 finals record undermines everything.</p>

<p>Her most frequent partner Bowen Wang has been there for 14 matches, watching Kylie collect silver medals like they're going out of style. At GS U11 #7, she's tantalizingly close to the top — just never quite able to grab it. The view from second place must be getting very familiar.</p>
