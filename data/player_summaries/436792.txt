Kamalini Packiyaraj (b. 2009) is a top-tier U19 player ranked GD U19 #12, XD U19 #35, and GS U19 #42, with an impressive 66% career win rate (100-50) over 18 tournaments. She has captured five titles, including GS U17 and XD U19 at the 2024 NE CRC and GS U15 and GD U15 at the 2023 NE OLC. Her 10-2 record at the 2023 MassBad NE OLC and 8-2 at the 2024 NE MassBad OLC highlight her dominance at northeast events. Packiyaraj's doubles partnerships span multiple players, with Yui Ching Cheung and William Zeng (9 matches each) among her most frequent. With 29,590 points in GD U19, she is firmly established in the upper echelon of the national U19 girls' bracket.
