<p><b>Three-Game Record: 4-16 (20%). Aiden Wu Wins 1 in 5 Close Matches. Even a Blindfolded Player Would Do Better.</b></p>

<p>Aiden Wu's 20% three-game record is one of the worst in junior badminton. Out of 20 close matches, he's won four. FOUR. That means when a match reaches a third game, Aiden has an 80% chance of losing. His 3-8 semis record completes the picture: he can't close out tight matches, and he can't survive pressure situations. His 45% career win rate confirms he's below average even in the easy matches.</p>

<p>His one title — XD U13 at the Dave Freeman Jr. OLC — came after 23 tournaments of disappointment. His two runner-up finishes in singles (both in BS U13) are the closest he's come to individual glory, and he lost both. Crystal Sun has suffered through 23 matches as his partner, enduring one of the worst clutch rates in the sport.</p>

<p>The blowout losses to Anirudh Rudharraju, Melvin Cui, and Arnav Satish reveal a player who gets demolished by anyone ranked above him. Aiden Wu: the anti-clutch.</p>
