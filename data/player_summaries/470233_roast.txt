<p><b>3-14 in Semis Despite 3 Titles: Boqian Zheng Can't Get Out of His Own Way</b></p>

<p>Boqian Zheng is ranked BD U13 #6 and BS U13 #11 with 3 titles and a 62% career win rate. Sounds elite? His 3-14 semis record is catastrophic. Fourteen semifinal losses. He reaches the semis constantly -- because he's genuinely good -- and then loses 82% of the time. His three-game record of 13-17 (43%) confirms that when matches get tight, Boqian finds a way to implode.</p>

<p>All three titles came in BD U11, including back-to-back wins at Nationals. Since moving to U13, he's been titleless, despite being ranked in the top 15 in all three disciplines. His 3-0 finals record looks perfect until you realize he only reaches finals 3 out of 17 semifinal attempts -- that's an 18% conversion rate from semis to finals.</p>

<p>His partner Magadheesan Velu (25 matches) has experienced this semis curse firsthand. Getting blown out by Siddharth Kuchi and Yashas Cheerlavancha in singles shows the gap between his ranking and his performance in key moments. He's the most talented choker in U13 boys' badminton.</p>
