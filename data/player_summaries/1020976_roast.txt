<p><b>Restaurant Review: Andy Gao</b> — A neighborhood establishment that's been open for 29 service days (82-89, 47%) and remains stubbornly below the break-even line. BD U19 #69, BS U19 #71, XD U19 #123 — these are the Yelp ratings of a restaurant that survives on location rather than cuisine.</p>

<p>The one Zagat-worthy dish: the BD U15 title at the 2023 NW OLC. Three runner-up finishes, including the BD U17 loss at the 2024 ABC OLC, prove the kitchen can produce when the ingredients align. Partner Vignesh Jami (18 matches) and Benjamin Zhuge (14 matches) form the prep crew.</p>

<p>The 2024 Midwest ORC (8-3) was the pop-up dinner that had everyone talking, but the 2024 NorCal ORC (1-3), 2023 Northwest ORC (1-3), and 2022 Junior Nationals (2-4) are the regular service nights that empty the dining room. At 47% with one title in 29 events, this restaurant needs a new head chef or a menu overhaul — preferably both.</p>
