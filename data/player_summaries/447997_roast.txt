His current U17 singles ranking of 847 means there are literally 846 teenagers who wake up every morning knowing they're better at hitting a shuttlecock than he is. But here's where it gets truly impressive: somehow, this statistical anomaly has managed to convince not one, not two, but five different doubles partners to step onto the court with him, including the seemingly patient Ishan Vinothkumar, who inexplicably returned for three separate beatings.</p>

In a sport where the net is only five feet high, Samarth has somehow found a way to consistently aim lower than that.</p>

<p>And yet, against all statistical probability and common sense, he keeps showing up.
