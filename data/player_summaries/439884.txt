Yashas Cheerlavancha (b. 2013) is one of the South region's most accomplished U15 players, ranked BD U15 No. 42, BS U15 No. 29, and XD U15 No. 103 with a 62% career win rate (118-72 across 23 tournaments). He has collected seven titles spanning U11 and U13 in both singles and doubles, including BS U13 crowns at the 2024 South OLC and 2025 NE OLC. His 9-3 record at the 2025 Midwest ORC showcases his ability to compete at major events. The long-running partnership with Surya Peechara (27 matches) is one of the most productive in the junior doubles circuit.
