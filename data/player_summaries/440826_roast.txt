<p><b>Zero Titles, Zero Finals, 0-7 in Semis. David Zhang Is the Gatekeeper Nobody Asked For.</b></p>

<p>David Zhang has played 23 tournaments and has exactly zero titles, zero finals appearances, and a perfect 0-7 semis record. He's the most reliable semifinalist in junior badminton — reliably terrible. Seven times he's reached the final four, and seven times he's been told to go home. At this point, the semis should have a restraining order against him.</p>

<p>His 45% career win rate means he loses more than he wins, and his 14 matches with Derek Huang have produced precisely nothing of consequence. The blowout losses to Aayush Jaladurgam (twice — in singles AND mixed doubles) suggest Aayush treats David like a personal punching bag.</p>

<p>At BD U13 #27 and BS U13 #29, David is ranked respectably enough to keep getting invited to tournaments, but not well enough to ever win one. He's the participation trophy of the rankings — always there, never relevant.</p>
