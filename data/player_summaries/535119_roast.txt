<p>But here's the thing about this racket game—everyone starts somewhere, and Arjun's somewhere just happened to be at the bottom of a very deep well. In this city of broken dreams and early exits, sometimes the real victory isn't winning—it's having the guts to step back on court when the scoreboard keeps telling you to stay home. Case closed, but the file stays open.</p>
