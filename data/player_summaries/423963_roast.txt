<p><b>31% three-game win rate. 5W-5L in finals. Chiranjeev Deepak: dominant record, paper-thin mentality.</b></p>

<p>A 66% career win rate and 5 titles look great on paper. Then you see the 7W-15L three-game record and everything makes sense. Chiranjeev wins 31% of close matches. That's not choking -- that's a medical condition. He wins easy matches comfortably and collapses the instant someone pushes him to three games.</p>

<p>His partnership with Anshu Gowda (61 matches) is one of the most prolific in junior badminton, which just means they've had 61 opportunities to underperform together. Blown out by Yian Yang twice. When everyone in your region knows your weakness, the weakness becomes the identity.</p>
