Trisha Mehta (b. 2013) is a dominant force in the U15 bracket, ranked GS U15 #25, GD U15 #32, and XD U15 #34, with a 69% career win rate (132-59) across 22 tournaments. She has won six titles, including GS U15 and GD U15 at the 2026 Wayside NE CRC and XD U13 at the 2025 LIBC Northeast ORC where she posted a remarkable 12-2 record. Mehta also swept the 2023 MassBad NE OLC at 10-0. Her key partnerships include Anshu Gowda (19 matches) in mixed doubles and Vindhya Chekuru (13 matches) in doubles. Consistently reaching finals across all three disciplines, Mehta is one of the most well-rounded players in her age group.
