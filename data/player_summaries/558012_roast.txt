Her mixed doubles ranking of #71 in U11 suggests the restaurant might actually work better as a collaborative dining experience, but with 363 points spread across five age groups, it's clear this establishment is trying to serve too many demographics at once.</p>

Two stars because the potential is there, and honestly, the dedication to keep showing up after those early reviews takes guts. Come back in a few years — this might just turn into the surprise hit of the junior circuit.</p>
