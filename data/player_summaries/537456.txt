Alison Liu has burst onto the U11 girls' scene with impressive results in her first three tournaments, posting a 15-8 record (65%) to earn rankings of #24 in doubles (871pts), #16 in singles (871pts), and #33 in mixed doubles. The 2017-born player captured her first title at the 2026 Bintang NorCal OLC in girls' doubles, where she went a dominant 8-2 overall. Liu has formed a strong mixed doubles partnership with Tyler Jiang (7 matches) and has also paired effectively with Saanvi Jha and Elina Xiao-En Yang in doubles. Her immediate impact at competitive events marks her as a rising talent to watch in the U11 division.
