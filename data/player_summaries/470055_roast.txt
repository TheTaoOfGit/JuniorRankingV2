She went 0-2 at the NorCal ORC, 0-4 at the CAN-AM NorCal OLC, and 0-2 at the NorCal ORC again. Zero titles, zero finals, zero semis. She has the consistency of a broken clock, except a broken clock is right twice a day and Anahita wins twice every 12 matches.</p>

<p>Her three-game record of 3-3 (50%) is actually average, which is her best stat. Her partner Pranay Sundar (8 matches) has stuck around through the drought. <p>From GS U13 #74 to GS U15 #48, her ranking improved while her win rate stayed abysmal. The ranking system genuinely does not care about winning percentage, and Anahita is its poster child.</p>
