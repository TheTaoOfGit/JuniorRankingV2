<p><b>0-1 in Finals: Adhira Raja Reaches the Big Stage and Trips</b></p>

<p>Adhira Raja has a 40% win rate at 6-9 and an 0-1 finals record, losing to Mokshagna Surpur in the XD at Peak Sports. She had the bracket, she had the partner, she had the opportunity -- and she blew it. Her 2-0 three-game record at 100% is genuinely impressive and makes the finals loss even more baffling: she can win the close ones but apparently freezes when a trophy is on the line.</p>

<p>Her 1-4 debut at Fortius was ugly, and while 5-5 at Peak Sports looked like growth, that included the finals choke. Three blowout losses including one in the same XD format she reached the final in tells a story of wild inconsistency -- she can beat some XD teams convincingly while getting demolished by others.</p>

<p>With three partners across two tournaments, she's already building a roster. Her doubles partner Aadhya Lingareddy has been along for four matches, sharing in both the highs of making a final and the lows of getting blown out by Samyutha Aluri.</p>
