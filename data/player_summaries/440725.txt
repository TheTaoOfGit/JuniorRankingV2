<p>Gadige's rapid progression through age groups demonstrates her fearless approach to competition and hunger for challenging opponents. Her current U15 rankings of #289 in singles and #315 in doubles, built on a solid foundation of 350 points each, reflect consistent tournament participation and steady improvement.</p>
