<p>Edward Chang has a 38% career win rate (20-32) across 11 tournaments. He went 0-4 at the 2024 Midwest ORC and 1-4 at the 2025 Midwest ORC — one year apart, one fewer loss, and somehow that counts as progress. His three-game record of 3-5 (37%) means tight matches are his enemy.</p>

<p>He did win one title — XD U19 at the 2026 Wayside NE CRC — which sounds impressive until you realize the NE CRC is where rankings go to be inflated. His 1-1 semis record and 1-0 finals record are propped up entirely by that single result.</p>

<p>He's cycled through partners like Ryan Chan, Jasmine Cheung, Michael Li, and Olivia Liu — five different frequent partners in 11 tournaments.
