<p><b>0-3 in Semis With Zero Finals: Joannah Stephen, the Eternal Semifinalist Who Can't Even Do That Right</b></p>

<p>Joannah Stephen has played 8 tournaments and lost all three of her semifinal appearances. Zero finals. Zero titles. Zero runner-ups. Her 14-22 career record (38%) suggests she shows up mostly to give other players confidence boosts. She went 0-2 at the 2025 Bellevue Northwest ORC -- two matches, two losses, one wasted weekend.</p>

<p>Her entire doubles career is with Adelaide Almeida (15 matches), forming what might be the most loyal yet fruitless partnership in junior badminton. They've been blown out together and they've lost together, but at least they have each other. Her blowout losses include getting destroyed by Allison Hsu -- twice, in different age groups. Hsu apparently enjoys the tradition.</p>

<p>On the bright side, her three-game record of 2-1 (66%) shows she can actually win close matches. The problem is she almost never gets to play close matches because she's getting blown out first.</p>
