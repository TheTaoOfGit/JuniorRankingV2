<p><b>4-6 in Finals Despite 10-5 Semis: Catherine Chen Destroys the Semis Then Chokes in the Final</b></p>

<p>Catherine Chen has a 10-5 semifinals record — dominant — and then a 4-6 finals record. She's a semis assassin who turns into a championship disaster. Ten semifinal wins and only 4 titles means she's converting less than half her finals opportunities. That's not a finals record, it's a psychological condition.</p>

<p>Her 14-7 three-game record (66%) is excellent, and her 58% overall win rate proves she's competitive. But 6 finals losses across 12 tournaments is a staggering amount of heartbreak. She went 3-5 at the 2024 Midwest OLC and 2-4 at the 2024 Midwest ORC — the big events humble her.</p>

<p>Partner Rachel Yuezhi Xu has been there for 26 matches, forming a partnership that gets to the biggest stage and then consistently falls short. Four titles is respectable — but Catherine should have twice as many given how often she's in the final. The gap between her talent and her trophy case is wide enough to park a shuttle bus in.</p>
