Jade Hu (b. 2011) is a top-tier U17 prospect ranked GD U17 No. 38, GS U17 No. 59, and XD U17 No. 70, placing her among the strongest all-around female players in her age group. Her 54% career win rate (58-48 in 15 tournaments) includes a GD U15 title at the 2024 South OLC. She went 6-3 at the 2025 Northeast ORC and 5-4 at the 2025 Austin Leander South OLC. Her progression from GD U13 No. 44 to GD U17 No. 38 has been impressive. Diksha Chandrasekaran (15 matches) and Yifei Zhang (13 matches) form her primary doubles partnerships.
