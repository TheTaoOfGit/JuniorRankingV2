Zack Nguyen, born in 2015, is ranked BS U13 #60, BD U13 #84, and XD U13 #150 with a strong 41-26 career record (61%) across 11 tournaments. He was a dominant U11 player, capturing three titles including BD U11 and XD U11 at the 2024 SoCal CRC (where he went 11-1) and BD U11 at the 2025 SGVBC SoCal CRC. His longtime mixed doubles partnership with Joanna Zhang (15 matches) has been one of the most productive in the age group. Nguyen also works with Keaton Ou (10 matches) in boys' doubles. His transition to U13 has seen a slight dip in results, but his championship pedigree suggests he will adapt.
