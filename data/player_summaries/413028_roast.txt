<p><b>72% three-game win rate. 14W-8L in finals. Bella Liu is annoyingly clutch -- so why is she only GS #11?</b></p>

<p>Here's the paradox of Bella Liu: she wins 72% of three-game matches (elite), she's 14-8 in finals (dominant), she's got a 70% career win rate across 26 tournaments.
