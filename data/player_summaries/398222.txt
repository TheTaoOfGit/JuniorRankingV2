His dominant 8-3 record at the Dave Freeman tournament, followed by an impressive 9-3 showing at the NE OLC, demonstrated his ability to peak when championships are on the line. These victories added to an already impressive trophy collection that includes titles dating back to his U11 days, where he swept both Boys and Mixed Doubles at the 2022 SoCal CRC.</p>

Whether reaching the Boys Singles U17 final at this year's Dave Freeman Jr. SoCal OLC or advancing deep into draws at prestigious events like the U.S. Junior National Championships, Ly consistently finds ways to exceed expectations.</p>
