Sasha Suman is ranked GD U19 #11 (31,300 pts), GS U19 #18 (19,110 pts), and XD U19 #24 (23,550 pts), placing her among the top women's players in U.S. junior badminton. Her 166-89 career record (65%) across 31 tournaments includes seven titles, highlighted by GD U17 at the 2025 Junior Nationals, XD U17 at the 2025 Junior Nationals, and GD U17 at the 2024 Northeast ORC. Her 16-5 showing at the 2023 Junior Nationals demonstrated her big-event consistency.

Suman's primary partnerships are with Yalan Zhu (33 matches) and Adhyaan Balaji (29 matches), two of the strongest pairings in the junior ranks.
