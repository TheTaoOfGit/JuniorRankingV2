<p><b>From Unranked to Title Winner: But Can He Beat Anyone Good?</b></p>

<p>Harrell Thomas rocketed from BS U11 #126 to BD U11 #27 on the strength of five tournaments and a 52% win rate. That sounds impressive until you notice his sole title came at the 2026 Wayside NE CRC -- a Circuit Ranking Championship -- where he beat Henry Qiu and Jayden Qiu in the final. Not exactly slaying dragons.</p>

<p>At the ORCs against stiffer competition, reality bites: 0-2 at LIBC Northeast and 0-2 at the 2025 NE OLC. His blowout losses to Franklin Xu Cheng and Neel Achari suggest the ceiling is very much still above him. A 1-0 finals record looks great until you realize the one final was at the smallest possible event.</p>

<p>His 66% three-game rate is legitimately solid, but with a 1-2 semis record, he's proof that clutch in the third game doesn't always translate to clutch when the stakes rise. Partner Mahith Jajula has been along for 7 of his matches -- loyalty or lack of options?</p>
