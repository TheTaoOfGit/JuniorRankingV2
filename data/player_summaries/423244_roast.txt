The kid bounced around partners like he was speed dating at a country club: Neel J Reddy, Yifei Zhang, Aditya Ramakrishn Hariharan, Ava Ilena Raju Cimala, Anya Virmani. In this city of broken dreams and shuttlecock schemes, sometimes that's all a detective – or a junior badminton player – can ask for.</p>
