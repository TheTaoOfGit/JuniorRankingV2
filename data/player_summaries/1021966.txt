Zimeng Zhou is a top U19 competitor ranked XD U19 #16 (27,750pts), GD U19 #18, and GS U19 #15, making her one of the highest-ranked players across all three disciplines. Despite an even 50% career record (115-111) over 35 tournaments, Zhou has four titles, all in mixed doubles and girls' singles at the regional level. Her primary partner Alex Anlai Song (40 matches) anchors her doubles game, while Jocelyn Zhang (28 matches) is another key collaborator. Zhou's rise from GS U17 #45 to GS U19 #15 represents one of the more dramatic ranking improvements in the circuit.
