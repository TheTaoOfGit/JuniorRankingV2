<p><b>1-7: The Three-Game Mirage</b></p>

<p>Siddhartha Garimella boasts a 100% three-game record (1-0), which sounds amazing until you realize he's 1-7 overall with a 12% win rate. He won one three-gamer and lost everything else in straight games. That single clutch win is like finding a diamond in a dumpster fire -- technically impressive but the surroundings are still a disaster.</p>

<p>At U.S. Junior Nationals he went 0-4. At Synergy NorCal, 0-2. The blowout losses to Toby Liu, Nandan Mallela, Jake Shi, and Tanush Jaltar suggest Siddhartha's game has all the structural integrity of wet cardboard. His lone partner Aayan Paruthi endured 3 matches of this. No current rankings. Three tournaments. The definition of "blink and you'll miss it."</p>
