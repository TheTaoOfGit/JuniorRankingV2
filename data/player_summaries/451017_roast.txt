<p><b>10-13 Finals Record. Former Triple #1. Laasya Polineni Is the Most Decorated Loser in U13 History.</b></p>

<p>Laasya Polineni was GD U11 #1, GS U11 #1, and XD U11 #1 — a former triple crown holder who has since accumulated a 10-13 finals record. She reaches finals more than almost anyone and loses more of them too. Thirteen finals losses is a staggering number that screams "I can get to the championship but I can't seal the deal." Her 23-5 semis record is elite, making the finals collapse even more baffling.</p>

<p>Her 51% three-game record (17-16) is barely above a coin flip for a former #1 player. Virat Satish (43 matches) is her ride-or-die partner, and together they form the most-finaled, most-runner-upped partnership in junior badminton. Ten titles is impressive; thirteen finals losses is more impressive in the wrong direction.</p>

<p>The blowout losses to Leo Ye, Crystal Yao, and Vindhya Chekuru show she can still get demolished by her peers. She went from triple #1 to GD U13 #12 and GS U13 #13 — a decline from the summit that's still ongoing. Laasya: the most talented player who can't stop finishing second.</p>
