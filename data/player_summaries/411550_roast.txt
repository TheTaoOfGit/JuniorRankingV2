<p><b>11W-15L in finals. Keira Winarto has lost more finals than most players have entered.</b></p>

<p>Twenty-six finals appearances and Keira lost fifteen of them. She's the Susan Lucci of junior badminton -- always there, always heartbroken. Her 52% three-game win rate across 46 three-game matches means she's a coin flip when it counts. Ranked in seven different discipline-age combinations, which sounds impressive until you realize it just means she enters everything and loses finals in all of them.</p>

<p>Her partner situation tells its own story: 54 matches with Varish Karri, 43 with Lilian Feng. That's called codependency, not chemistry. She's got 11 titles which is genuinely good, but the 15 finals losses haunt every one of them. At the 2022 Junior Nationals she went 6W-6L. Perfectly balanced. Perfectly mediocre.</p>

<p>Blown out by Alice Meng twice. When someone owns you like that, it stops being a rivalry and starts being a subscription service.</p>
