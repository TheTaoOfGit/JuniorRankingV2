His doubles game has been even more impressive, with his partnership alongside Mihir Madiman becoming one of the most consistent pairings in the region across six competitive matches.</p>

<p>Strayer's fearless approach to competition shines through his willingness to test himself against the nation's elite. This experience has clearly paid dividends, as evidenced by his remarkable ranking surge from #97 to #37 in boys' doubles U19 between February 2025 and March 2026.</p>

<p>What sets Strayer apart is his versatility across all three disciplines, currently ranked #38 in boys' singles, #37 in boys' doubles, and #75 in mixed doubles at the U19 level.
