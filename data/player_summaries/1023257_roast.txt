<p><b>Semis record: 19-21. Aditya Namboodiripad loses more semifinals than he wins. For a former BS U11 #1, that's a spectacular fall from grace.</b></p>

<p>Aditya was once the #1 singles player in U11. Now he's BS U15 #13 and BD U15 #16, which is the ranking equivalent of peaking in middle school. 28 tournaments, 69% win rate, 11 titles -- all sounds great until you see those 21 semifinal losses. He reaches the semis constantly and then gets bounced like a bad check.</p>

<p>The three-game record of 41-29 at 58% means he wins more than he loses in close ones, but 29 three-game losses is a staggering number. The blowout losses include getting demolished by Ethan Qiu three separate times in mixed doubles. Ethan essentially has a standing reservation to destroy Aditya.</p>

<p>37 matches with Sai Ratan Shankar, 30 with Sitar Sanghani. At this point the partners deserve a support group. Aditya's trajectory says "plateaued" in all caps.</p>
