<p><b>0-3 in Semis: Alyssa Lee Can Smell the Finals but Can't Taste Them</b></p>

<p>Alyssa Lee has made it to the semifinals three times and lost every single one, posting a pristine 0-3 record when it matters most. She's 5-12 overall at 29%, which means she's losing nearly three out of every four matches, yet somehow keeps stumbling into semis. That takes a special kind of draw luck combined with an even more special kind of choking ability.</p>

<p>Ranked #77 in GD, #113 in GS, and #113 in XD U15, she's triple-ranked in the land of "nobody cares." Her 0-2 at the Yonex Synergy was a particularly efficient display of futility -- showed up, lost twice, went home. At least she didn't waste anyone's time.</p>

<p>Her three-game record of 2-1 at 66% is actually her best stat, which is ironic because it means she's better when she's already in trouble. Maybe she should start every match down a game to activate her clutch gene. Five different partners in four tournaments is speed-dating for badminton, and the blowout losses to Sienna Lee and Samyukta Karthik suggest the U15 field views her as free points.</p>
