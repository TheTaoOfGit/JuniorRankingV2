Daniel Fong is one of the most versatile U17 players in the country, ranked #37 in doubles, #61 in singles, and #86 in mixed doubles. The 2011-born competitor boasts five titles across three disciplines, including a perfect 9-0 sweep at the 2025 OBA NW OLC where he claimed BD U17, XD U15, and BS U15 crowns. He added a BS U17 title at the 2026 Dave Freeman Jr. SoCal OLC.

With a 52% career win rate across 23 tournaments (77-71), Fong brings consistency and big-match pedigree. His 6-3 outing at the 2025 Northeast ORC showed he can compete nationally. He rotates effectively among several doubles partners, including Felicia Ho (10 matches), Kaitlyn Kim (9 matches), and Nathaniel Volfbeyn (8 matches), demonstrating adaptability that bodes well for continued success.
