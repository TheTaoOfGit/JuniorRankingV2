Leslie Wang (b. 2014) ranks BD U13 #9 and BS U13 #13, establishing himself as one of the top U13 boys nationally. With a 61% career win rate (138-85) across 26 tournaments, he has claimed three titles at the 2025 SoCal CRC -- XD U13, BD U13, and BS U13 -- demonstrating triple-threat ability. His defining partnership is with brother Jeremy Wang (50 matches), one of the deepest sibling pairings in junior badminton. Wang also pairs with Regina Lee (24 matches) in mixed doubles and Zelenia Shen (13 matches) in doubles. His six runner-up finishes, including BD U11 at the 2024 Midwest ORC, show he consistently reaches the latter stages of draws. He posted a strong 8-3 at the 2024 NorCal ORC.
