<p><b>0-6 in Semis: Emma Qin Has Made a Career Out of Losing When It Matters</b></p>

<p>Emma Qin has an almost-respectable 48% career win rate across 9 tournaments, which just means she wins enough to get her hopes up before reality kicks in. Her signature stat is that 0-6 semifinals record — six times she's knocked on the door of a final, and six times the door has been slammed in her face. At some point, you have to wonder if the door is even real.</p>

<p>She has zero titles and zero runner-up finishes. She can't even lose in a final because she can't get there. Her blowout losses include getting demolished by players in U15 — her own age group — which removes the "I was playing up" excuse entirely.</p>

<p>Sri Nidhi Vasamsetti has been her ride-or-die doubles partner for 15 matches, making them the most committed losing duo in Midwest junior badminton. Together they've perfected the art of the semifinal collapse.</p>
