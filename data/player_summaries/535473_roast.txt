<p><b>8-30: The Human Participation Trophy</b></p>

<p>Xintong Lu has played eleven tournaments and won eight matches. Eight. Out of thirty-eight. That's a 21% win rate, which means nearly four out of every five opponents walk away from Xintong feeling great about themselves. He's basically a confidence booster for the entire U11 field.</p>

<p>The recent results are a masterclass in futility: 0-4 at SoCal ORC, 0-3 at LIBC, 0-3 at Midwest ORC, 0-3 at DFW South ORC. That's four straight tournaments without a single win. Srinivas Shankar has blown him out TWICE in the blowout losses, which means Xintong didn't learn from the first time. His three-game record is 1-2 at 33%, and he's 0-1 in semis -- though how he even reached a semi is one of badminton's great mysteries.</p>

<p>Six different doubles partners across eleven tournaments. At this point, the partner rotation isn't strategy -- it's a support group.</p>
