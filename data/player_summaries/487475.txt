Devaansh Mahato is one of the more accomplished U13 players in the country, ranked BS U13 #19, XD U13 #26, and BD U13 #42 after a dominant U11 campaign. The 2015-born competitor has collected three titles -- BD U11 at the 2025 ABC OLC and two XD U11 crowns at the 2025 CAN-AM NorCal OLC and Dave Freeman Jr. SoCal OLC -- plus four runner-up finishes. His 77-46 career record (62% win rate) across 15 tournaments reflects elite-level consistency. Mahato's primary partnership with Venba Senkadir (24 matches) has been his mixed doubles anchor, while he works with Timothy Xu and Justin Yang in boys' doubles. His 9-2 showing at the 2025 CAN-AM NorCal OLC stands as his most dominant single tournament.
