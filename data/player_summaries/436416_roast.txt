<p><b>Restaurant: "Yuki Guan's -- Small Kitchen, Big Titles."</b> Eight titles from 16 tournaments is a Michelin-per-tournament ratio that most chefs only dream about. Yuki Guan's 65% satisfaction rate (77-41) and the trophy case spanning GD, GS, and XD across SoCal CRC and Dave Freeman Jr. events prove this kitchen has mastered the local cuisine. The GS U11 at SoCal CRC 2023 was the opening night that sold out, and the 9W-2L at SoCal CRC 2024 was the follow-up that earned the second star.</p>

<p>The menu has expanded: GD U13 #48, GS U13 #39, and even XD U15 #136 show the age-up transition is underway. Partners Joey Wang (14 matches), Eason Chin (10 matches), and Angie Lu (9 matches) keep the kitchen running across disciplines. The 8W-1L at Dave Freeman Jr. 2026 -- including GS U13 at that event -- is the most recent rave review.</p>

<p>The challenge: the 0W-3L at SoCal ORC 2023 and 1W-3L at SoCal ORC 2026 show this kitchen struggles when the competition level reaches ORC intensity. The SoCal CRC and OLC circuit is where the magic happens. Outside that comfort zone, the soufflé sometimes collapses.</p>
