Adhira Raja is an U13 girls player ranked No. 85 in singles and No. 116 in doubles, with a 6-9 career record from two South region tournaments. Her most notable result was a runner-up finish in XD U13 at the 2025 Peak Sports South OLC, where she posted a 5-5 record overall. Raja has built partnerships with Aadhya Lingareddy in doubles (four matches) and Magadheesan Velu in mixed (three matches). The 2015-born player showed she can compete at the OLC level and has mixed doubles as a clear strength.
