<p><b>10-2 at a CRC, 0-3 at an ORC: Tia Chen Only Wins When It Doesn't Count</b></p>

<p>Tia Chen went 10-2 at the SGVBC CRC and probably thought she was the next big thing, then reality came crashing down with an 0-3 at the Yonex Arena ORC. Her career breaks down perfectly: dominate the low-stakes local events, fold completely when the competition actually matters. With a 51% win rate across four tournaments, she's the definition of average dressed up in selective memory.</p>

<p>Ranked #65 in GD, #83 in GS, and #167 in XD U13, that XD ranking is particularly hilarious -- she's so far down the list she's practically in a different zip code from relevance. Her 0-1 semis record keeps the tradition alive of wilting under pressure, and her blowout losses to Paris Liang, Grace Park, and Yuki Guan show that anyone with real talent sends her packing quickly.</p>

<p>Five different partners across four tournaments means Tia is either incredibly social or nobody wants to commit to a second tour of duty. Her three-game record of 3-1 is genuinely decent, which makes it all the more painful that she keeps getting blown out before three games are even necessary.</p>
