<p><b>0-3 in Finals: Parnika Thakare, the Bridesmaid of the Northeast</b></p>

<p>Parnika Thakare has reached 3 finals and lost all 3. Her semis record of 3-0 means she's literally unbeatable in semifinals and then completely beatable in finals. It's like she hits a wall specifically labeled "Final Round" and runs straight into it every time.</p>

<p>Her 20-17 career (54%) is respectable, and her 6-3 three-game record (66%) shows she's competitive. But losing finals to Rachel Yao, Edward Chang, and Jessica Zhang -- and never winning one -- defines her career more than any other stat. She went 4-6 at Junior Nationals, which is fine but not the kind of result that earns titles.</p>

<p>At GS U19 #31, she's ranked high enough to be expected to win but never actually does when it counts. The gap between "ranked" and "champion" is called Parnika Thakare.</p>
