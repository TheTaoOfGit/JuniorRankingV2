<p><b>Five Titles and 5-6 in Finals — Aarav Gupta Wins Big But Loses Bigger.</b></p>

<p>Aarav Gupta is BD U17 #14 and BS U17 #18, which is genuinely impressive until you see his 5-6 finals record. He reaches championship matches constantly and then loses more than he wins. His 53% three-game record confirms he's average in tight situations, and his 12-6 semis record means the damage only happens in the final. He's the player who always makes the show but can't write the ending.</p>

<p>His blowout losses in XD are spectacular — Srinivas Raghav Subash, Allen Yang, and Renee Liu all demolished him in mixed doubles, suggesting that discipline is his kryptonite. He compensates by dominating in singles (BS U15 titles at the South CRC and South ORC), but the mixed doubles record is a gaping wound in an otherwise solid resume.</p>

<p>With 19 matches alongside Saee Thorat and 17 with Abhiram Tripuraneni, Aarav cycles through partners looking for the one who'll fix his finals problem. Spoiler: the problem isn't the partner.</p>
