<p><b>2-4 in Semis: Wins Finals She Rarely Reaches</b></p>

<p>Isha Suresh has a 2-0 finals record, which sounds great until you see her 2-4 semis record. She wins when she makes the final, but getting there is the problem -- she fails two-thirds of the time. It's like being great at parking but terrible at driving.</p>

<p>Her 50-32 career (60%) is legit, but her three-game record of 7-7 (50%) is a perfect coin flip. She's been blown out by Aayush Jaladurgam, Krishnavi Kasu, and Venba Senkadir -- the who's who of U11/U13. The transition from U11 titles to U13 anonymity has been swift and merciless.</p>
