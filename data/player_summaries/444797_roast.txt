<p><b>Career Record: 38-44 (46%). Two Titles After 15 Tournaments of Mediocrity. Bavish Dornala Is a Late Bloomer — Emphasis on "Late."</b></p>

<p>Bavish Dornala spent his entire career below .500 before suddenly winning two titles — one in XD U15 and one in BD U15 — in 2025-2026. His 46% overall win rate, however, hasn't recovered from years of losing. He started at 0-4 in his first tournament (2023 Midwest OLC) and has been clawing back ever since. His 2-2 finals record and 4-1 semis record are actually solid, suggesting a recent hot streak propping up a cold career.</p>

<p>The blowout losses to Kiyansh Chaudhary, Melvin Cui, and Sai Ratan Shankar tell the tale of someone who gets absolutely demolished by top competition. His 18 matches with Nithesh Madishetti make them the Midwest's most persistently average doubles team — two players who refuse to be good or bad.</p>

<p>At 60% three-game record, Bavish has the mental game for close matches. He just can't avoid getting blown out in the ones that aren't close.</p>
