Srineet Atluri, born 2012, holds an astonishing eight rankings, headlined by BD U15 #2 (12,976pts), XD U15 #4, BS U15 #4, and BS U17 #21. His 72% win rate (261-100) over 34 tournaments reflects sustained elite performance. He has eight titles, including BD U15 at the 2026 South ORC, XD U15 at the 2026 SoCal ORC, and wins at the 2022 Junior Nationals. His 13-1 record at the 2022 Nationals was dominant. The Atluri-Stephanie Song partnership (71 matches) is one of the most prolific in the sport, complemented by his equally productive pairing with Mikael Chang (70 matches). Atluri's ability to rank in the top tier of both U15 and U17/U19 categories marks him as one of the most complete young players in American badminton.
