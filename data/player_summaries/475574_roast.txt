<p><b>3 Titles and Rising Fast: Aiden Yu's Only Weakness Is Getting Blown Out by Other 10-Year-Olds</b></p>

<p>Aiden Yu has 3 titles, a 3-1 finals record, and a solid 53% win rate — which for a 2016-born U11 player is actually impressive. But let's not get carried away. His blowout losses include getting demolished by Peter Lei in BD, by Keaton Ou in XD, and by Chris Lin and Ayaan Kale — meaning the top tier of U11 boys has already measured him and found him wanting.</p>

<p>His three-game record of 4-4 (50%) is a coin flip, and his semis record of 4-2 shows he can advance but not dominate. He went 0-3 at the 2024 Midwest OLC, which means the first time he showed up to a big event, he got sent home empty-handed in record time.</p>

<p>Partner Matthew Alexander Lau has been by his side for 13 matches — their longest doubles partnership — suggesting Aiden at least has the social skills to keep someone around. Whether those 3 titles are the start of something big or the peak remains to be seen.</p>
