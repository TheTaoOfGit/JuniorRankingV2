<p>Her breakthrough performance came at the 2024 NE MASSBAD OLC, where Xu dominated with an 8-3 record that showcased her ability to sustain excellence across multiple days of competition. This wasn't a fluke—she followed it up with a gutsy 5-6 showing at the prestigious 2025 YONEX U.S. Junior National Championships, proving she belongs among America's elite junior players. Even her recent tournaments demonstrate consistent competitiveness, including a solid 2-2 performance at the 2026 YONEX DFW BADMINTON SOUTH ORC.</p>

<p>While her overall 24-31 record might suggest work in progress, the trajectory tells a different story entirely.
