<p><b>0-3 in Semis: Clovee Chen Is the Semifinals' Biggest Fan (and Victim)</b></p>

<p>Clovee Chen has reached the semifinals three times and lost all three, joining the exclusive club of players who consistently prove they belong in the top four but never the top two. With a 31% career win rate at 7-15, she's losing more than twice as often as she's winning, which makes those three semis appearances feel less like achievement and more like a scheduling error.</p>

<p>She went 0-2 at the Yonex Synergy ORC -- a tournament so brief for her it barely counted as exercise. Her partner Alyssa Lee shares both her doubles ranking (#77 and #78 in GD U15) and her inability to win when it matters, making them the most perfectly matched losing duo in U15 badminton.</p>

<p>Her three-game record of 2-1 at 66% is actually respectable, which makes her semis collapses even more confusing. She can win tight matches against normal opponents but apparently enters a fugue state when semifinal stakes are on the line. Four blowout losses across all three disciplines confirm she's an equal-opportunity victim.</p>
