His ability to adapt his game was on full display as he also reached the Boys' Singles U15 final and Mixed Doubles U15 final at the same tournament, showcasing rare versatility across all three disciplines. This triple-final appearance marked him as one of the most complete junior players in the region.</p>

His willingness to compete with both male and female partners demonstrates tactical flexibility and court intelligence beyond his years, while his 59% career win rate reflects consistent excellence across different playing styles and partnerships.</p>

<p>Goh's steady climb through the rankings tells the story of a player committed to continuous improvement.
