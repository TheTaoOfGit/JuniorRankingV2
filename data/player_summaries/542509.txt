Sahasra Vudum has emerged as a top-15 U11 girls' player, ranked #14 in doubles (1,534pts), #15 in singles (967pts), and #31 in mixed doubles (872pts) -- a dramatic rise from outside the top 60 a year ago. The 2017-born competitor boasts a 26-24 record (52%) across eight tournaments and has captured two titles at the 2026 CBA Midwest OLC, winning both girls' singles (defeating Jayasmi Dhruvaraj) and mixed doubles (with Aaditya Lanka). She also reached the girls' doubles final at the 2026 Bintang NorCal OLC. Her dominant 8-0 run at the CBA Midwest OLC was a career highlight. Vudum's most frequent partnerships include Aaditya Lanka (7 matches) in mixed doubles and Vedha Chegireddy and Saanvi Jha (4 matches each) in girls' doubles.
