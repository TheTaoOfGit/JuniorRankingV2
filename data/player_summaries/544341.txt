Aadya Patil is one of the top U11 girls' players in the nation, ranked #6 in doubles (2,529pts), #3 in singles (2,337pts), and #9 in mixed doubles (1,721pts). The 2017-born competitor has compiled a 38-25 record (60%) across eight tournaments, consistently reaching championship rounds. Though she has yet to claim her first title, Patil has amassed four runner-up finishes, including the singles final at the 2026 Yonex Arena SoCal ORC (falling to Thea Yuan) and the doubles final at the 2026 DFW South ORC. Her most prolific partnership is with Vihaa Reddy Keesara (12 matches) in girls' doubles, while Abhinav Praveen (10 matches) has been her go-to mixed doubles partner. Patil's consistency across all three disciplines and her ability to compete at the highest ORC level make her a perennial title contender.
