Tao Peng, a 2011-born player, ranks #58 in BD U17, #63 in BS U17, and #82 in XD U17 with a 48% career win rate across 24 tournaments. His breakthrough came at the 2025 ABC OLC, where he posted a strong 9-2 record and claimed the XD U15 title, while also reaching the BD U15 final and BS U15 semifinal. Runner-up finishes at the 2025 Can-Am NorCal OLC in mixed doubles and singles further demonstrate his competitiveness. Peng's most established partnership is with Kenneth Wu (18 matches), while Pranav Vivek (11 matches) and Mihika Jaiswal (11 matches) are key collaborators in doubles and mixed respectively.
