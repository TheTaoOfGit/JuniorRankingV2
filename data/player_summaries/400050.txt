Erica Lin, born in 2011, has emerged as a top-tier U17 competitor with rankings of GD U17 #13, XD U17 #18, GS U17 #33, and strong U19 positions as well. Her 64% career win rate over 22 tournaments is backed by eight titles spanning all three disciplines. Her 2025 campaign was exceptional -- she won XD U15 at both the Yonex Midwest ORC and U.S. Junior National Championships, and added an XD U19 crown at the Egret Midwest OLC.

Lin's most prolific partnership is with Pranav Gyajangi (18 matches in mixed), while Allison Zhao (17+12 matches across name variations) is her primary girls doubles partner. Her rise from GD U13 #47 in late 2023 to GD U17 #13 charts one of the most impressive progressions in the Midwest, and her dual U17/U19 rankings signal readiness for the highest level of junior competition.
