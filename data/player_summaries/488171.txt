Mizuki Ikeuchi is a dominant force in U15 girls' badminton, ranked GS U15 #5 with 9,274 points while also holding GD U15 #19 and GS U17 #21. The 2013-born player boasts a remarkable 92-25 career record (78%) across 11 tournaments and has amassed eight titles, including the GS U13 crown at the 2025 U.S. Junior National Championships and a perfect 10-0 sweep at the 2026 Wayside NE CRC where she won both GS U17 and XD U15. Her rise has been meteoric -- from GS U13 #55 in December 2024 to her current elite status. Ikeuchi's most established partnership is with Alaina Benjamin (14 matches) in girls' doubles, while Arnav Satish (12 matches) and Aayush Jaladurgam (10 matches) serve as mixed doubles partners. She consistently reaches deep rounds at every event she enters.
