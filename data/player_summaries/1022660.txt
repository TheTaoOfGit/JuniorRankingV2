Ishi Reddy is one of the nation's premier U17 girls, ranked GD U17 #3 (21,600pts), GS U17 #3 (20,520pts), and XD U17 #8 (18,225pts). Her 67% win rate (210-102) over 34 tournaments is backed by eight titles, including a dominant 2025 that produced GD U17 and GS U17 crowns at the Midwest CRC, Northeast ORC, and Midwest ORC. She went 10-3 at the 2024 Midwest ORC. Her longtime partnership with Clarinda Jin (68 matches) is one of the most prolific in junior badminton history, and Brendan Chen (22 matches) features in mixed doubles. Reddy's top-3 rankings across all three disciplines mark her as a genuine triple-threat.
