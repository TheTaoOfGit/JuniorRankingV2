Leon Luo, a 2013-born Northeast standout, ranks #14 in BD U15 and #39 in XD U15 with a 66% career win rate across 33 tournaments -- an elite mark for his age group. His 203 career wins and eight titles reflect sustained excellence, from BD U11 victories in 2023 to a BD U15 title at the 2026 Wayside NE CRC. His 2025 included BD U13 gold at the LIBC Northeast ORC and XD U13 at the 2025 South ORC.

Luo's defining partnership is with Ethan Qiu (99 matches), one of the most extensive pairings in all of junior badminton. Lilian Feng (32 matches) and Adriel Ammy Chandran (32 matches) are his primary mixed and girls doubles collaborators respectively. His progression from BD U13 #26 in late 2023 to BD U15 #14 cements his status as a top doubles player at the U15 level.
