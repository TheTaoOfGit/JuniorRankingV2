<p><b>12-13 in Three-Gamers Despite Being Elite: Eesha Vikram Somehow Loses Close Matches at a 48% Rate</b></p>

<p>Eesha Vikram has 8 titles, a 66% win rate, a 14-6 semis record, and is ranked in the top 25 in all three U13 events. She's genuinely good. But her 12-13 three-game record (48%) is an embarrassing blemish on an otherwise excellent resume. When matches go the distance, this supposed elite player loses more than she wins. That's like a surgeon who's great at everything except the part where they sew you back up.</p>

<p>Her 8-6 finals record means she's lost 6 championship matches — proof that she can be beaten when it matters most. She went 12-0 at the 2025 Schafer SLG South OLC, which sounds dominant until you note her 0-3 at the 2026 DFW South ORC, proving that her confidence is as fragile as it is inflated.</p>

<p>Chris Lin has been her XD partner for 42 matches — practically a marriage in junior badminton terms. At least someone's committed, even if Eesha's three-game record isn't.</p>
