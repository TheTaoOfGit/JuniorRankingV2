<p><b>Restaurant Review: Kriti Vasireddy</b> — A well-balanced prix fixe that delivers consistent quality without the fireworks of a destination restaurant. GD U19 #33, GS U19 #32, XD U19 #55 at a 57% rate (110-81 across 21 tournaments) — solid comfort food across the board.</p>

<p>The recent additions to the menu are the most exciting: GD U17 and XD U17 titles at the 2025 ABC OLC show the kitchen finally producing signature dishes. Long-term partnerships with Andy Xu Zhang (28 matches) and Sai Sahasra Gajula (27 matches) form the reliable prep team, while Angelina Lin Wu (22 matches) rounds out the doubles line crew.</p>

<p>The 2023 Junior Nationals (14-6) was a catering event that impressed everyone, but the 2024 NorCal ORC (2-3) and 2021 NorCal CRC (6-4) are the weeknight services where orders get backed up. Zero runner-up finishes is oddly remarkable — Kriti either wins the title or doesn't make the final at all. No silver medals, no heartbreaking losses, just a clean binary: champion or out. Two titles in 21 tournaments is modest, but that 2023 Nationals showing hints at a bigger menu waiting to be unveiled.</p>
