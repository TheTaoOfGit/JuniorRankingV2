<p><b>7-3 Finals and 10-5 Semis: Prarthana Sanghani Dominates U11 Like She's Picking on Kindergartners</b></p>

<p>Prarthana Sanghani is GD U11 #2, GS U11 #4, and XD U11 #2 with 7 titles and a 64% win rate. She went 12-1 at the DFW South ORC and 10-0 at the Peak Sports South OLC. Sounds unstoppable, right? Except her three-game record is 7-9 (43%), meaning when someone actually puts up a fight, she loses more often than she wins.</p>

<p>All her titles are from regional OLCs and CRCs -- Peak Sports South, Capital NE, DFW. She hasn't won at an ORC or Nationals yet, and her 3-3 at the LIBC Northeast ORC and 2-3 at the Midwest ORC show the cracks appear when competition stiffens. She was blown out by Samyutha Aluri, Eesha Vikram, and Xiner Hu -- players who proved the gap between regional dominance and national relevance.</p>

<p>Her 7-3 finals record and 10-5 semis record are impressive, but the three-game kryptonite lingers. She's building a trophy case at the local level that may not survive the transition to U13, where the players she's beating won't be 2 years younger anymore.</p>
