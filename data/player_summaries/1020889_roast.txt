<p><b>Cooking Competition: Aarya Vijay</b> — Chef Aarya's early tasting menus were legendary: 8 titles spanning BD U13, BS U13, BD U15, XD U13, and a BD U15 title at the 2024 Junior Nationals. The 71% satisfaction rate (159-64 across 27 tournaments) and those dominant 2022 campaigns (Junior Nationals 13-4, SPBA MW ORC 9-1) were culinary perfection.</p>

<p>The signature partnership with Vincent Chen (44 matches) is the most decorated co-chef arrangement in this age group. Together they swept U13 events at the 2022 South Frisco ORC and Synergy NorCal ORC. Annie Meng (19 matches) provides the doubles sous-chef support.</p>

<p>But the current menu is curious: BD U17 #16 and BS U17 #4 — strong — yet zero XD rankings and no U19 presence. The 2023 Northwest ORC (7-3) was the last confirmed tournament with full results. Three runner-ups, including the BD U13 loss at the 2022 Junior Nationals to Seth Li and Rithik Rasamsetti, are the souffles that didn't rise. The kitchen clearly has talent; the question is whether it's transitioned from the prodigy menu to the adult one.</p>
