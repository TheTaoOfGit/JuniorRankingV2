Born 2010, his 68-62 career record (52%) across 22 tournaments barely existed in the rankings before mid-2025, when BD U17 #110 and BS U17 #66 first appeared on the docket. By March 2026: BD U17 #59 and BS U17 #33. That is a rapid ascent through the appellate system.</p>
