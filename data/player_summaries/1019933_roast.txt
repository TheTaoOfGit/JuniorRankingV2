<p><b>Video Game Review: Allen Yang</b> — A well-rounded RPG character currently in the middle of a prestige class quest. With 6 active rankings spanning both U17 and U19 divisions (BD U17 #19, BS U17 #8, XD U17 #9 being the highlights), this is a build that spec'd into everything and somehow made it work. The 61% win rate (166-102 across 33 tournaments) is a solid difficulty rating.</p>

<p>The 2024 NorCal ORC (14-2) was the legendary dungeon clear that every gamer brags about. Four titles across BD U15 categories and the XD U17 crown at the 2025 NorCal ORC confirm the DPS output is real. Partner Vivian Feng (44 matches) is the dedicated healer, while Brendan Chen (36 matches) serves as the tank in the doubles raid party.</p>

<p>The inventory has gaps, though: four runner-up finishes and zero U19 titles mean the endgame content remains uncleared. The 2023 Junior Nationals (4-6) and 2023 Northwest ORC (1-2) were the wipe-screen moments. But with XD U17 #9 and BS U17 #8 at age 15, Allen Yang is still in the leveling phase — and the XP curve suggests the best boss fights are ahead.</p>
