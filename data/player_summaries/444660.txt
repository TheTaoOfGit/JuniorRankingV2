Qingyao Liu (b. 2014) is a top-10 U13 player in both singles and doubles, ranked GD U13 No. 7 and GS U13 No. 8 with a 53% career win rate (94-81 across 23 tournaments). Her 13-1 performance at the 2024 NorCal ORC -- where she captured both XD U11 and GD U11 titles -- remains a career highlight. She has climbed from GD U11 No. 21 to the upper echelon of U13, competing consistently at national-level events. Her partnership with Regina Lee (22 matches) is among the most established in the U13 girls' doubles bracket, while Aayush Jaladurgam (10 matches) handles her mixed doubles.
