<p>What sets Chao apart is her remarkable ability to excel whether playing solo or with a partner. Her nine career titles span every discipline and age group from U15 to U19, including prestigious victories at the U.S. Junior National Championships in girls' doubles and multiple ORC tournaments. Her partnership versatility shines through her successful collaborations with players like Justin Xia (31 matches), Charity Lam (28 matches), and Sasha Suman (24 matches), demonstrating an adaptability that's made her one of the most sought-after doubles partners on the junior circuit.</p>

Her breakthrough 2025 season included a girls' doubles title at the U.S. Junior National Championships, while her recent 9-3 performance at the same event showcased her ability to compete at the highest level. The young star's consistency is perhaps most evident in her dominant 14-2 record at the 2023 Midwest ORC, where she captured the girls' singles U15 title.</p>

<p>From ranking outside the top 25 in most categories just three years ago to her current position among America's junior elite, Chao represents the future of U.S. badminton.
