Lydia Chao has established herself as one of the premier players in U.S. junior badminton, currently ranked GS U19 #2 (44,500 pts) and GD U19 #4 (44,750 pts). With a career record of 186-80 (69%) across 27 tournaments, she combines consistency with big-event pedigree. Her eight titles include the 2025 Junior Nationals GD U19, the 2026 South ORC GD U19, and multiple U17 crowns. Her recent form remains sharp, going 7-3 at the 2026 SoCal ORC and reaching the GS U19 final.

Chao's versatility extends across all three disciplines, holding XD U19 #12 ranking as well. She has built productive partnerships with Justin Xia (31 matches), Charity Lam (28), and Sasha Suman (20). Her trajectory from U15 top-10 to U19 top-5 in just over two years marks her as one of the most complete players in the junior ranks.
