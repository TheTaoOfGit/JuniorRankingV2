<p><b>0-2 in Semis, 2-3 in Three-Gamers: Tanvika Kantipudi, the Player Who Peaks One Round Too Early</b></p>

<p>Tanvika Kantipudi has a 26-36 career (41%) and is 0-2 in semis with zero finals appearances across 10 tournaments. She's been blown out by Kira Chu, Xiner Hu, Emily Feng, and Ananya Kumar -- basically every good player in her age group has used her as a stepping stone.</p>

<p>Her partner Ayaan Kale has been along for 14 matches, making them the most prolific unsuccessful XD pair in the South region. The transition from U11 (where she was top-40) to U13 (where she's #76 in doubles) has been the kind of age-group "promotion" that comes with a reality check and a demotion.</p>
