<p><b>Restaurant Review: "Daisy's -- An Eight-Title Destination."</b> Eight titles. A 65% satisfaction rate (155-81) across 26 tournaments. GS U15 #10 (7,646 points). Daisy Xu runs the kind of establishment where the Yelp reviews write themselves. The GS menu features titles at Dave Freeman Jr. OLC (both 2024 and 2025), SGVBC SoCal CRC, SoCal ORC, and NW ORC -- a multi-regional empire built on singles excellence.</p>

<p>The doubles kitchen is equally well-staffed: partner Yian Yang (41 matches) is the executive doubles chef, and Hanyi Lei (21 matches) provides the legacy SoCal flavor. The XD U13 title at Dave Freeman Jr. 2025 and the GD U11 at NorCal ORC prove the menu spans both disciplines and age groups. The 11W-6L at 2023 Nationals and 7W-3L at Midwest ORC were the multi-course evenings that earned national recognition.</p>

<p>The dishes that didn't land: six runner-ups including GS U13 at Junior Nationals (losing to Mizuki Ikeuchi) show Daisy keeps reaching the final course but sometimes gets outcooked by the absolute best chefs. At GS U15 #10 and GD U15 #20, she's firmly established as one of the premier restaurants in the U15 age group. Reservations recommended.</p>
