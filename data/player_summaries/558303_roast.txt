<p>⭐⭐ "Naomi Chen's Badminton Bistro - A Work in Progress"</p>

<p>Visited this establishment three times recently and honestly, the service is still finding its footing. The YONEX DFW location was a complete disaster - not a single dish came out properly, went 0-2 and left hungry. The VISTARA SOUTH location wasn't much better, another 0-2 showing that had me questioning my dining choices. However, I will say the SCHAFER SLG location showed some promise with one decent course out of three attempts, though still closed with a disappointing 1-2 experience overall.</p>

<p>The kitchen staff seems to rotate frequently - worked with Sahana Samarthy, Allen Wang, and Nihal Reddy Gajjala on different occasions, but the chemistry just isn't there yet. It's like they're all cooking their first shift together every time. The doubles menu in particular needs serious work - currently ranked #245 in the GD category and a concerning #443 in XD offerings.</p>

<p>What's most troubling is that this place actually went downhill since opening - started the year at a respectable #125 GD ranking and somehow slipped to #245. That's not the trajectory you want to see from any dining establishment. The 14% success rate speaks for itself.</p>

<p>Look, I get it - this is clearly a new restaurant (only been open since 2025) and the chef is still learning the ropes. There's potential here, but right now you're paying full price for what feels like a training kitchen. Maybe come back in a year or two when they've figured out the basics. For now, there are 189 better GS options in the U15 category alone.</p>
