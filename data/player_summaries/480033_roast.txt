<p><b>2-1 in Finals But 3-4 in Semis: Rianshi Kuchi Wins Championships She Shouldn't Reach</b></p>

<p>Rianshi Kuchi has a 2-1 finals record, which is great — except her 3-4 semis record means she barely gets there. She's won 2 titles but lost 4 out of 7 semifinals, making her the rare player who's better in finals than in the round before it. It's like being terrible at job interviews but great at the actual job.</p>

<p>Her 4-5 three-game record (44%) and 49% overall win rate (33-34) paint a picture of a player who's right at the edge of being good. The blowout losses to Anirudh Rudharraju, Qingyao Liu, and Hanyi Lei show the names that haunt her bracket nightmares.</p>

<p>Brother Siddharth Kuchi is her most frequent partner at 15 matches, making this a family affair of inconsistency. She went 7-2 at the 2025 Capital NE OLC — her breakout event — and 1-3 at the Midwest ORC. The talent is there; the consistency is on vacation.</p>
