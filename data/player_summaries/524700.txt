Devaansh Surpur has surged into the upper tier of U11 boys, ranked BD U11 #8, BS U11 #10, and XD U11 #26 with a 41-39 record (51%) across 14 tournaments. The 2016-born player claimed his first title at the 2025 Fortius South CRC in BD U11 and was BS U11 runner-up at the 2025 Peak Sports South OLC. His 7-3 mark at the 2025 Fortius South CRC and 5-3 at the 2026 YONEX Arena SoCal ORC highlight his best form. Surpur's primary partnership with Avyukth Reddy Pati (8 matches) pairs two top-10 U11 players, while Anirudh Ananth (6 matches) and Sahasra Samarthy (6 matches) handle additional duties. His climb from BD #41 in June 2025 to #8 has been swift.
