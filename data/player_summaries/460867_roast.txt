<p><b>0-1 in Finals and Getting Blown Out by Derek Huang: Brayden Liu Can't Escape the Shadow</b></p>

<p>Brayden Liu has a 22-40 career record (35%) and his one finals appearance was a loss. He went 1-6 at Junior Nationals, which is the kind of performance that makes you reconsider your life choices at age 12. His three-game record of 3-4 (42%) and semis record of 1-3 confirm he's not clutch in any meaningful way.</p>

<p>His blowout losses include getting destroyed by Derek Huang in both mixed doubles and singles -- Derek owns him in two different disciplines. He also got blown out by Kennedy Wu and Nihal Gajjala in U11, and apparently hasn't learned much since then. His BD U15 #80 ranking exists purely because he keeps showing up.</p>

<p>His partners rotate through like a revolving door -- Raymond Wang, Kira Chu, Tinghao Tan, Andy Starostin -- five matches max with any of them. When nobody wants to commit to more than a handful of matches with you, the problem isn't them.</p>
