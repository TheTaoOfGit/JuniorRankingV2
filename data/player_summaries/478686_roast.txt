<p><b>3-4 Finals and 8-6 in Three-Gamers: Vihaa Keesara Can't Finish What She Starts</b></p>

<p>Vihaa Keesara is ranked #5 in all three U11 girls' events, has 3 titles, and went 6-0 at the 2025 Fortius South CRC. Sounds elite — until the 3-4 finals record surfaces. She's lost more championship matches than she's won. For a top-5 player, that's not a record, it's a cry for help.</p>

<p>Her 7-5 semis record and 8-6 three-game record (57%) are decent but nowhere near dominant for someone with her rankings. She went 0-3 at the 2025 LIBC Northeast ORC and 1-4 at the 2025 Midwest ORC — proving that outside her comfort zone, she's a different (much worse) player.</p>

<p>Partner Avyukth Reddy Pati has been along for 23 matches, making him either her greatest supporter or her most frequent witness to underperformance. Getting blown out by Samyutha Aluri twice is the kind of recurring humiliation that builds character — or destroys it.</p>
