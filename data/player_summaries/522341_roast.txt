<p><b>2 Titles, 4 Rankings: Sarah Xie, the Most Confusingly Ranked Player</b></p>

<p>Sarah Xie has 4 rankings across U13 and U15 age groups, 2 titles, and a 19-21 career (47%). She won both titles at the 2025 Egret Midwest OLC, and without that single tournament she'd be 12-17. One great weekend is propping up the whole career.</p>

<p>She went 0-4 at the 2024 Midwest OLC and 1-3 at the 2025 Midwest ORC, because the same region that gave her titles also gives her humbling reality checks. Her 2-2 semis record is coin-flip territory. At least her 2-0 finals record means she finishes when she gets there -- she just rarely gets there.</p>
