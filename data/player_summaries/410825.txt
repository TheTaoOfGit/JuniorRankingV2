She has built strong partnerships with Zhenxiang Deng (20 matches) in mixed doubles and Coco Cenke Tao and Riya Suresh (13 matches each) in doubles. Recent runner-up finishes at the 2026 South ORC and 2025 LIBC Northeast ORC in doubles confirm her status as a consistent contender at the national level.
