<p><b>0-1 in Finals: Danny Luo, Half of SoCal's Most Cursed Doubles Pairing</b></p>

<p>Danny Luo's career record of 22-34 (39%) across 12 tournaments features zero titles and one runner-up finish in BD U11, lost alongside William Lin at the 2025 SGVBC SoCal CRC. Together with Lin, they form a doubles team that specializes in getting close but never converting. His 2-2 semis record is the most interesting thing about him, and that's saying something.</p>

<p>He went 0-2 at both the NorCal ORC and SoCal ORC, proving he's an equal-opportunity loser regardless of which Cal he's in. His three-game record of 5-5 (50%) is perfectly average. His blowout losses include Boqian Zheng, Aiden Wu, Mingyuan Zang, and Matthew Zhang -- basically the entire top tier of U11/U13 boys has used him as a stepping stone.</p>

<p>His partner William Lin (12 matches) is equally titleless, making them the most loyal yet fruitless partnership in SoCal junior badminton. At least they have each other's company on the losers' side of the bracket.</p>
