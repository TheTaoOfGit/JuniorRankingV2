<p><b>65% Win Rate But Those Blowout Losses Are Something</b></p>

<p>Alison Liu burst onto the scene with three tournaments and a 65% win rate including a GD U11 title at Bintang NorCal. Sounds great on paper -- until you see the blowout losses to Kylie Ou, Thea Yuan, Vihaa Reddy Keesara, and Prarthana Sanghani. That's basically the entire top tier of U11 girls saying "not yet, sweetheart."</p>

<p>Zero three-game matches is a fascinating stat: she either wins comfortably or loses comfortably. There is no middle ground in Alison's world, no dramatic comebacks, no heartbreaking collapses -- just decisive outcomes that currently tilt toward the "getting walloped by anyone ranked above her" end of the spectrum.</p>

<p>Her 1-0 finals record and 1-1 semis record show promise, and Tyler Jiang as her XD partner for 7 matches provides stability. But three tournaments is a small sample, and the murderers' row of blowout losses suggests the ceiling might arrive sooner than the rankings imply.</p>
