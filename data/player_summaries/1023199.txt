Ayaaz Baig is one of the most accomplished U17 players on the junior circuit, ranked #13 in singles and #26 in doubles with an impressive 63% career win rate over 37 tournaments. The 2011-born athlete has collected eight titles including the marquee BS U15 crown at the 2025 U.S. Junior National Championships, defeating Aarav Gupta in the final. His 8-1 record at the 2024 Midwest OLC produced both singles and doubles gold, underscoring his ability to dominate at the regional level.

Baig's doubles game benefits from established partnerships with Nathan Lee (18 matches) and Haziqa H Mathani (16 matches in mixed), while his mixed doubles pairing with Mihika Jaiswal (14 matches) has yielded consistent results. His rapid rise from BS U13 #25 in 2023 to BS U17 #13 marks him as a player with national-level ambitions.
