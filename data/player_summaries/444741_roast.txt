<p><b>Semis Record: 2-10. BS U15 #10 But Only Three Titles. Arnav Satish Chokes in the Semis Like It's His Job.</b></p>

<p>Arnav Satish is ranked BS U15 #10 and BS U17 #36 — genuinely elite numbers. So why does he have a 2-10 semis record? He reaches the semifinal twelve times and gets bounced ten of them. That's an 83% failure rate in the round before the final. His three titles and one runner-up came despite, not because of, his semifinals play. He must be skipping the semis entirely some weeks.</p>

<p>His 3-1 finals record when he actually MAKES a final is excellent — the problem is getting there. Combined with a 59% three-game record, Arnav has the weird profile of a player who's clutch in finals but a disaster in semis. Sports psychologists could write a dissertation on this contradiction.</p>

<p>His partner Joey Wang (25 matches) has witnessed most of this semifinal carnage firsthand. The blowout losses to Zisheng Fan, Melvin Cui (twice!), and Vignan Chandrasekaran show the truly elite players don't even let him get to the semis to choke. Arnav: the best player who can't get out of his own way.</p>
