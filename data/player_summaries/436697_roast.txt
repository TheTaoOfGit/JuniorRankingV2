<p><b>11W-5L in finals. 53% three-game rate. Elena Zhang owns the Midwest -- and nothing else.</b></p>

<p>Elena's resume is stacked: 70% career win rate, eleven titles, ranked top 10 in GD. But her 25W-22L three-game record (53%) is shockingly mediocre for someone this talented. She wins close matches barely more than half the time.</p>

<p>Eleven titles across Midwest events -- she owns the Midwest like a franchise. But put her in NorCal or SoCal and the dominance fades: 5W-3L at NorCal ORC, 1W-2L at MassBad NE OLC. Regional queen, national question mark. Her partnership with Charvi Srivastava (36 matches) is the backbone -- and the crutch.</p>
