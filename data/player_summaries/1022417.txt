Varish Karri, born 2013, is an elite U15 talent ranked BS U15 #5 (9,469pts), BD U15 #7, and XD U15 #17, with a remarkable 78% win rate (281-75) across 32 tournaments. A former BD U11 #1, Karri has eight titles including multiple U11 and U13 golds at national events. He went 13-3 at the 2024 NorCal ORC and 12-2 at the 2023 Junior Nationals. His most prolific partnerships are with Keira Winarto (54 matches) and Taneesh Musunuru (39 matches). Karri's sustained dominance from U11 through U15 positions him as one of the brightest prospects in American junior badminton.
