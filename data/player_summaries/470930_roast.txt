<p><b>3 Titles, 5-6 Semis, and 64% Three-Game Rate: Pradhyumn Surendran Is Good but Not That Good</b></p>

<p>Pradhyumn Surendran has 3 titles and a 57% career win rate across 16 tournaments, which puts him in the "solid but not special" tier. His 5-6 semis record means he loses more semis than he wins, and his 3-2 finals record hides the fact that he only makes finals about 30% of the time he reaches semis.</p>

<p>His three titles all came at CRCs -- Midwest CRC twice and MW CRC once. He's never won at an ORC or OLC, let alone Nationals. His three-game record of 9-5 (64%) is genuinely good, but his blowout losses to Amogh Lanka, Shrihayagrivan Karthigayan, and Fonghong Lin show the gap between him and the elite tier.</p>

<p>His partner Adrian Zhang (14 matches) and Rachel Yuezhi Xu (11 matches) have been his most frequent collaborators. He went 0-2 at the 2024 South ORC and 2-3 at the Midwest ORC, confirming the pattern: competitive at CRCs, outclassed at ORCs. He's the CRC champion who can't escape CRC-level competition.</p>
