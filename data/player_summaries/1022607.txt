<p>Zuhao Fan burst onto the junior badminton scene with an unforgettable perfect tournament run, sweeping all six matches at the 2021 HPBC NW ORC without dropping a single game. This dominant performance showcased the tactical intelligence and court awareness that would become his trademark, setting the stage for a promising career in competitive badminton.</p>

<p>The mixed doubles court is where Fan truly shines, capturing his first major title at the 2022 SoCal CRC XD U13 championship. His versatility across disciplines is evident in his impressive finals appearance in boys doubles at the same year's SoCal Arena CRC, proving he can excel whether partnered with male or female players. Fan's collaborative spirit has led to successful partnerships with multiple players, most notably his 11-match partnership with Jacob Zhou and productive doubles combinations with Renee Leung and Felicia Ho.</p>

<p>Despite facing the intense competition at the 2022 Junior National Championships, Fan has maintained a solid 51% career win rate across 10 tournaments, demonstrating remarkable consistency for a young player. His strategic approach to the game and ability to adapt to different playing styles has earned him rankings as high as #53 in BD U13, #66 in BS U13, and #77 in XD U13, establishing him as a well-rounded competitor in the U13 division.</p>

<p>What sets Fan apart is his fearless approach to high-level competition and his natural ability to elevate his game in crucial moments. From his flawless debut season to his championship-winning performances, he continues to develop as a player who thrives under pressure and brings out the best in his doubles partners, making him one to watch as he transitions into higher age groups.</p>
