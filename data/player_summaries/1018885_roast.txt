<p>Ah yes, the 2006 vintage Alan Premkumar - a surprisingly complex junior with notes of inconsistency and a finish that leaves you wondering what might have been. <p>In conclusion, Alan Premkumar circa 2023 was a decent house wine - not terrible, occasionally surprising, but ultimately forgettable. His 56% win rate suggests a player best served with low expectations and perhaps a side of humble pie. One suspects this bottle has already turned, though we'll always have that magical 2022 NJBC vintage to remember what could have been - before time and talent caught up with him.</p>
