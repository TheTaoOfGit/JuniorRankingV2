Evan Liu stands atop U.S. junior badminton as the #1-ranked player in both BD U19 (44,000 pts) and BS U19 (43,000 pts), with XD U19 #4 rounding out a dominant three-discipline profile. His 177-65 career record (73%) across 21 tournaments is the highest win rate among the elite tier. He owns eight titles, highlighted by the BD U19 crown at the 2026 SoCal ORC, where he posted a 12-2 mark, and multiple XD U19 titles at the 2025 Northeast and Midwest ORCs.

Liu's partnership with Justin Xia (51 matches) is the most prolific pairing in the junior ranks, while his mixed doubles work with Sophia Wu (27) and Cassidy Yeh (21) provides additional depth. His rise from BS U19 #16 in late 2023 to the outright #1 spot reflects a player who consistently delivers in big moments, reaching finals even when he does not win them.
