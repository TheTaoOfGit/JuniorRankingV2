Adalyn Puah has emerged as one of the premier U11 girls' players in the country, ranked #10 in doubles (1,764pts), #8 in singles (1,282pts), and #8 in mixed doubles (1,919pts). The 2017-born competitor boasts an impressive 70% career win rate (41-17) across seven tournaments, an extraordinary climb from outside the top 55 in all disciplines just a year ago. Puah has claimed four titles, including the 2026 Yonex Arena SoCal ORC mixed doubles, the 2025 Austin Leander South OLC girls' doubles, and both the mixed doubles and girls' singles at the 2026 VISTARA South OLC. She also reached four finals she did not win, underscoring her consistency at the championship level. Her most productive partnerships are with Harry H Song (13 matches) in mixed doubles and Riana Soprala (7 matches) in girls' doubles.
