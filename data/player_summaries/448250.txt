Nikhil Peechara (b. 2010) is ranked BD U17 No. 51 and BD U19 No. 83 with a 41% career win rate (26-37 in 16 tournaments). His biggest achievement is the BD U17 title at the 2025 South OLC, defeating a field that included Srineet Atluri and Aarav Gupta. Adithya S Burisetty (8 matches) and Vedanth Gundarapu (6 matches) are his regular doubles partners. His recent form has been inconsistent, with strong showings at major events followed by early exits.
