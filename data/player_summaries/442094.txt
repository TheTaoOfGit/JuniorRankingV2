Eli You (b. 2014) is an elite U13 singles player ranked BS U13 No. 9 with a 58% career win rate (104-73 across 20 tournaments). He has been competing at the highest levels since U11, including U.S. Junior Nationals, and posted an impressive 11-3 at the 2024 NorCal ORC where he captured the BD U11 title. His progression to the top 10 in BS U13 cements his status as one of the premier young male players in the country. Claire QiuChen Li (14 matches) and Zelenia Shen (13 matches) are his main mixed doubles partners.
