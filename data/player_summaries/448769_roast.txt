<p>Ah yes, the Theodore Wang vintage—a 2014 pressing from the junior circuit that presents with an intriguingly uneven bouquet. On the nose, we detect promising hints of potential with subtle undertones of "almost there," though the palate reveals a somewhat thin body that struggles to achieve the full-bodied dominance one expects from premium players. The initial attack shows decent structure with a 54% win rate, but the finish lacks the punch of a true champion.</p>

<p>This particular bottle has been aged across seven distinct terroirs, from the harsh, unforgiving soil of the 2023 MassBad NE OLC (a brutal 0-2 debut that would make a novice sommelier weep) to the more hospitable conditions of the 2026 WAYSIDE NE CRC, where it finally showed some legs with a respectable 7-3 performance. The mixed doubles blend with Laasya Polineni produces some fascinating complexity, though the partnership with Matthew Zhang—while frequent—seems to lack the chemistry needed for a truly memorable vintage.</p>

<p>What's particularly fascinating about this specimen is its tendency to reach the final stages—twice achieving runner-up status—only to lose its nerve at the crucial moment, like a promising Bordeaux that oxidizes just as you're about to uncork it for special guests. The rankings tell a tale of inconsistency: climbing from #98 to #70 in boys singles while simultaneously plummeting from #111 to #154 in doubles, suggesting this vintage hasn't quite decided what style of wine it wants to become.</p>

<p>While some critics might dismiss this as an overpriced bottle trading on potential rather than performance, I detect subtle notes of resilience and a willingness to return to competition despite repeated disappointments. Give this vintage another year or two to develop—the tannins of youth are still too harsh, but there are promising undertones that suggest it might eventually produce something worth cellaring. For now, it's perfectly serviceable for casual consumption, though you wouldn't want to serve it at a championship dinner party.</p>
