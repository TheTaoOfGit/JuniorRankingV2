Justin Xia is the #1-ranked XD U19 player in the country (44,000 pts) and holds BD U19 #4 (38,000 pts) and BS U19 #7 (28,000 pts), making him one of the most complete male players in the junior ranks. His 215-74 career record (74%) across 24 tournaments is elite, with eight titles spanning every discipline, including XD U17 at the 2024 Northeast ORC and 2025 SoCal ORC.

Xia's defining partnership with Evan Liu (51 matches) is the most prolific in U.S. junior badminton, forming the backbone of the nation's top doubles pairing. His mixed doubles work with Lydia Chao (31 matches) and Cassidy Yeh (16) has been equally productive. His 16-3 record at the 2023 Junior Nationals remains one of the most dominant single-tournament performances in recent years, and his consistency across all three disciplines at the U19 level is unmatched.
