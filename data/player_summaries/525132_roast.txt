<p><b>1-2 in Finals, 3-5 in Semis: Arushi Saha, the Perpetual Almost</b></p>

<p>Arushi Saha has a 22-33 career (40%) and a 1-2 finals record. She won one title at the 2025 South OLC and has been chasing that high ever since. Her 3-5 semis record means she loses more semis than she wins, and her two runner-up finishes confirm she can get close but usually falls short.</p>

<p>She went 0-3 at both the LIBC Northeast ORC and DFW South ORC -- the big events are kryptonite. Partner Henry Qiu has endured 13 matches, making him either devoted or desperate. Her blowout losses in XD to Fonghong Lin and Virat Satish confirm that mixed doubles is a special disaster zone.</p>
