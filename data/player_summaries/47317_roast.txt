<p><b>48% Win Rate After 10 Tournaments: Justine Yong Is the Definition of Average</b></p>

<p>Justine Yong has played 10 tournaments and managed a 23-24 record — one loss away from .500, which is somehow the most exciting thing about her career. She won a GS U17 title back in 2023, and has been coasting on that single achievement ever since like it's a retirement fund. Three years later, she's GS U19 #53, which is "good enough to show up, not good enough to matter."</p>

<p>Her three-game record of 3-4 (42%) means close matches are not her friend. She went 0-3 at the 2023 SoCal ORC and 0-2 at the 2025 YONEX SOCAL ORC — apparently SoCal tournaments are her kryptonite. Getting blown out by Lydia Chao and Ardra Mohan proves the top tier is a different planet.</p>

<p>Her most frequent partner is Atticus Yong — 12 matches together — which means she had to recruit family. When your best doubles option shares your last name and your dinner table, that's not a partnership, it's a carpool.</p>
