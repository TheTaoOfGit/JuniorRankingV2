Ayushmaan Senapati has surged up the U11 rankings this season, climbing from #67 to #7 in boys' singles (1,583pts) and from #64 to #18 in doubles (1,297pts). The 2017-born competitor has an 11-tournament career record of 28-34 (45%), but his recent form tells a different story -- he reached the finals of the 2026 VISTARA South OLC in singles, falling to Harry H Song, and posted a strong 5-3 at the 2025 Austin Leander South OLC. Senapati has developed steady doubles partnerships with Aaditya Lanka (7 matches) and Avyukth Reddy Pati (6 matches), while also competing in mixed doubles with Eeshani Josyula (5 matches). His rapid ranking ascent across all three disciplines marks him as one of the most improved U11 players in the country.
