<p>Stella Pan has carved out a reputation as one of the most formidable doubles players in junior badminton, capturing an impressive eight titles across her 16-tournament career with a stellar 72% win rate. Her doubles dominance is undeniable—seven of her eight championships came in girls' doubles and mixed doubles, including a remarkable sweep at the 2021 Egret OLC where she claimed both the girls' doubles U17 and mixed doubles U17 titles in the same weekend.</p>

<p>What sets Stella apart is her exceptional ability to form winning partnerships across the court. Her most successful collaboration with Orlando Himawan spanning 15 matches helped deliver multiple mixed doubles championships, while her 14-match partnership with Rishita Tubati proved equally effective in girls' doubles. This adaptability to different playing styles and her natural court chemistry have made her a sought-after partner, evidenced by her successful pairings with Kai Chong, Daphne Huang, and Mikayla Cruz.</p>

<p>Stella's tournament performances showcase both power and consistency, with standout campaigns like her near-perfect 9-1 record at the 2022 South CRC and dominant 8-1 showing at the 2021 USAB South Open Local Championships where she captured the girls' singles U17 title. Her ability to excel across age divisions became evident when she stepped up to U19 competition, immediately making her mark by winning both girls' doubles and mixed doubles at the 2022 Northwest ORC with an impressive 6-1 record.</p>

<p>By late 2023, Stella had established herself firmly in the national rankings with positions of #35 in girls' doubles U19, #44 in mixed doubles U19, and #54 in girls' singles U19. Her 111-44 career record reflects not just talent, but the mental toughness and strategic acumen that have allowed her to maintain excellence across multiple disciplines and age groups, positioning her as a player to watch as she continues her competitive journey.</p>
