<p>Ah yes, the 2007 vintage Arushi Raghavendra—a particularly ambitious young blend that has been aging in tournament cellars since 2021. Upon first inspection, this offering presents a bold bouquet of optimism with undertones of Northeast Championship Regional ambition, though the finish tends toward a rather sharp 38-73 tang that lingers uncomfortably on the palate. The initial nose is quite promising, with hints of three finals appearances, but experts will note the complete absence of that elusive "title" note that distinguishes premium selections.</p>

<p>The pairing with Surya Ilamurugu across 13 matches created an interesting doubles blend, though connoisseurs will detect a certain inconsistency in the partnership's expression—much like a wine that promises complexity but delivers mostly confusion. The 2023 NE OLC produced her finest moment, a delicate balance of mixed doubles finals heartbreak paired with a girls' doubles runner-up finish that showcased her range, even if the cork remained stubbornly unopened on both bottles.</p>

<p>What's particularly fascinating about this vintage is its remarkable transformation from ranked to utterly unranked—a feat of reverse alchemy that would impress even the most jaded sommelier. In 2023, she boasted a respectable GD U19 #42 ranking, but by 2025, the vineyard appears to have gone completely fallow. It's as if the grapes decided to retreat back into the vine, leaving us with nothing but the memory of what could have been a perfectly adequate table wine.</p>

<p>The terroir of various tournaments—from South ORC to Midwest ORC—has clearly influenced this player's development, though not always favorably. Her 0W-3L debut at 2022 South Frisco ORC was particularly noteworthy, like a wine so green it hadn't even begun to ferment. Yet there's something charmingly persistent about this vintage, continuing to show up at tastings year after year, hoping that perhaps this time the magic will finally happen and those 73 losses will transform into something more palatable.</p>
