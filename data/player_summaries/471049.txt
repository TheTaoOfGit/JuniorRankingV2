Sriyansh Chigullapally has been one of the biggest risers in U15 boys' competition, surging to BD #32 and BS #41 after entering the rankings just months ago. The 2013-born player posted a dominant 7-3 record at the 2026 Dave Freeman Jr. SoCal OLC, capturing the BD U15 title. He also reached the XD U15 final at that same event. His 40% career win rate (32-47) across 15 tournaments understates his recent surge.

Chigullapally's primary doubles partner is Logan Lu (12 matches), the pair that delivered the SoCal OLC doubles crown. He also works with Sanjanaa Priya (8 matches) in mixed doubles and Harshith Yoganand (6 matches). With strong form heading into the second half of the season, he projects as a player capable of competing for titles at upcoming national-level events.
