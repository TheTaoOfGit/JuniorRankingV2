Bowen Wang is one of the top U11 players in the country, ranked #9 in both boys' doubles and singles, and #11 in mixed doubles. The 2016-born competitor has compiled a 53% win rate (51-45) across 14 tournaments and has been surging in recent months, going 7-3 at both the 2026 Dave Freeman Jr. SoCal OLC and the 2026 DFW South ORC. He claimed the BS U11 title at the 2026 Bintang NorCal OLC and has reached five other finals.

Wang's primary partnerships with Kylie Ou (14 matches) and William Lin (11 matches) have been productive. His meteoric rise from BD U11 #45 in mid-2025 to #9 by March 2026 is one of the most dramatic climbs in the age group. With his ability to compete nationally across all three disciplines, Wang projects as a future contender at the U13 level.
