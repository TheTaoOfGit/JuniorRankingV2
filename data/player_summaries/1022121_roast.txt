But until she starts collecting some hardware instead of participation ribbons, "Lanting Jiang: The Badminton Chronicles" remains a passion project that's more passion than project. Two stars - one for effort, one for the optimism that maybe the next tournament will finally be her breakout hit. In Hollywood terms, she's still waiting for her "Rocky" moment, currently stuck in "Rocky V" territory.</p>
