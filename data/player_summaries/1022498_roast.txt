Like, pick a lane, Noah! Are you a fine dining establishment or a food truck that sometimes gets lucky?</p>

<p>Don't even get me started on their "Alexander Gu Special" - apparently they've served this combo 29 times and somehow convinced people it's a signature dish. That's like claiming you're a master chef but can't make toast without burning it.</p>

<p>Look, I'll give credit where it's due - when this place hits, it absolutely destroys the competition. But for every YONEX U.S. JUNIOR NATIONALS appearance, there's a painful reminder that consistency is apparently not on the menu. Two stars because when the food is good, it's *really* good - but I'm not making a reservation again until they figure out what kind of restaurant they actually want to be.</p>
