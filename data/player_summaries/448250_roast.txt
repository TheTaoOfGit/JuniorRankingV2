<p><b>One Title in 16 Tournaments. BD U17 #51 with a 41% Win Rate. Nikhil Peechara's Ranking Is a Lie.</b></p>

<p>Nikhil Peechara is ranked BD U17 #51 and BD U19 #83 despite a 41% career win rate (26-37). His one title — BD U17 at the 2025 South OLC, where he beat Srineet Atluri and Aarav Gupta — is genuinely impressive. It's also his only final, and he won it. So technically he has a perfect 1-0 finals record, which is the most misleading stat in junior badminton.</p>

<p>His 75% three-game record (6-2) is excellent, but the sample size is tiny. Most of his losses are straight-set affairs, which is why his overall win rate is in the gutter. The blowout losses to Vincent Chen, Brendan Chen, Advik Kevlani, and Aarav Gupta show the quality of players who routinely two-game him.</p>

<p>Adithya Burisetty (8 matches) and Vedanth Gundarapu (6 matches) have been his doubles partners, and somehow his BD ranking is 30 spots higher than his BS ranking. Nikhil: a doubles specialist who can only win one tournament per career.</p>
