Nihal Kadamba once sat atop the U17 boys' singles rankings but has transitioned unevenly into U19, where he holds BS #12 (22,750 pts) and BD #34 (19,250 pts). His career record of 160-67 (70%) across 26 tournaments remains exceptional, and his eight titles include the 2024 Junior Nationals BS U17, the 2023 Northwest ORC BS U17, and the 2026 Bintang NorCal OLC BD U19. However, six runner-up finishes in doubles suggest he has not yet found the consistency at U19 that defined his U17 career.

Kadamba's partnership with Justin Kan is the most extensive in his career at 73 matches. His singles prowess, built on early dominance including an undefeated BS U17 run at the 2023 Northwest ORC, continues to make him a threat in any bracket.
