<p>⭐⭐☆☆☆ Tried Ananya Ks based on the hype from her 2023 South OLC performance, but honestly? This establishment is giving me major "inconsistent chain restaurant" vibes. The service started promising in 2021 at NE ORC, but then she mysteriously closed for TWO YEARS before reopening. When I finally got a table again in 2023, the menu had barely improved.</p>

<p>The signature dish here seems to be "Almost Mediocre" with a career record of 6-7 that'll leave you neither satisfied nor completely disappointed. She's clearly experimenting with fusion cuisine, constantly switching between partners like she's trying different seasonal menus - Ahan Senthilkumar for the hearty doubles special, then sampling lighter fare with Anya Krishna and Nidhi Sujin. By the time she got to Tejo Ram Mukkamala, it felt like she was just throwing random ingredients together.</p>

<p>The real comedy is in the mixed doubles section - ranked #198 in XD U15, which is like advertising your worst dish on the front page of the menu. Her singles rankings of #126 and #132 were already questionable, but that mixed doubles performance? Chef's kiss of chaos. It's like she specialized in creating confusion rather than points.</p>

<p>What's truly baffling is that this place went from having actual rankings in December 2023 to being completely unranked now. Did health inspectors shut her down? Did she forget how to cook? The mystery deepens. Would I come back? Only if I'm really desperate and everything else is closed. Two stars because at least she shows up, which is more than some places can say.</p>
