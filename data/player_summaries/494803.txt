Harseerat Arora, born in 2015, has climbed to GD U13 #22 with 3,229 points, making her one of the highest-ranked girls' doubles players in U13. She also holds GS U13 #38 and XD U13 #112. Her rise from GD U11 #63 to GD U13 #22 has been impressive. Arora competes with Nandan Mallela (7 matches) in mixed doubles and Isha Suresh (6 matches) and Ananya Kumar (5 matches) in girls' doubles.
