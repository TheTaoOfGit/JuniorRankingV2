Lexuan Wen, born 2012, is a prodigious talent ranked across six categories, headlined by GS U17 #7 (17,804pts), GD U17 #9, and GD U15 #6. Her 78% career win rate (296-82) over 32 tournaments is among the best in the junior circuit. A former GS U13 #1 and GD U13 #2, she has continued her dominance at the U15 and U17 levels, claiming the GD U17 title at both the 2026 SoCal ORC and the 2026 South ORC. With eight titles and six runner-up finishes, Wen is a perennial championship contender. Her most prolific partnerships are with Mihika Jaiswal (42 matches), Cayden Wang (34 matches), and Shraddha Prakash (26 matches). Her 12-3 mark at the 2026 SoCal ORC confirms she remains in top form.
