Justin Kan is one of the most accomplished junior players in the country, ranked BD U19 #61 and BS U19 #37 with a 68% career win rate (120-54 across 24 tournaments). The 2008-born competitor previously held BD U19 #5 and has collected five titles, including BD U19 at the 2026 Bintang NorCal OLC and BS U17 at the 2024 ABC OLC. His legendary partnership with Nihal Kadamba (73 matches) is one of the most storied in junior doubles. Kan has reached six major finals and competed at the U.S. Junior Nationals multiple times. Though his rankings have settled from their peak, he remains a dangerous competitor at the U19 level.
