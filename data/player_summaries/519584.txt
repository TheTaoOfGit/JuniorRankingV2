Xiner Hu is one of the premier U13 girls in American junior badminton, ranked GD U13 #5 with 5,205 points, GS U13 #9, and XD U13 #16. The 2015-born star has compiled a dominant 92-31 career record (74%) across 12 tournaments and has claimed an extraordinary eight titles, including the GD U11 crown at the 2025 U.S. Junior National Championships and GS U11 at the 2025 YONEX Synergy NorCal ORC. Her rise from GS U11 #44 in December 2024 to her current elite standing has been one of the most impressive trajectories in the age group. Hu's mixed doubles partnership with Naksh Shetty (27 matches) is among the most prolific in the U13 division, while Qingyao Liu and Sophia Z Liu (10 matches each) share girls' doubles duties.
