Sherlyn Mei has been on a strong upward trajectory, capturing four titles in recent months while climbing to GS U15 #57 and GD U15 #93. The 2013-born player carries a 62% career win rate (48-29) across 10 tournaments, highlighted by a remarkable 13-1 run at the 2025 ABC OLC where she won GD U13 and XD U13 titles. She added a GD U15 title at the 2025 Can-Am NorCal OLC and a GS U15 crown at the 2026 Bintang NorCal OLC.

Her primary mixed doubles partner is Jonathan Loh (14 matches), while Irene Xu (6 matches) anchors her girls' doubles. Mei's ability to produce dominant performances at OLC-level events, combined with her high win rate, makes her one of the more dangerous players outside the top tier. The transition from U13 to U15 competition appears to be going smoothly.
