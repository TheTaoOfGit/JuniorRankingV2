<p><b>0-4 in Semis, Zero Finals: Michael Chen's Glass Ceiling Is the Quarterfinals</b></p>

<p>Michael Chen has played 14 tournaments with a 36-37 record (49%) and hit 0-4 in semifinals. Four times he's reached the penultimate round, and four times he's gone home empty-handed. Zero finals, zero titles, zero runner-ups. His semis record is a monument to almost-but-not-quite.</p>

<p>His three-game record of 5-6 (45%) and XD U13 #214 ranking tell the story of a player who is competent in singles and doubles but absolutely invisible in mixed. He went 0-3 at NorCal ORC 2023 and 1-3 at the Northeast ORC. His recent run of 3-2 at the SoCal ORC and 5-2 at Dave Freeman is actually his best stretch -- which makes the timing of this roast particularly unfortunate for him.</p>

<p>His partner Aiden Wu (10 matches) and Ryan Li (7 matches) have shared the semifinal heartbreak. Getting blown out by Leon Luo and Leo Ye in U11 was forgivable; still being unable to crack the semifinals at U13 is not.</p>
