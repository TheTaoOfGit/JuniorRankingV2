<p><b>0% Three-Game Win Rate: Pressure? What Pressure?</b></p>

<p>Palaksh Thakare has a 35% career win rate across four tournaments, which already isn't great, but the real headline is his three-game record: 0-1 with a bonus 0-1 in semis. So the one time a match went the distance, Palaksh lost. And the one time he sniffed a semifinal, same result. It's like watching someone trip at the finish line, except the finish line is still two laps away.</p>

<p>At the 2025 U.S. Junior Nationals he went 1-4. At the Midwest ORC, 0-3. The blowout losses to Pradhyumn Surendran, Shubhan Sarikonda, and Keaton Ou suggest Palaksh's game plan is less "strategy" and more "hope." His two doubles partners -- Henry Qiu (4 matches) and Sudeep Janakiraman (2 matches) -- have stuck around, which either speaks to their loyalty or their inability to find alternatives.</p>
