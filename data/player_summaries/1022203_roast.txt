But this isn't a story about rising stars—this is about gravity, and what goes up in the unforgiving world of shuttle diplomacy.</p>

<p>The 2024 MIDWEST OLC should have been Katie's coronation. Two finals in one tournament—Girls' Doubles U17 and Mixed Doubles U19. For most players, reaching two finals would be the highlight of a career. For Katie Moy, it was the beginning of a masterclass in how to snatch defeat from the jaws of victory. She went 6-2 that weekend, her best tournament performance, and somehow walked away with nothing but runner-up ribbons and the kind of trauma that makes champions or breaks dreams.</p>
