<p>Welcome to the Rishita Tubati Career Trail, a moderately challenging 14-tournament hike through the unpredictable terrain of junior badminton. Please note that this trail has been temporarily closed to ranking officials since late 2023, though the exact cause remains under investigation by park services.</p>

<p>We'll begin our journey at the 2021 Egret OLC summit, where our subject achieved her career peak elevation by bagging both the GD and GS U17 titles in a rare double-header victory. From this scenic overlook, you can actually see the rest of her career stretching out below like a series of progressively smaller hills. Notice how the trail becomes increasingly rocky after this point, with several Partner Junction switchbacks where she traded hiking companions more frequently than a indecisive day-tripper swaps trail mix flavors.</p>

<p>As we descend toward the 2022 NE NJBC OLC valley, observe the dramatic terrain shift where Tubati nearly reached another summit in XD U17, only to stumble at the final ascent—a classic case of what rangers call "finals fatigue syndrome." The trail markers here show heavy foot traffic with partner Stella Pan, who logged an impressive 12 matches before apparently deciding to explore other hiking routes.</p>

<p>Our final documented waypoint brings us to the 2023 SOUTH OLC overlook, where despite reaching the GS U19 finals with a respectable #3 ranking, our trail subject experienced what can only be described as a complete navigational failure. Since then, the trail has become increasingly overgrown and unmapped, with no recent sightings reported to the ranking authorities. Park rangers recommend bringing extra provisions if attempting to locate the current trail terminus, as GPS coordinates appear to have been permanently scrambled.</p>
