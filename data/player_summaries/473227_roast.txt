<p><b>0-3 in Semis But 100% in Three-Gamers: Andy Liu Peaks at the Wrong Time</b></p>

<p>Andy Liu has a 44% career win rate and a perfect 3-0 three-game record, which sounds heroic until you see he's 0-3 in semifinals. So he fights like a warrior in the early rounds and then just... evaporates when there's actually something at stake. It's like training for a marathon and quitting at mile 25.</p>

<p>He went 0-2 at his first ORC in 2024, which is the classic "welcome to competitive badminton" initiation. His 5-6 record at Junior Nationals shows he can hang, but just barely — like a kid on a roller coaster who meets the height requirement by wearing thick shoes.</p>

<p>Zero titles, zero finals. He's played with 5 different partners across 6 tournaments, which is a new partner roughly every tournament. Either he's very social or nobody wants a second date on the court.</p>
