<p><b>BS U11 #7 With a 45% Win Rate: The Most Overranked Player in America?</b></p>

<p>Ayushmaan Senapati is ranked #7 in Boys Singles U11 and somehow has a losing record across 11 tournaments. Let that sink in. 28 wins, 34 losses. He loses more than he wins and yet there he sits in the top 10, the living proof that showing up to enough events can outweigh actually winning at them.</p>

<p>His finals record is 0-1 (runner-up to Harry H Song), his semis record is 1-2, and his three-game record is a miserable 3-6. That's a 33% clutch rate -- when the match gets tight, Ayushmaan's game gets loose. The blowout losses are chef's kiss: Devansh Ponnapalli, Chris Lin, Srinivas Shankar, and Neel Achari have all taken him to the woodshed.</p>

<p>Five different doubles partners across 11 tournaments, with Aaditya Lanka logging the most matches at 7. That's not versatility, that's a revolving door. At least he's got range -- he can lose in BD, BS, and XD with equal enthusiasm.</p>
