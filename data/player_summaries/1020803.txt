Despite falling short of the title, that final appearance marked her as one of the most promising young talents in American badminton.</p>

<p>Her breakout tournament came at the 2023 Midwest ORC, where Anand delivered her most dominant performance to date with an impressive 7-4 record. This stellar showing demonstrated her ability to compete at the highest levels of junior competition and adapt to different playing conditions across the country. Her experience spans coast to coast, from the Northeast circuits to California tournaments, giving her invaluable exposure to diverse playing styles and court conditions.</p>

<p>What sets Anand apart is her remarkable versatility in doubles partnerships. She has formed successful combinations with a diverse group of players, including standout partnerships with Anvitha B Ranganna and Lydia Ng, each spanning seven matches. This adaptability speaks to her court intelligence and ability to quickly sync with different playing styles, whether in girls' doubles, mixed doubles, or training partnerships with male players like Nishchith G Manjunatha.</p>

<p>Throughout her junior career spanning 15 tournaments, Anand has maintained a competitive 46% win rate while steadily climbing the rankings. As she transitions into the senior ranks, her extensive tournament experience across major circuits and her proven ability to reach finals position her as a player to watch in American badminton's future.</p>
