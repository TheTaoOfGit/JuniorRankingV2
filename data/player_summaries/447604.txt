Arundhathi Mohan (b. 2014) is one of the premier U13 female players in the country, ranked GD U13 No. 9 and GS U13 No. 7 with a 65% career win rate (102-54 across 17 tournaments). She has five titles including a dominant sweep at the 2026 Bintang NorCal OLC (GS U13, GD U13, and XD U13) plus the GS U11 crown at the 2024 U.S. Junior Nationals. Her 9-4 at the 2026 SoCal ORC and 9-3 at the 2026 DFW South ORC show elite-level consistency. The partnership with Shravan Chockalingam (45 matches) is the most established mixed doubles pairing in the U13 bracket, while Ayushi Raheja (20 matches) anchors her doubles game.
