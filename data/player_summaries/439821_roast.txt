<p><b>Semis Record: 4-7. Sankeerthi Marasani: Always a Bridesmaid, Usually Not Even That.</b></p>

<p>Sankeerthi Marasani has a 42% career win rate across 16 tournaments, which places her firmly in "losing more than winning" territory. Her 1-3 finals record and 4-7 semis record tell a story of a player who occasionally glimpses the promised land before being firmly escorted out. She went 0-2 at the 2024 Midwest ORC and 0-3 at the 2026 South ORC — tournaments where she apparently served as a warm body to fill out the brackets.</p>

<p>Her one title came at the 2024 South CRC in mixed doubles, where she and her partner Vihaan Darbha were presumably the last pair standing after everyone else went home. <p>Yashas Cheerlavancha has carried her through 18 matches, and somehow her results are still this bad. That takes a special kind of anti-talent.</p>
