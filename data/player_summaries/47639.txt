Jasmine Yeung is a former U19 national-caliber player with an extraordinary 84% career win rate (184-33 across 18 tournaments). The 2008-born competitor previously held the GD U19 #1 and XD U19 #1 rankings, collecting eight titles including the GD U19 crown at the 2024 NorCal ORC and XD U17 at the 2023 U.S. Junior Nationals. Her partnerships with Angela Ye (27 matches) and Zicheng Xu (25 matches) have been prolific. Now ranked GD U19 #53, GS U19 #69, and XD U19 #72, Yeung's rankings have dropped as she appears to have reduced her tournament activity. When active, she remains one of the most accomplished junior players in the country.
