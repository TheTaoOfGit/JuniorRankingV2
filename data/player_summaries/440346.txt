Grace Cheng is one of the most versatile players in the U13 girls' division, currently ranked #2 in mixed doubles, #3 in girls' doubles, and #4 in girls' singles — all in U13 as of March 2026. Born in 2014, she carries a 68% career win rate across 23 tournaments and 244 matches, but her recent form has been exceptional. At the two most recent ORCs — DFW South (February 2026) and Arena SoCal (January 2026) — she posted a combined 24-4 record, claiming XD U13 titles at both events while reaching the GS U13 and GD U13 finals. Her trajectory has been a steady, impressive climb: from GS U11 #16 and GD U11 #10 when she first appeared in rankings in late 2023, to GS U11 #1 by December 2024, and now firmly established in the U13 top 4 across all three girls' disciplines after aging up in January 2025.

Grace is strongest in doubles, where she has developed potent partnerships with several players. Zelenia Shen has been her most frequent partner (39 matches together), a pairing that has produced consistent results in GD since their U11 days. More recently, she has formed a dominant mixed doubles combination with Michael Yufu Sun, winning back-to-back XD U13 titles at the SoCal and DFW ORCs in early 2026. In girls' doubles, she has also teamed effectively with Sophia Z Liu, reaching finals at both DFW and the Bintang NorCal OLC — where the pair won GD U15, competing up an age group. With eight tournament titles to her name and six additional finals appearances, Grace has established herself as a player who consistently contends for hardware at every event she enters.

Her primary rival remains Krishnavi Kasu, the top-ranked GS U13 player, who has been a recurring obstacle in both singles and doubles finals. Grace holds wins over Kasu but trails in the head-to-head, and narrowing that gap represents perhaps the clearest path to the #1 ranking. With the 2026 Junior Nationals on the horizon, Grace's current form — particularly her doubles dominance and rising singles consistency — positions her as a serious contender across all three U13 disciplines.
