Alaina Benjamin is one of the premier U15 girls in the country, ranked #20 in singles, #23 in doubles, and #23 in mixed doubles. The 2013-born player carries an impressive 67% career win rate (145-69) across 23 tournaments and has amassed eight titles, including a GS U15 crown and GD U15 title at the 2025 Capital NE OLC. Her 10-4 performance at the 2025 Northeast ORC underscored her ability to compete at the highest level.

Benjamin's long-standing doubles partnership with Medha Gudavalli (27 matches) has produced multiple titles, including GD U13 wins at the 2024 NE MassBad OLC and 2025 Northeast ORC. She also pairs effectively with Arnav Satish (16 matches) in mixed doubles and Mizuki Ikeuchi (14 matches) in girls' doubles. Her steady progression from U11 to a top-25 U15 ranking across all three disciplines marks her as an elite multi-event talent.
