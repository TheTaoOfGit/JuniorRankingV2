<p>Warren Huang is the restaurant critic's favorite kind of subject: a chef who keeps reaching for the next star and almost grasping it every time. Born in 2015, his 12-13 career record (48%) across 5 tournaments lands him at BD U13 #72, BS U13 #101, and XD U13 #175. But it's the THREE runner-up finishes that tell the real story -- BD U13 and BS U13 at the 2026 Play N Thrive MW CRC, plus BS U11 at the 2025 Egret Midwest OLC. Three silver plates displayed prominently where the gold ones should be.</p>

<p>The 5W-4L at Play N Thrive MW CRC is the tasting menu that got rave reviews despite the dessert falling flat (losing to Hrishikesh Gollamudi in the doubles final and Pradhyumn Surendran in singles). The 3W-3L at Egret Midwest and 2W-3L at DFW South ORC add consistent middle-course quality. With Sarah Xie (6 matches) as his primary mixed doubles sous chef and Ellen Ouyang (3 matches) providing backup, the kitchen runs smoothly. At 10, Warren keeps plating beautiful dishes that finish second -- the Michelin star is sitting right there, waiting for one clean service.</p>
