His remarkable 62% win rate across 77 career wins demonstrates the consistency that separates good players from great ones.</p>

<p>Liu's doubles prowess shines brightest in his partnership with Zisheng (GaoGao) Fan, where their 32-match collaboration has become one of the most formidable pairings in junior competition. His ability to adapt and excel with multiple partners—including standout performances with Sahasra Reddy, Zi Yang, and others—showcases the court intelligence and communication skills that make him a coveted doubles partner across the circuit.</p>

<p>What sets Liu apart is his seamless transition between age divisions while maintaining elite-level performance. Currently ranked in the top 260 in boys' singles U15 and top 340 in boys' doubles U15, he's proven he can compete with players years older, holding rankings across U15, U17, and U19 divisions simultaneously. His ranking trajectory tells the story of steady ascension—climbing from BD U13 #25 to BD U15 #18 and BS U13 #49 to BS U15 #25 as he moved up age groups.</p>
