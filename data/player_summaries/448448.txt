Gabby Xiang (b. 2011) is ranked GD U17 No. 64, GS U17 No. 58, and XD U17 No. 101 with a near-even 49% career win rate (54-55 in 16 tournaments). She holds three titles including GS U17 at the 2025 Fortius South CRC, where she went 5-2, and both GD U15 and XD U15 at the 2025 South OLC. Her partnership with Erela Liu (30 matches) is one of the most prolific in the U17 girls' doubles circuit. She has also paired effectively with Aarav Gupta (11 matches) in mixed doubles.
