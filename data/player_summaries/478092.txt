<p>Daksh Thakur has emerged as one of the most promising talents in junior badminton, rocketing from #48 to #4 in Boys Singles U11 rankings in just over a year while maintaining an impressive 71% win rate across 13 tournaments. The 10-year-old's meteoric rise reached its peak with two flawless tournament performances, capturing titles at both the 2025 PEAK SPORTS SOUTH OLC and 2025 EGRET MIDWEST OLC without dropping a single match.</p>

<p>What sets Thakur apart is his versatility and strategic partnership with doubles specialist Srinivas Shankar, with whom he's contested 27 matches and claimed the Boys Doubles U11 title at the PEAK SPORTS SOUTH OLC. Their chemistry on court has elevated Thakur to #14 in the U11 doubles rankings, demonstrating his ability to excel in both individual and team formats. His recent 8-2 performance at the prestigious YONEX DFW BADMINTON SOUTH ORC showcased his continued upward trajectory against top-tier competition.</p>

<p>Thakur's competitive fire burns brightest in championship moments, having reached five finals in his young career and converting three into titles. Even his losses carry weight - falling in closely contested finals at the SCHAFER SLG SOUTH OLC and 2026 VISTARA SOUTH OLC against elite competition. With rankings stretching across multiple age groups up to U19, Thakur consistently challenges older opponents, gaining invaluable experience that accelerates his development beyond his years.</p>
