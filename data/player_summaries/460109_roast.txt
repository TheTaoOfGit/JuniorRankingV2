<p><b>4 Titles and 13-1 at ABC OLC: Sherlyn Mei Only Dominates When Nobody's Watching</b></p>

<p>Sherlyn Mei went 13-1 at the 2025 ABC OLC and won three titles there, which sounds incredible until you realize ABC OLCs are where the NorCal players who can't hack it at ORCs go to feel good about themselves. She followed that up with a 5-3 at Bintang NorCal OLC. Her entire title collection is from local NorCal events -- she's the queen of a very small kingdom.</p>

<p>Her 62% career win rate is boosted by those local stomps, while her ORC appearances tell a different story: 3-3 at NorCal ORC, 4-3 at the others. When the competition actually shows up, Sherlyn becomes mortal. Her three-game record of 8-4 (66%) is solid, but with only 10 tournaments played, the sample size is tiny.</p>

<p>She's ranked GS U15 #57 but GD U15 #93 -- a massive gap that screams "her singles is carrying dead weight in doubles." Her 4-1 finals record looks great, but that one loss was in singles at the same OLC where she won everything else. Even in her comfort zone, she can't go perfect.</p>
