<p><b>5W-0L in finals. 33% three-game rate. Mukil Nambikumar: unbeaten in finals, can't survive the match before.</b></p>

<p>Five titles and zero finals losses is genuinely incredible. But his 5W-10L three-game record exposes the fraud: Mukil wins 33% of close matches. <p>His 54% career win rate tells the same story: he's barely above average outside of finals. All five titles came from South region events. The South is his security blanket. Strip away those titles and he's just another grinder with a losing three-game record.</p>
