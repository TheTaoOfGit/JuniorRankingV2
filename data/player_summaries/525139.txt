Avyukth Pati is the #3-ranked BS U11 player in the country (2,805 points) and ranks #5 in BD U11 and #6 in XD U11, making him one of the most decorated U11 boys nationally. The 2016-born player boasts a 75-45 career record (62%) across 16 tournaments, with three titles including a perfect 7-0 run at the 2025 Fortius South CRC. He has also amassed six runner-up finishes, underscoring his consistency at reaching finals. Pati's 10-3 performance at the 2026 YONEX Arena SoCal ORC was a highlight. His primary mixed doubles partnership with Vihaa Reddy Keesara (23 matches) is the most prolific in U11, while Srinivas Shankar (11 matches) and Devaansh Surpur (8 matches) form his boys' doubles core.
