<p><b>3-4 in Finals: More Trophies for the Other Guy's Shelf</b></p>

<p>Devaansh Mahato has a 62% career win rate and 3 titles across 15 tournaments, so this should be easy to praise, right? Wrong. His finals record is 3-4 -- he loses more finals than he wins. That's not bad luck over 7 appearances, that's a pattern. His three-game record of 10-11 (47%) confirms it: when things get tight, Devaansh folds like origami.</p>

<p>Naksh Shetty has blown him out, Fonghong Lin handed him an embarrassing loss, and his XD U13 blowout against Anirudh Rudharraju is the kind of result you hide from your parents. He went from U11 dominance to U13 where BD #42 and BS #19 don't come with trophies anymore. The transition from big fish to medium fish has been humbling.</p>

<p>Partner Venba Senkadir has stuck around for 24 matches, which is either loyalty or a lack of options. Either way, that 47% three-game rate will keep haunting him.</p>
