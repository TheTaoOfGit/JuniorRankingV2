Harrell Thomas has enjoyed a breakout season in U11 boys' competition, climbing from unranked in doubles to #27 (1,025pts) and from #126 to #25 in singles (617pts). The 2017-born player holds a career mark of 10-9 (52%) across five tournaments and captured his first title at the 2026 Wayside NE CRC in doubles, where he and partner Mahith Jajula defeated Henry Qiu and Jayden Qiu in the final. Thomas went 5-1 at that event, his strongest outing to date. His primary doubles partnership with Jajula (7 matches) has been the engine behind his rapid rise, and he appears well-positioned to push further into the U11 top 20.
