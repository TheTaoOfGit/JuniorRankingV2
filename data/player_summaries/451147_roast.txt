<p><b>Zero Finals in 14 Tournaments: Rheya Kirikera Can't Find the Finish Line</b></p>

<p>Rheya Kirikera has played 14 tournaments with a 29-48 record and zero finals appearances. That 0-0 finals record isn't a typo -- she has literally never been important enough to play in a final. She's 0-2 in semis too, so the few times she sniffed relevance, she promptly turned around and went home.</p>

<p>Credit where it's due: her three-game record of 7-4 (63%) is actually decent, meaning she's capable of grinding out wins when she's in a dogfight. The problem is she keeps getting blown out before it ever gets to a third game. Getting destroyed by players in U11 when she was in U11 is one thing -- but those blowout losses to Crystal Yao and Vaishnavi Packiyaraj at that age still sting.</p>

<p>Her sister Shreya has a 21% win rate, and Rheya's sitting at 37%. Congratulations on being the "good" Kirikera -- the bar was underground.</p>
