<p><b>4-3 With an XD Runner-Up: Kevin Yang's One Good Weekend</b></p>

<p>Kevin Guowei Yang played one tournament, went 4-3, made the XD U19 final, lost to Vaughn Zhao and Jennifer Xu, and apparently decided that was enough competitive badminton for his career. He's ranked XD U19 #80, BD U19 #112, and BS U19 #103 -- all from a single event.</p>

<p>His 50% three-game record (2-2) and zero blowout losses are genuinely respectable. But building an entire ranking profile on one tournament is like writing a resume based on one shift at work. Partner Sydney Li (3 matches) and Daniel Seungchan Park (2 matches) form his tiny but not-embarrassing support crew. The 0-1 finals record stings, but at least he got there once. Whether he'll ever show up again is the real question.</p>
