<p><b>3-1 in Semis But Can't Win a Title: Stephen Huang Gets Close Then Flinches</b></p>

<p>Stephen Huang has a 3-1 semis record and a 2-1 finals record, which actually looks decent. But dig deeper and you find a 47% career win rate (28-31) that betrays the truth: he racks up enough easy wins to reach the business end, but the overall record says "average." He went 1-2 at the NW ORC, 3-4 at nationals, and 2-3 at the NorCal ORC — the big events expose him.</p>

<p>His three-game record of 4-2 (66%) is genuinely good, and his two titles at the 2026 Seattle NW CRC show he can win — just against a Northwest field that's more polite than competitive. The blowout losses to Melvin Cui, Michael Yufu Sun, and Mokshagna Surpur confirm that against national-level talent, Stephen is outclassed.</p>

<p>Six different frequent partners in 10 tournaments means he's still auditioning for chemistry. Ranked across six categories, spread thin, master of none.</p>
