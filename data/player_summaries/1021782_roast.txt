<p>Sophia Sun is the restaurant that earned its first Michelin star before the chef turned 14. The menu includes eight titles spanning GS U11 through GD U15, with signature dishes like the 2022 Junior Nationals GS U11 crown and the 2024 South ORC GS U13 title.</p>
