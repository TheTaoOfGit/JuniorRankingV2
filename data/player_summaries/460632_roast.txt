<p><b>3-5 in Finals Despite 8-1 in Semis: Fiona Liu Peaks One Round Too Early</b></p>

<p>Fiona Liu has a spectacular 8-1 semis record but a deflating 3-5 finals record. She wins her way INTO finals with ruthless efficiency, then immediately forgets how to play badminton once she's there. It's like she saves all her energy for the penultimate round and has nothing left in the tank when the trophy is on the line.</p>

<p>Her 51% career win rate (65-61) across 19 tournaments is the definition of middling. She went 9-3 at the 2024 Midwest OLC, then immediately followed it with 2-3 at the Midwest ORC, confirming the age-old truth that OLC dominance means nothing at the ORC level. Five runner-up finishes means she's experienced the agony of defeat in finals more often than the thrill of victory.</p>

<p>Her three-game record of 10-12 (45%) means she actually loses more close matches than she wins. Ranked GS U13 #20, she's a top-20 player who loses to other top-20 players and then also loses to some players who aren't even close to top-20. Her partner Allen Wang has endured 19 matches of this emotional rollercoaster.</p>
