<p><b>6-3 in Finals But Blown Out by His Own Partner's Former Team: Harry Song, Third Isn't First</b></p>

<p>Harry Song is BD U11 #3, BS U11 #6, and XD U11 #4 with 6 titles and a 69-34 career (66%). His finals record of 6-3 is strong, but those 3 losses include two to the same Chris Lin partnership -- getting blown out in XD by the same pair TWICE is the kind of recurring nightmare therapy can't fix.</p>

<p>His 9-7 three-game record (56%) is decent but means he loses 44% of tight matches, which for a top-5 player is concerning. His 9-6 semis record has 6 semifinal losses -- a lot of early exits for someone this highly ranked. Getting demolished by Reo Qian Bao in BD and by Adrian Zhang in XD proves the armor has gaps. He's elite but not untouchable, and the Chris Lin blowouts will haunt him.</p>
