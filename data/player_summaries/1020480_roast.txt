<p><b>Archaeological Dig: Christopher Gunawan</b> — The excavation of this 27-tournament site has yielded modest but steadily improving artifacts. At 53% (87-76), the stratigraphy shows a civilization in the growth phase: BD U17 #21, BS U17 #34, XD U17 #27.</p>

<p>The Jayden Mar layer (48 matches) dominates this dig — nearly every significant find has Mar's fingerprints on it. Together they produced the BD U17 title at the 2024 SoCal CRC and the BD U15 title at the 2024 Dave Freeman Jr. OLC. The 2024 SoCal CRC (7-2) and the 2026 SoCal ORC (7-3) were the richest excavation seasons.</p>

<p>Four runner-up artifacts, including the BD U17 loss at the 2025 Dave Freeman Jr. SoCal OLC and the BS U17 runner-up at the 2025 ABC OLC, sit in the display case alongside those 2 titles. The 2023 Northwest ORC (0-1) and 2022 Junior Nationals (4-4) are the barren layers. But the 2024 Midwest ORC (6-3) and 2026 SoCal ORC (7-3) suggest this dig site is getting richer with depth.</p>
