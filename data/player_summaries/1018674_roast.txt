<p><b>Movie Franchise Review: Sophia Wu</b> — Welcome to the Sophia Wu Cinematic Universe, a blockbuster trilogy where GD U19 #2, GS U19 #3, and XD U19 #3 are all performing at box-office-record levels. With a 65% career hit rate (140-74) and 7 titles across 25 installments, this franchise prints money.</p>

<p>The ensemble cast is stellar: Evan Liu (27 matches) plays the reliable action co-lead in XD, while Jennifer Xu (26 matches) brings the doubles chemistry that audiences demand. The 2025 season was the Avengers moment — GS U19 titles at both the NE ORC and Northwest ORC, plus GD and XD titles at the Midwest ORC. That 2024 Midwest ORC run (7-1) was the franchise's highest-rated episode.</p>

<p>Even the best franchises have their flops, though. The 2024 NorCal ORC (3-3) and 2023 Northwest ORC (2-3) were the direct-to-streaming duds. Five runner-up finishes, including the 2024 Junior Nationals GS U17 loss to Brianna Y Lee, are the sequels that couldn't quite stick the landing. But when a franchise is ranked top-3 in all three disciplines, the critics are just nitpicking at this point.</p>
