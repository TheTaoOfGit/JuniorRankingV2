<p>Our most significant archaeological find remains a perfectly preserved 0-7 win-loss record, discovered in what scholars believe was once a trophy case but appears to have been repurposed as a storage unit for participation certificates. <p>In conclusion, while the Gattoju civilization may have been brief and winless, it represents a fascinating case study in athletic persistence archaeology.
