<p><b>0-1 in Finals and 1-7 in Semis: Esther He Can See the Promised Land But Will Never Reach It</b></p>

<p>Esther He has made it to the semifinals 8 times and won exactly once. Her 1-7 semis record is a masterclass in getting tantalizingly close and then folding like a cheap chair. Her one finals appearance was a loss, giving her a perfect 0-1 championship record. Zero titles across 10 tournaments — she's the Susan Lucci of junior badminton.</p>

<p>Her 46% overall win rate (36-41) is mediocre, and her three-game record of 4-3 (57%) is her only redeeming stat. She went 1-4 at the 2025 Midwest ORC, 2-3 at the DFW South ORC, and 5-6 at the 2025 South CRC — always finding creative ways to finish just below .500.</p>

<p>Partner Ella Zhang has weathered 20 matches of this, forming the kind of doubles bond that's built on shared disappointment. Getting blown out by Emily Feng twice in different events is especially cruel when Emily herself isn't exactly a juggernaut.</p>
