<p><b>1-4 at 20%: Ishani Vommi Wins Once and Calls It Progress</b></p>

<p>Ishani Vommi has a 20% win rate across two tournaments, which means she's won exactly one match in her entire career. That solitary victory at the Bintang NorCal probably felt like winning Wimbledon given the drought surrounding it. Her first tournament was an 0-2 washout, and the follow-up was only marginally better at 1-2.</p>

<p>Ranked #57 in GD and #72 in GS U11, she's deep enough in the rankings that you'd need to scroll past several pages to find her. Three out of four losses were blowouts, giving her a 75% blowout rate that would be impressive if it weren't so sad. Getting demolished by Kira Chu, Royi Huang, and Aiden Yu is a cross-discipline humiliation tour.</p>
