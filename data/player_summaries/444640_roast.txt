<p>EXCAVATION REPORT: Site designation JR-BADMINTON-2024-LI. Our archaeological team has completed a comprehensive dig of the Grace Li career stratum, dated approximately 2023-2026 CE. Initial surface surveys revealed promising deposits, including two finalist artifacts from the U11 layer, suggesting early civilization potential.
