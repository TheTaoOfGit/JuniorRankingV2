<p><b>Zero Titles. 0-2 Finals. Semis Record: 2-6. Grace Li's Career Is a Case Study in Wasted Potential.</b></p>

<p>Grace Li peaked at GD U11 #8 — a genuinely impressive ranking that suggested a bright future. Fast forward to GD U13 #47, and that future has arrived looking significantly less bright. Zero titles across 15 tournaments, an 0-2 finals record, and a 2-6 semis record paint the picture of a player whose best days were at age 10.</p>

<p>Her 50% three-game record is the dictionary definition of average, and her 51% career win rate means she's basically splitting matches down the middle. Iniyan Arun has been her partner for 22 matches, creating the most underperforming partnership in NorCal badminton. Together they've achieved the remarkable feat of zero titles despite being a consistent pairing.</p>

<p>The blowout losses to her own doubles partner's competitor pool — including Shaoxuan Gong, who herself can't win a final — suggest Grace is losing to players who are also losing to everyone else. It's turtles all the way down.</p>
