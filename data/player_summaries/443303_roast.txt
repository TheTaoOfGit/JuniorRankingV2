<p>The most confusing part is how she managed to rank #72 in girls' doubles and #94 in girls' singles in early 2025, then completely disappeared from the rankings faster than a bad Yelp review gets buried. I'll probably still come back because I'm a glutton for punishment, but maybe lower your expectations and bring snacks.</p>
