<p>Rachel Wang is the nature documentary subject who keeps foraging in the right territory but hasn't quite figured out which berries are edible. Born in 2017, her 7-11 record across 3 tournaments (38%) earns her GD U11 #43, GS U11 #62, and XD U11 #112 -- all debut rankings from a season of Southern circuit events. The 4W-5L at Austin Leander is her biggest expedition: nine matches in one weekend, almost as many as some players see all year.</p>

<p>With Meera Bhalekar (5 matches) in girls' doubles and Richard Wang (4 matches) in mixed -- yes, likely a sibling pairing -- the family foraging party has at least mapped the terrain.
