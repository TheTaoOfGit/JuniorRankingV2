Shraddha Prakash has established herself as one of the premier U17 girls in the country, holding elite rankings across all three disciplines: GS U17 #6, GD U17 #10, and XD U17 #10. Born in 2012, she boasts a commanding 77% career win rate over 29 tournaments -- one of the highest among her peers. Her recent form has been exceptional, going 9-1 at the 2026 SoCal ORC and collecting titles in girls doubles at that event while also winning GD U17 and XD U17 at the 2026 DFW Badminton South ORC.

With eight titles to her name and a progression from GS U13 #31 in late 2023 to GS U17 #6, Prakash's ascent has been steep and sustained. Her primary partnerships with Abhiram Tripuraneni (27 matches in mixed) and Lexuan Wen (26 matches in doubles) form a formidable foundation across disciplines.
