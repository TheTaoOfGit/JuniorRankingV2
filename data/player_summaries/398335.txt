<p>Radha Kuchi has emerged as one of the most promising multi-discipline talents in junior badminton, demonstrating remarkable growth across singles and doubles competition. The 2009-born athlete has achieved impressive rankings of #58 in Girls' Singles U19, #61 in Girls' Doubles U19, and #90 in Mixed Doubles U19, showcasing her versatility and competitive drive across all three disciplines.</p>

<p>Her meteoric rise through the rankings tells a story of dedication and rapid improvement. In just over a year, Kuchi has climbed from #82 in Girls' Singles U17 to her current #58 U19 ranking, while making even more dramatic gains in doubles, jumping from #76 to #61 in Girls' Doubles and from #138 to #90 in Mixed Doubles. This upward trajectory reflects her growing mastery of the sport and her ability to compete effectively against older, more experienced players.</p>

<p>Kuchi's breakthrough moment came at the 2025 CAPITAL NE OLC, where she reached the Mixed Doubles U17 finals, marking her first appearance on a championship stage. Her strong performance at this tournament (6W-2L) demonstrated her ability to perform under pressure and compete at the highest levels of junior competition. She has also tested herself against elite competition at the 2025 YONEX U.S. JUNIOR NATIONAL CHAMPIONSHIPS, gaining valuable experience on the sport's biggest junior stage.</p>
