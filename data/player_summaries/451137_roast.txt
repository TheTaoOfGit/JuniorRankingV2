<p><b>1-2 in Finals: Sienna Lee, the Queen of Almost</b></p>

<p>Sienna Lee has rocketed from unranked to GD U15 #40 in just two years, which sounds impressive until you realize she's lost twice as many finals as she's won. Her one title came in GS U13 at a local OLC -- basically the participation trophy of tournament circuits. Meanwhile, her two runner-up finishes at the 2026 Wayside NE CRC suggest she saves her best choking for when it actually counts.</p>

<p>With a career record of 43-38, Sienna is the definition of "just good enough to get your hopes up." She went 0-4 at the 2025 Midwest ORC before bouncing back to win the next one, because nothing says elite consistency like a coin flip. Her semis record of 3-4 means she loses more often than she wins when the stakes get high.</p>

<p>Her most loyal doubles partner Jida Wu has stuck around for 11 matches, which is either dedication or a lack of better options. At least her three-game record of 8-5 shows she can grind -- she just can't finish.</p>
