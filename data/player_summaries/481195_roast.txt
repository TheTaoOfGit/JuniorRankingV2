<p><b>1-3 in Three-Gamers Despite a Title: Timothy Xu's Success Was a One-Tournament Wonder</b></p>

<p>Timothy Xu won a BD U11 title at the 2025 ABC OLC and went 8-1 there — an incredible result that masks a 1-3 three-game record (25%) and a career that otherwise consists of mediocre performances. He went 2-3 at the NorCal ORC, 3-3 at the SoCal ORC, and 2-2 at the NorCal ORC. Remove that one magical ABC OLC, and his record drops from 56% to basically average.</p>

<p>His BS U13 ranking of #165 and XD U13 #209 reveal that outside doubles, he barely exists in the rankings. The blowout losses to Shravan Chockalingam, Albert Xiao, and Fonghong Lin show the U11/U13 field has his measure.</p>

<p>Partner Devaansh Mahato has been along for 9+ matches. That one title will look great on the refrigerator, but the underlying numbers suggest it was a hot streak, not a sustainable level of play.</p>
