<p><b>2-1 in Finals But 3-2 in Semis: Actually Pretty Good (We'll Find Something)</b></p>

<p>Sahasra Vudum is ranked GD U11 #14, GS U11 #15, XD U11 #31, has two titles, a 52% win rate, and went 8-0 at the 2026 CBA Midwest OLC. Disgusting. But let's dig into the ugly bits: she went 0-2 at the 2025 OBA NW OLC, 2-3 at SoCal ORC, 2-3 at DFW South ORC, and 3-6 at Austin Leander. That Austin Leander record is particularly bad -- three wins against SIX losses in a single weekend.</p>

<p>Seven different partners across eight tournaments, with Aaditya Lanka leading at 7 matches. At some point "versatility" becomes "can't find a steady doubles partner," but Sahasra's titles at least give her bragging rights over most of this batch.</p>
