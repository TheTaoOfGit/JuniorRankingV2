Lilian Feng, born in 2013, has emerged as a top-tier U15 competitor with rankings of #14 in GD U15, #17 in GS U15, and #35 in XD U15. Her 74% career win rate across 28 tournaments and 200 career wins reflect elite-level consistency. She achieved the remarkable distinction of being ranked #1 in both GD U13 and GS U13 by mid-2025 before transitioning to U15 competition. Her 2026 season opened with titles in both singles and doubles at the Bintang NorCal OLC.

Feng's deepest partnership is with Keira Winarto (43 matches), while her mixed doubles work with Ethan Qiu (33 matches) and Leon Luo (32 matches) adds breadth. With eight career titles including the GD U11 crown at the 2023 U.S. Junior Nationals, she brings championship pedigree to every event she enters.
